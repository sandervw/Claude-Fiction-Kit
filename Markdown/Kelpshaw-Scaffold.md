# The Kelpshaw Scaffold

## Overview

**Target Length:** 3,000–4,000 words  
**Structure:** 13 sections (Rite of Spring template)  
**Setting:** The Kelpshaw Soutrenn and surrounding compound  
**Protagonists:** Three brothers — Dremm (lead), Kress, Torvvenn  
**Central Conflict:** Rescue Mother Drelvi from the raider-held soutrenn before the Forgetting takes her  
**Climax Foe:** The Grundkholm  
**Ending:** Victory thrust, cut to black — no epilogue

---

## Character Profiles

### Dremm (Lead — "Bassoon")

- **Role:** Eldest. Decision-maker. Carries guilt for agreeing to this mission.
- **Arc:** From reluctant duty → desperate resolve → sacrificial clarity
- **Voice:** Terse. Gives orders in fragments. Thinks before speaking.
- **Physical Tell:** Grips his knife hilt when anxious.

### Kress (Second — "Clarinet")

- **Role:** Middle brother. The skeptic. Physically strongest.
- **Arc:** From cynicism → fear → trust in Dremm's judgment
- **Voice:** Sardonic. Asks the questions the reader is thinking.
- **Physical Tell:** Cracks his knuckles. Spits when nervous.

### Torvvenn (Third — "Flute")

- **Role:** Youngest. Trained briefly under Mother Drelvi. Holds fragments of shamanic knowledge.
- **Arc:** From uncertainty → recognition of what Drelvi has become → willingness to act
- **Voice:** Quiet. Speaks in half-finished thoughts. Observes more than speaks.
- **Physical Tell:** Touches his throat (where a shaman's talisman would hang).

   - _The Grundkholm's reach_ — massive, shattering the platform Dremm leaps from.
