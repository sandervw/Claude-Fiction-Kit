**Exterior Compound**
A ruined fortification with two long buildings, a pair of towers, and a central wooden guard post. Multiple covered and uncovered corridors connect the structures. Ramps lead up to the towers; stairs descend to the main entrance. Bandits are stationed at the guard post, at a table midway through the corridor, and by a central fire pit. Three additional bandits patrol the exterior and will reinforce once combat begins.
**Upper Dungeon (Ruins)**
Entry descends via a dimly lit corridor with multiple turns. Destroyed automatons (spiders, spheres) litter the path as environmental storytelling. Two bandits are overheard talking near a campfire before the first encounter. The corridor continues descending with a patrolling bandit at one corner. A second bandit camp with multiple enemies occupies a side room with an archery target.
A junction offers two paths: north through a locked door (key carried by a guard) leading to a sleeping quarters with a quest NPC, or south down a ramp to a gate. Beyond the gate is a two-level combat room with raised walkways—bandits and automatons are actively fighting when the player arrives. Two bandits hold each walkway; two spheres and two bandits occupy the lower area.
Past this room, corridors wind through Dwarven architecture with enemies dropping from ceiling scuttles (spawn points). A spear-trap puzzle blocks progression, requiring lever manipulation across six sections.
**Lower Dungeon (Caves)**
A lift descends to a flooded cavern system. Raised walkways span the water, with paths branching west, east, and south. Falmer occupy tents and hives throughout; a chaurus guards a cobweb-covered building. Underwater exploration (requiring water-breathing) reveals hidden alcoves and chests.
A courtroom-like chamber holds two Falmer across split levels. Beyond it, tunnels lead to caves with tripwire-triggered claw traps, more Falmer tents, and chaurus egg sacs. A gated courtyard with a central fountain contains multiple Falmer and two tents. A curving ramp ascends a tower where a button lowers spears blocking the forward path.
A second courtyard features more Falmer, tents, and a hive. An optional locked room contains centurion remains. Stairs lead up to the final zone.
**Boss Chamber (Gatehouse)**
A Dwarven centurion waits at a docking station as the primary boss. A sphere may spawn mid-fight from ceiling scuttles. Raised corner platforms are accessible via pipes. Spears block an alcove with a chest; a lever lowers them. A gated area contains a mechanism requiring a special key item to unlock access to a deeper region (Blackreach). An exit lift returns to the surface west of the main entrance.
