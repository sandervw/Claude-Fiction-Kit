# The Cursed Chapel

## DESCRIPTION
- Ruined chapel nave, pews overturned, stained glass shattered
- Faint hymn echoing from nowhere — unsettling, atonal
- Altar at the far end glowing with sickly green light

## NARRATOR
- This place was holy once; now it feeds on devotion
- The Dreadlord claimed it three centuries ago
- Warns the reader: nothing consecrated survives here long

## TABLE (4)
- Four items scattered across the chapel the player can scavenge
- Mix of useful (divine relic, holy water) and cursed (tainted chalice, bone candle)
- Each has a risk/reward tradeoff

## ACTION
- Stay in starting area when fight begins
- Let Dreadlord come to you
- Moving deeper into the room aggros hidden portrait wraiths
- If you do aggro them, use a divine weapon to permanently kill the wraiths
- No divine weapon? Just dodge until Dreadlord arrives

## DIALOGUE — The Penitent Ghost
- Begs you not to touch the altar
- Says the green light is a lure
- Mentions a hidden passage behind the organ
- Whispers that the Dreadlord fears his own reflection

## DESCRIPTION
- After the fight, chapel falls silent
- Dust settling, green light extinguished
- A door behind the altar grinds open on its own
