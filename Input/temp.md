# Shuffled Combinations

## 1

Location: Mirewatch Lighthouse - Its beacon illuminates only things that no longer exist, revealing phantom ships and dead cities
Threat: The Unfinished - Hunts exclusively those who abandoned creative works; appears as a shifting mass of half-formed shapes, demanding completion or consumption
Ritual: Blade Offering - When concluding any negotiation, both parties draw a drop of their own blood onto the contract as a seal of accountability

## 2

Location: The Drowned Bell Tower - Submerged in a black lake, yet its interior remains dry and sounds echo as if in open air
Threat: Kindness Debt - Appears as a helpful stranger who saves lives unsolicited, then returns years later to collect—the saved must now save it, no matter the cost
Ritual: The Silence Hour - One hour after sundown, all speech ceases; business, arguments, and confessions must wait until the hour passes

## 3

Location: Char Hollow Well - Objects dropped in fall upward into a starless sky that exists only within the shaft
Threat: Hollow Choir - A mass of fused bodies that only hunts those who have been forgotten by everyone they once knew—it can sense the absence of being remembered
Ritual: Name Carrying - Citizens introduce themselves with the name of their most recent dead relative appended to their own, keeping the deceased in public memory

## 4

Location: Thornspire Keep - All rooms share the same doorway—which door you enter depends on the emotion you carry
Threat: The Seamstress - Stitches sleeping victims into their own nightmares using thread spun from regret; victims wake paralyzed, living their worst memories on loop
Ritual: Hollow Pockets - Before sleep, all pockets and bags must be turned inside-out and shown empty, symbolizing one carries no hidden burdens into dreams

## 5

Location: The Ossuary of Whispered Names - The deeper you descend, the higher you climb—visitors enter through a cellar but emerge atop a mile-high spire
Threat: Gravedebt - Manifests near anyone who survived when others died in their place; doesn't attack, just follows—until the survivor is alone
Ritual: The Grief Tithe - Monthly, citizens cut a lock of hair and surrender it to communal pyres, externalizing private sorrow into shared smoke

## 6

Location: Gallowsreach Bridge - The bridge appears 100 feet long but takes three days to cross on foot, regardless of pace
Threat: Stillborn Echo - Mimics the voice of a loved one calling from just out of sight, leading victims in circles until exhaustion—it feeds on the hope that precedes despair
Ritual: Ash Thresholds - Doorways are lined with ash each morning, swept away each night—a visual record that the home was undisturbed

## 7

Location: The Weeping Colossus - A statue visible from any point in the surrounding wasteland, yet impossible to approach closer than a league
Threat: The Tithekeeper - Appears at thresholds offering fair bargains, but each deal subtly redefines what the victim considers 'fair' until they willingly trade away everything
Ritual: The Morning Accounting - Each household recites aloud the names of debts owed and grievances held before breaking fast, ensuring nothing festers unspoken

## 8

Location: The Silent Chancel - Physically rotates to always face away from whoever is looking at it
Threat: Vowbreaker - Tracks oath-makers obsessively but never harms them—instead kills everyone the oath-breaker loves while forcing them to watch
Ritual: Scar Baring - When entering another's home, visitors expose their most prominent scar as proof they have suffered and thus can be trusted

## 9

Location: The Flayed Oak - Casts seven shadows from a single light source, each pointing toward a different grave
Threat: The Patient Feast - Infests a community as a shared delusion of prosperity; victims fatten and celebrate while slowly being digested from within over months
Ritual: The Dusk Census - Households call out their number of living members at nightfall; neighbors listen and note discrepancies

## 10

Location: Graven Tooth Monolith - Appears two-dimensional from every angle except when viewed through a corpse's eyes
Threat: The Asking Price - A faceless merchant that appears in moments of desperation, trades exactly what you need for 'something you won't miss yet'
Ritual: The Last Bite - The final morsel of every meal is left untouched on the plate, acknowledging that nothing is truly finished
