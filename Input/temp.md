*Prey that runneth sidelong harboreth either cunning or terror - rarely nothing betwixt.*

*A name? A command?*

*Villainy hath no set habitation.*

*'Tis a migrating word, now nesting in one heart, now another, and at each lighting with different plumage.*

*No warmer here, methinks, than on the scoured moor.*

*What claim have I, that I should trespass amongst such names?*

*But does the river ask permission of the gorge it cleaves? Does rain apologize to the cistern?*

*Rains might come, belike.* He thought idly. *Let them drown me in this culvert.*

*The doom whereof the goldfinch sang. Here? In this muddy ruin of a yard?*
