# Conan — Quotes

_Beyond the Black River by Robert E. Howard_

1. "There's nothing in the universe cold steel won't cut."

2. "I'm not going out of my way looking for devils; but I wouldn't step out of my path to let one go by."

3. "Civilized men laugh. When they can't explain something by their half-baked science, they refuse to believe it."

4. "When they smoke my head the whole river will know it."

5. "He was a man. I drink to his shade, and to the shade of the dog, who knew no fear."

6. "The heads of ten Picts shall pay for his, and seven heads for the dog, who was a better warrior than many a man."

7. "If I can get within ax-throwing distance of him I'll settle that question."

8. "Let him come. He can smell the devils before we can see them."

9. "They're not afraid of anything, very long."

10. "No use to try to follow a demon."
