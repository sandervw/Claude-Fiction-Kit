# Ashitaka — Actions

_Princess Mononoke by Hayao Miyazaki_

1. Rides out to confront the approaching demon boar threatening his village
2. Shoots arrows at the Tatari Gami, piercing its eye and forehead
3. Kills the corrupted boar god Nago to protect his people
4. Presents the iron ball extracted from the boar to the village elder
5. Cuts his hair as ritual severance from his community
6. Departs his village eastward with only his elk Yakul as companion
7. Pays a rice seller with gold for provisions during his journey
8. Shoots an arrow to stop a soldier from killing a fleeing woman
9. Discovers his curse grants him supernatural strength in combat
10. Shows the mysterious lead bullet to the monk Jiko-bou at a market town
11. Learns from Jiko-bou about the western forest and its deity
12. Rescues two wounded iron workers from a ravine by the river
13. Tends to the injured porters' wounds before moving them
14. Carries one wounded man on his back through the forest
15. Follows the Kodama tree spirits as guides through the woods
16. Delivers both wounded men safely to Tatara-ba (Iron Town)
17. Reveals his cursed arm to Lady Eboshi in Iron Town
18. Confronts Eboshi after learning she shot the boar that cursed him
19. Intervenes in San's assassination attempt on Lady Eboshi
20. Subdues both San and Eboshi to stop their duel
21. Carries the unconscious San out of Iron Town
22. Gives his crystal dagger to San as a gesture of trust
23. Refuses to retaliate when shot in the back by a townsperson
24. Allows San to take him into the forest despite his wound
25. Asks San to spare the Kodama spirits during their journey
26. Stands before the Forest Spirit at the sacred pool
27. Warns Eboshi against killing the Forest Spirit
28. Attempts to mediate peace between Iron Town and the forest gods
29. Convinces San that not all humans deserve her hatred
30. Pursues Jiko-bou to recover the stolen Forest Spirit head
31. Evacuates Iron Town residents from the Nightwalker's deadly ooze
32. Retrieves the Forest Spirit's head alongside San
33. Returns the head to the dying Nightwalker at sunrise
34. Pledges to help rebuild Iron Town with its survivors
35. Promises San he will visit her in the restored forest
