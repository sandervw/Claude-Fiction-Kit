# Scene Parameters Card: Little Comfort's Last Rest

## Locked Axes

- **Senses:** sound, proprioception, temperature
  - The scene is built on echoes and mumbles, the dryness and wringing of the man's own hands, and the radiant pull of the long fire. Sight stays peripheral.

- **Rhythms:** long-coordinated, balanced
  - Long appositive-chained sentences carry the setting; balanced clauses keep the old man's furtive movement legible inside the sprawl.

- **POV distance:** close
  - We need to feel the sparky friction of his fingers and hear the echoes the way he hears them, not from a chronicler's height.

- **Time handling:** real-time
  - The scene tracks one passage through the vault, footstep by footstep.

- **Scene shape:** reversal
  - The opening note (familiar, convivial transit) flips: the inconspicuous skulk and mumbled fragments turn the convivial house into something he is fleeing through.
