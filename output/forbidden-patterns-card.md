# Forbidden Patterns Card: Little Comfort's Last Rest (Predictive)

## Simile bans

- No similes whose vehicle is a household item.
- No `X like Y` similes where Y is domestic or food.

## Cadence bans

- No `not X, but Y` constructions.
- No parallel-clause balanced sentences (e.g., `he did X. she did Y.`).

## Syntactic bans

- No sentences beginning with `And` or `But`.

## Imagery bans

- No description of breath as visible vapor.
- No glowbug, lantern, or torchlight throwing shadows imagery.

## Register bans

- No philosophical asides from the narrator.

## Notes

These are in addition to the standing house rule: no em-dashes anywhere. Use commas, colons, parentheses, or sentence breaks instead.
