# Forbidden Patterns (Predictive): little-comfort

**Scene:** Hunched old man skulking through Little Comfort's frequented crypt at scene opening.

## Simile Bans

- no metaphors built on light or darkness
- no `X like Y` similes where Y is domestic or food

## Cadence Bans

- no `not X, but Y` constructions
- no sentences that end on a single-word coda

## Syntactic Bans

- no comma splices

## Imagery Bans

- no silence described as a physical weight
- no rain-on-stone or rain-on-armor imagery

## Register Bans

- no modern slang if the scene is pre-modern

*The writing agent treats every ban above as a hard contract.*
