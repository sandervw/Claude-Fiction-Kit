## Test 1

### Input

His gait was delicate, as of some lithe beast of prey newly wakened out of slumber, and he greeted with lazy grace the many friends who hailed his entrance. Very tall was that lord, and slender of build, like a girl. His tunic was of silk coloured like the wild rose, and embroidered in gold with representations of flowers and thunderbolts. His buskins were laced with gold, and from his belt hung a sword, narrow of blade and keen, the hilt rough with beryls and black diamonds. Strangely light and delicate was his frame and seeming, yet with a sense of slumbering power beneath, as the delicate peak of a snow mountain seen afar in the low red rays of morning. His face was beautiful to look upon, and softly coloured like a girl's face, and his expression one of gentle melancholy, mixed with some disdain; but fiery glints awoke at intervals in his eyes, and the lines of swift determination hovered round the mouth below his curled moustachios.

### Prompt

A tall, slender lord with predatory grace and feminine beauty, dressed in ornate silk and gold, whose gentle melancholy and delicate frame belie a dangerous, fiery power lurking beneath the surface.

### Output

Tall he stands, and slender as a candle flame, and in his movements there is the unhurried grace of something that has never known the need to flee. A mantle of smoke-grey silk drapes from shoulders narrow as a boy's, falling open to reveal a doublet of crushed velvet the color of dried blood, its surface worked with thread-of-gold in patterns that spiral like the whorls of distant fires; rings of pale gold and carnelian adorn fingers too long and too delicate, fingers that move when he speaks as though conducting some silent and mournful music. His face possesses that dangerous beauty which belongs to poisonous flowers and to saints in their final agonies, the cheekbones high and sharp beneath skin white as temple marble, the mouth soft and sad and faintly curved, the eyes heavy-lidded and burning amber beneath brows fine as brushstrokes. When lamplight touches him it seems to linger overlong, pooling in the hollows of his throat and gilding the loose fall of his coal-black hair, and when he inclines his head in greeting there is something in the gesture of the wolf that lowers itself before it springs, something patient and famished and inevitable beneath that gentle melancholy. His hands, resting now upon the pommel of a chair, are steady as a surgeon's hands.

## Test 2

### Input

A lady light of build and exceeding fair to look upon, brave of carriage like a war-horse, soft of feature, clear-browed, gray-eyed and proud-eyed: sweet-mouthed, but not as one who can speak nought but sweetness. Her robe was of pale buff-coloured silk, with corsage covered as by a spider's web with fine golden threads; and she wore a point-lace ruffle stiffened with gold and silver wire and spangled with little diamonds. Her deep hair, black as the raven's wing, was fastened with pins of gold, and a yellow rose that nestled in its coils was as the moon looking forth among thick clouds of night.

### Prompt

A tall, fair noblewoman with raven-black hair and proud gray eyes, wearing elegant buff silk with golden thread and diamonds, who carries herself with martial grace despite soft features and a sweet but not merely sweet mouth.

### Output

Tall she stood, and pale as winter moonlight, a noblewoman whose bearing spoke of courts where blades were drawn as readily as words, and in her proud gray eyes there dwelt something of the hawk—watchful, patient, and not quite tame. Her hair fell black as raven-wing, black as old iron, gathered in braids wound through with thin chains of gold, and her face, though soft in its shaping, carried in the line of the jaw and the set of the sweet, curving mouth a certain firmness that whispered of oaths kept and vengeances remembered. She wore a gown of buff-colored silk, costly and fine, the bodice stitched with golden thread in patterns like climbing thorns, and at her throat and wrists diamonds glittered cold in their delicate filigreed settings; yet she moved through the candlelit hall with a warrior's grace, her white hands resting easy at her sides, the fingers long and still.

## Test 3

### Input

His cloak of sky-blue silk was flung back from his shoulders. His chest, broad as a bull's, swelled beneath the shining silver scales of his byrnie, that was short-sleeved, leaving his strong arms bare to view with golden rings about the wrists. Proudly he stood before the King, his head firm planted above his mighty throat and neck; his proud luxurious mouth, made for wine-cups and for ladies' lips, firm set above the square shaven chin and jaw; the thick fair curls of his hair bound with black bryony; the insolence that dwelt in his dark blue eyes tamed for the while in face of that green bale-light that rose and fell in the steadfast gaze of the King.

### Prompt

A proud, powerfully-built warrior with a broad chest, bare muscled arms adorned with golden rings, thick fair curls bound with black bryony, and dark blue eyes that hold a tamed insolence—dressed in a sky-blue silk cloak and short-sleeved silver scale byrnie.

### Output

Broad he stands, and proud, this warrior whose chest swells like the curved hull of a war-galley, whose bare arms speak of the forge-hammer's labor in every corded sinew and sun-darkened vein; golden rings clasp those arms from wrist to shoulder, bands of beaten metal that catch the torchlight and hold it jealously, and thick fair curls spill across his shoulders in a lion's abundance, bound at the temples with twists of black bryony whose dark berries gleam like droplets of old blood against the pale gold. His byrnie hangs to mid-thigh, each silver scale sewn overlapping upon boiled leather so that he ripples when he moves like a river under moonlight, and above this martial skin his cloak falls in folds of sky-blue silk, pinned at one shoulder, trailing behind him as he walks. His face holds the weathered arrogance of men who have killed and will kill again, the jaw heavy and blunt beneath a thin-lipped mouth, the brow a shelf of bone above eyes of dark and tidal blue that regard the world with an insolence only half-mastered, a wolf brought reluctantly to heel yet watchful still, patient still, hungry still beneath the golden rings and lordly raiment.
