Claimant filed an Application for Alternate Medical Care on December 19, 2025, 
seeking a second orthopedic opinion with Steindler Orthopedic for her accepted right 
hip injury. The matter was scheduled for an alternate medical care hearing on January 
2, 2026, at 10:30 a.m. However, prior to hearing, counsel for the parties communicated 
and Defendants agreed to authorize the second orthopedic opinion appointment with/at 
Steindler Orthopedic, and also CONSENTED to get an appointment scheduled within 
21 days of this Order. The appointment only needs to be scheduled within the 21 days 
of this Order; the actual in-person appointment does not need to occur within the 21 
days of this Order.  
As the Parties CONSENT to the foregoing, IT IS ORDERED that, within the next 
twenty-one (21) days, Defendants get Claimant scheduled for an authorized 
appointment with/at Steindler Orthopedic for her accepted right hip injury. 
Signed and filed this _31ST _ day of December, 2025.
