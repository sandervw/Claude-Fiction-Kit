Claimant filed an application for alternate medical care on September 21, 2022 
requesting that defendants be ordered to schedule an EMG and an orthopedic 
consultation. Hearing was scheduled for October 3, 2022, at 10:30 a.m. 
Prior to hearing on October 3, 2022, the parties advised the undersigned that 
they have reached an agreement concerning Claimant’s Petition for Alternate Medical 
Care. The parties request a Consent Order be entered in this case, in lieu of a hearing.  
The parties agree that defendants will provide claimant with an EMG and 
orthopedic care with Ian Crabb, MD. Defendants have scheduled claimant’s EMG for 
November 4, 2022 at 9:00 a.m. with David Franco, MD, at 16120 West Dodge Road, 3rd 
floor, Omaha, Nebraska, 68114. The appointment with Dr. Crabb will be as soon as Dr. 
Crabb has an opening, and in any case, no later than the end of October, 2022 but if Dr. 
Crabb cannot see claimant sooner than the end of October, then defendants will choose 
a different orthopedic provider who can see claimant sooner than the end of October. 
Defendants will notify claimant’s counsel of the date and time of the orthopedic 
appointment. A hearing is therefore determined to be unnecessary, based on the 
agreement of the parties. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
Defendants shall immediately authorize and pay for the EMG appointment 
outlined above, and shall arrange for the appointment with Dr. Crabb or other suitable 
orthopedic provider as outlined above.
