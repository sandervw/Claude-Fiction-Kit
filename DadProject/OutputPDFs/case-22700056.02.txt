On January 28, 2022, Paul Roth applied with the agency for alternate care 
relating to injuries to his back and right shoulder. The agency scheduled the matter for a 
telephone hearing on February 9, 2022. The defendants answered on February 7, 2022, 
but did not admit or deny liability due to an ongoing investigation.  
Before the start of the hearing, the undersigned had a discussion with counsel for 
the parties. They shared that they had reached an agreement on care for Roth’s 
injuries. They asked for a consent order memorializing their agreement that the 
defendants would authorize care with Dr. Jacobson at Capital Orthopedics. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) The defendants shall authorize care with Dr. Jacobson at Capital Orthopedics 
in accordance with the parties’ agreement. 
2) The alternate care hearing is CANCELLED. 
Signed and filed this _9th _ day of February, 2022.
