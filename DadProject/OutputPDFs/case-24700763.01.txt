This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, Barbara Bell.  On October 1, 2024, the 
parties reached out to the undersigned via e-mail indicating that the defendants agreed 
to authorize the care requested by the claimant.  The parties indicated that they wished 
to enter into a consent order rather than proceed with a hearing on the issue. 
 
The defendants consent, stipulate, and agree to authorize the right shoulder 
procedure as recommended by Dr. Pape.  At the time of the issuance of this order, the 
defendants were proceeding with completing an employer authorization form provided 
by a doctor.   
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order via e-mail on 
October 1, 2024; thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The parties have agreed that defendants will authorize the right shoulder 
procedure as recommended by Dr. Pape, and also that the defendants will proceed with 
promptly completing an employer authorization form regarding the same.
