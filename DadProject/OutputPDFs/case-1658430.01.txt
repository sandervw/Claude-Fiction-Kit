On June 10, 2021, Mary Lou Cardenas applied for alternate care for an alleged 
work injury to her back sustained on October 6, 2018. Simpson Memorial Home and 
Amerisure Partners Insurance Company did not file an answer. Before the scheduled 
hearing, counsel for the parties informed the presiding deputy they had reached an 
agreement on care and requested a consent order memorializing the agreement. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
1) The defendants shall authorize and arrange follow-up care with Benjamin D. 
MacLennan, M.D., for Cardenas’s work injury of October 6, 2018. 
 
2) The defendants shall hold Cardenas harmless for the reasonable expenses 
relating to the authorized follow-up care with Dr. MacLennan.
