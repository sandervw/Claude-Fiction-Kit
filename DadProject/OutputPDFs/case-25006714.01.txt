Claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care on August 14, 2025.  This 
matter was set for a telephone hearing on August 25, 2025 at 8:30 A.M. 
Prior to the August 25, 2025 hearing, the parties reached a resolution by 
consent.  Defendant has authorized Claimant to see an orthopedic specialist for further 
treatment options and will coordinate scheduling.  If the orthopedic specialist 
recommends treatment that is causally related to the work injury, Defendant agrees to 
authorize and arrange such treatment.  Defendant will schedule Claimant’s initial 
appointment at the earliest available date. 
By reaching this agreement and consenting to this order, neither party waives 
any rights they may have to address medical treatment in the future. 
The parties agreed to the undersigned issuing this consent order confirming the 
agreement reached. 
IT IS SO ORDERED.
