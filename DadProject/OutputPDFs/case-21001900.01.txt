Claimant filed an application for alternate medical care on May 10, 2021 
requesting that defendants be ordered to authorize a treatment provider for his lumbar 
spine condition.  
Prior to hearing, the parties contacted the undersigned deputy and indicated that 
defendants have agreed to authorize a medical provider and represent that they have 
made inquiries with UnityPoint Health Work Well Clinic about providing treatment to 
claimant and forwarded records on May 16, 2022.  
Claimant desired that a Consent Order be entered in this case, in lieu of a 
hearing. Defendants agreed. 
A hearing is therefore determined to be unnecessary, based on the agreement of 
the parties.  
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED THAT: 
Defendants shall provide written authorization to UnityPoint Health Work Well 
Clinic to treat claimant and an appointment shall be scheduled within 30 days from the 
date of this order. The appointment need not occur within 30 days of this order, but an 
appointment date shall be scheduled within that period of time.
