ORDER RE: 
PETITION FOR ALTERNATE 
MEDICAL CARE 
 
With respect to the specific alternate medical care requested, defendants 
have agreed to authorize and arrange medical treatment for claimant at Parkview 
Orthopedics.  Defendants are ordered to provide all pertinent records to Parkview 
Orthopedics as soon as possible, but in no event any later than October 7, 2022.   
 
The parties desired that a consent order be entered in this case, in lieu of 
a hearing.  After discussion between the parties and an agreement to provide the 
requested care, there is no justiciable issue for hearing and determination.  Both 
parties agree that a consent order is appropriate and a formal hearing is 
unnecessary to resolve the contested case dispute.   
 
By reaching this agreement and consenting to this order, neither party 
waves any rights they may have in the future to address medical treatment.  
Defendants maintain the right to direct medical care in this case. 
 
IT IS SO ORDERED:
