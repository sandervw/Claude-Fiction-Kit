On February 12, 2025, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care, 
seeking treatment for alleged injuries to her bilateral shoulders and neck. Defendants 
filed an answer on February 12, 2025, denying liability for the right shoulder and neck, 
but admitting liability for the left shoulder. Hearing was scheduled to take place on 
February 21, 2025, with respect to the admitted left shoulder only. 
Prior to the scheduled hearing, the parties advised that they had reached an 
agreement regarding the requested care for the admitted left shoulder. Given the 
agreement of the parties, the hearing was determined to be unnecessary. The parties 
requested formal entry of the consent order to confirm and document the agreement. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED:   
Per the agreement of the parties, defendants agree to authorize Mark Gorsche, 
M.D., to treat claimant’s left shoulder. 
The hearing scheduled for February 21, 2025 is cancelled. 
Failure to comply with this order may result in sanctions under Iowa 
Administrative Code rule 876—4.36.
