On October 25, 2024, Susan Erlandson applied to the agency for alternate care 
under Iowa Code section 85.27 and Iowa Administrative Code rule 876—4.48. The 
agency issued an order setting the case for a telephone hearing to be held November 5, 
2024. The defendants, employer Don Hummer Trucking Corp. and insurance carrier 
West Bend Mutual Insurance Co., answered on October 30, 2024.  
On November 4, 2024, the parties emailed the undersigned, stating they have 
reached an agreement on care. The defendants have authorized care with Ashar Afzal, 
M.D., under Iowa Code section 85.27. The parties will work together to promptly 
schedule care at a time and place that is convenient for Erlandson. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) The defendants shall authorize care with Dr. Afzal, at a time and place agreed 
to by the parties. 
 
2) The alternate care telephone hearing scheduled for November 5, 2024, is 
CANCELLED.
