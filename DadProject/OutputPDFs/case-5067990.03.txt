This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 IAC 4.48 is 
invoked by claimant, Dale Hayes.  The alternate medical care claim was scheduled for 
telephone hearing on October 25, 2021, at 8:30 a.m.   
 
Prior to the commencement of the hearing, on October 25, 2021, the parties and 
the undersigned discussed the entry of a consent order in this matter.  Counsel for the 
defendants indicated that the defendants agreed to authorize care as noted in the 
petition of: physical therapy, aqua therapy, and referral to a pain psychologist per the 
recommendations of Dr. Wikle.  The defendants also agreed to continued authorization 
of treatment with Dr. Miller for pain management.  The defendants agreed that this care 
is authorized. 
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal hearing.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order verbally on 
October 25, 2021; thus no alternate medical care hearing was necessary.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The parties have agreed that Defendants will authorize care and treatment for 
physical therapy, aqua therapy, and a referral to a pain psychologist.    
 
The parties have also agreed that Defendants will authorize care and treatment 
for Claimant’s work-related conditions with Dr. Miller.
