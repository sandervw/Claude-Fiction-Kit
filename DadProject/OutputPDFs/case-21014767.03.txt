On November 15, 2022, Helen Hall filed an application for alternate care under 
Iowa Code section 85.27 and agency rule 876 IAC 4.48. The defendants, employer 
American Baptist Homes of the Midwest and insurance carrier United Wisconsin 
Insurance Company, filed an answer, accepting liability. The agency scheduled the 
case for a hearing to be held November 30, 2022.  
Before the scheduled hearing, counsel for the parties informed the undersigned 
they had reached an agreement on care under which the defendants have agreed to 
authorize Hall to undergo the recommended cervical surgery with Dr. Schmitz.  The 
parties requested a consent order from the agency memorializing the agreement in lieu 
of a hearing and determination of the application.   
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED:  
1) Defendants shall authorize the recommended cervical surgery with Dr. 
Schmitz; and 
 
2) The alternate care hearing scheduled in this matter is CANCELLED. 
Signed and filed this 30th day of November, 2022.
