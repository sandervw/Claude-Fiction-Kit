On January 7, 2026, claimant filed a Petition for Alternate Medical Care. Hearing 
was scheduled to take place on January 16, 2026. 
 
 
During the hearing on January 16, 2026, counsel for the parties advised the 
undersigned that they had reached an agreement regarding the requested care. That 
agreement is as follows: 
 
The parties agree that the defendants will approve an appointment for pain 
management treatment with Dr. Ronald Kloc and an appointment with Dr. 
Sandeep Bhangoo or Dr. Solomon Ondoma (whichever is available first) for 
a neurosurgery consult. The appointments will be approved and scheduled 
by Monday, January 26, 2026. 
 
To protect the interests of their clients, the parties agree to entry of a consent 
order, requiring defendants to authorize treatment as described above. The parties 
requested entry of a consent order to confirm and document the agreement.  
 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
 
The parties agree that the defendants will approve an appointment for pain 
management treatment with Dr. Ronald Kloc and an appointment with Dr. Sandeep 
Bhangoo or Dr. Solomon Ondoma (whichever is available first) for a neurosurgery 
consult. The appointments will be approved and scheduled by Monday, January 26, 
2026.
