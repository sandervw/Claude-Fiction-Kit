On March 17, 2020, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care.  The 
matter was scheduled for hearing on March 30, 2020, at 10:30 a.m.   
Claimant’s requested relief was authorization to proceed with Stephen A. Ash, 
M.D.’s recommended surgery to the right shoulder.  Prior to the commencement of the 
hearing, defendant agreed to authorize the surgery.  In light of the current global 
pandemic virus COVID-19, a Proclamation of Disaster Emergency was issued on 
March 26, 2020, by Governor Kim Reynolds.  This proclamation imposed a moratorium 
on “nonessential or elective surgeries and procedures that utilize personal protective 
equipment.”  See 
governor.iowa.gov/sites/default/files/documents/Public%20Health%20Proclamation%20
-%202020.03.26.pdf.  This moratorium is effective until April 16, 2020, “unless sooner 
terminated or extended in writing” by Governor Reynolds.  Id.   
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
1) Defendants agree to authorize the right shoulder surgery recommended by 
Dr. Ash as soon as practicable upon termination of the March 26, 2020, Proclamation of 
Disaster Emergency.   
2) Entry of this Consent Order does not prejudice the defendant’s right to 
authorize care pursuant to Iowa Code 85.27.
