On May 28, 2024, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care. Hearing 
was scheduled to take place on June 7, 2024. 
Prior to the scheduled hearing, counsel for the parties advised the undersigned 
that they had reached an agreement regarding the requested care. Given the 
agreement of the parties, the hearing was determined to be unnecessary. The parties 
requested entry of a consent order to confirm and document the agreement. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED:   
Per the agreement of the parties, defendants agree to set up an appointment for 
treatment of claimant’s left shoulder with Dr. John Hart at Physician’s Clinic of Iowa as 
soon as possible. 
The hearing scheduled for June 7, 2024 is cancelled.
