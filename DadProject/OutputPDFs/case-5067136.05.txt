On December 3, 2025, claimant filed a Petition for Alternate Medical Care. 
Hearing was scheduled to take place on December 16, 2025 at 1:00 p.m.. 
Prior to the scheduled hearing, counsel for the parties advised the undersigned 
that they had reached an agreement regarding claimant’s concerns relating to her 
ongoing, authorized care at Mayo Clinic. Given the agreement of the parties, the 
hearing was determined to be unnecessary. The parties requested entry of a consent 
order to confirm and document the agreement.   
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
The parties agree that claimant will continue to schedule her ongoing, authorized 
care with Mayo Clinic, and will further provide the appropriate person at the Mayo Clinic 
with the name and contact information of the adjuster who is to be billed for such 
causally related, authorized care. 
The hearing scheduled for December 16, 2025, is canceled. 
Signed and filed this _17th _ day of December, 2025.
