Claimant filed an Original Notice and Petition for Alternate Medical Care with this 
Agency on April 28, 2023.  
The Claimant has an accepted work injury to his lower back. Although surgery was 
recommended in May of 2021, the authorized surgeon has now retired. To date, 
Defendants have been unable to secure a surgeon agreeable to accept the Claimant as 
a patient and continue the recommended treatment. Claimant seeks an order requiring 
Defendants to authorize a new treating provider for evaluation of treatment options and 
to authorize such treatment as recommended.  
 
As the Defendants have been unable to secure a new provider, Claimant has 
recommended treatment be authorized by one of the following providers: Dr. John S. 
Treves, Dr. Ric E. Jensen, Dr. Stephen E. Doran, or Dr. Morgan T. LaHolt. Defendants 
have agreed to authorize and schedule further treatment with one of the proposed 
providers, with an appointment confirmed by no later than May 19, 2023.  
 
In the event none of the proposed providers can take over care of the Claimant, 
Defendants agree to expand the geographic search and authorize a new treating provider 
for the Claimant. Defendants agree to continue to seek treating providers at the 
recommendation of the Claimant.

To avoid an unnecessary hearing, and yes protect the interests of the clients, the 
parties agree to entry of a consent order, requiring Defendants to authorize treatment as 
described above.  
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
Pursuant to the agreement of the parties, Defendants agree to seek authorization 
with the recommended providers proposed by the Claimant, and, if no proposed providers 
are able to take over treatment for the Claimant, continue to pursue authorization with 
other recommended providers made by the Claimant, included expanded the 
geographical region for which treatment will be authorized. Claimant retains the right to 
file another petition for alternate medical care regarding this matter if necessary.
