On February 8, 2024, Susan Erlandson filed her third application for alternate 
care under Iowa Code section 85.27 and Iowa Administrative Code rule 876—4.48 for 
care relating to a work injury she sustained on January 25, 2022, for which the 
defendants, employer Don Hummer Trucking Corp. and insurance carrier West Bend 
Mutual Insurance Co., have accepted liability. Previously, the agency issued a consent 
order under No. 22009139.03 on December 5, 2023, memorializing the defendants’ 
agreement to arrange for care with a pain specialist at UnityPoint Allen Hospital. 
However, the appointment to which the parties agreed has yet to be scheduled. 
The process of arranging care for a workers’ compensation injury has taken time, 
and after communication with the Cedar Valley Pain Management Clinic, which is under 
UnityPoint Allen Hospital, and providing medical records and imaging, the defendants 
are submitting a final form on February 20, 2024, which should allow for the scheduling 
of an appointment. The delay caused Erlandson understandable frustration, so she filed 
the petition initiating this case, seeking an appointment with a pain specialist. After 
discussion before the scheduled hearing, the parties agreed that the defendants would 
schedule an appointment within fourteen days. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
The defendants shall schedule an appointment with Cedar Valley Pain 
Management within fourteen (14) days of the date of this order.
