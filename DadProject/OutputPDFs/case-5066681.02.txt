Claimant filed an application for alternate medical care on September 18, 2020, 
seeking authorization for (1) Mayo Pain Rehabilitation Center three-week program and 
(2) Cefaly device. The matter was scheduled for hearing on September 30, 2020.  
Prior to hearing, the parties contacted the undersigned Deputy and indicated that 
defendants have agreed to authorize the care requested by claimant.  
Claimant desired that a Consent Order be entered in this case, in lieu of a hearing. 
A hearing is therefore determined to be unnecessary, based on the agreement of 
the parties.  
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
Defendants are ordered to provide authorization for claimant to attend the Mayo 
Pain Rehabilitation Center three-week program and to provide claimant a Cefaly device.   
IT IS SO ORDERED.
