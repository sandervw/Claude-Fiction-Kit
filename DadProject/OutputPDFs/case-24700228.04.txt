Claimant filed an application for alternate medical care on October 4, 2024. The 
application sought a finding from this agency there was an abandonment of care due to 
an unreasonable delay in treatment. Claimant states defendant failed to schedule a 
neurological appointment within fourteen days as ordered in the Alternate Medical Care 
Decision issued on September 16, 2024. (File No. 24700228.03). 
Defendant does not dispute an appointment was unscheduled until October 9, 
2024, but states they moved with all possible speed to schedule the appointment and 
there was no unreasonable delay in scheduling treatment. 
Prior to the hearing, the parties advised the undersigned that an agreement to 
provide care had been reached. Defendant will authorize the following doctor as the 
treating neurologist for claimant’s work injury: 
Robert Struthers, M.D. 
PCI Medical Pavilion 2 
275 10th Street SE 
Cedar Rapids, IA 52403 
Defendant represents to this agency that an appointment has been scheduled for 
December 9, 2024, at 9:15 AM and this appointment will be authorized. The parties 
have requested a Consent Order be entered in lieu of a hearing. 
ORDER 
THEREFORE IT IS ORDERED: 
Claimant’s petition for alternate medical care is granted in part and denied in 
part.
