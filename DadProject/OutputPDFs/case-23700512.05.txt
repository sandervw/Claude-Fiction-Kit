On December 5, 2023, claimant filed an alternate care application against DJ 
Auto and Norguard Insurance.  A review of the record demonstrates this is the fourth 
application filed since July 2023.  Claimant sought to force authorization of an MRI 
recommended by the authorized physician. 
On December 18, 2023, defendants “received word that it [the MRI] has been 
authorized through Mercy Pain Management, and all we are waiting for is a date.”  
(Email from Defense Counsel, 12/18/23)  Claimant has requested a Consent Order and 
defendants agree with this plan. 
ORDER 
Defendants shall authorize, pay for and otherwise provide the MRI ordered by 
the authorized treating physician.
