Claimant filed an original notice and petition seeking alternate medical care.  An 
expedited hearing on that petition is scheduled for Wednesday, May 10, 2023.  The 
parties have notified the undersigned that they have reached an agreement that 
resolves the disputes involved in the impending hearing.   
The parties agree: 
Defendants are and have been attempting to facilitate the referral by Dr. 
Bingham for neurosurgical care and treatment. Defendants are working to set up 
an appointment with Dr. Igram but learned that a family emergency took him 
away from his clinic last week and this week. Defendants will provide 
appointment details with Dr. Igram as soon as they hear back from his clinic upon 
his return to the office. Should the Defendants be unable to secure appointment 
details with Dr. Igram within the next 14 days, Defendants will locate another 
provider for neurosurgical care and treatment. 
Pursuant to the parties’ agreement, it is so ordered. 
Signed and filed this 9th day of May, 2023.
