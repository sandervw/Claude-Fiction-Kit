This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, Daniel Rutherford.  An alternate 
medical care hearing was scheduled for October 20, 2025.  Prior to the hearing, the 
parties agreed via email that a consent order would be appropriate in lieu of a formal 
decision.   
 
The defendants agree to authorize and schedule surgery as recommended by 
Dr. Steven Aviles on August 26, 2024, and again by Dr. Andrew Thompson on 
September 10, 2025.  The defendants shall schedule the recommended surgery with an 
alternative doctor of the defendants’ choice within the next 45 days. 
 
The parties consented to the entry of a consent order via email on October 14, 
2025; thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
 
Should the defendants fail to abide by the stipulations in this consent order, 
sanctions may be imposed pursuant to 876 Iowa Administrative Code 4.36. 
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1. Within 45 days of the date of this order, the defendants shall authorize and 
schedule surgery, as recommended by Drs. Aviles and Thompson, with an 
alternative doctor of the defendants’ choice.
