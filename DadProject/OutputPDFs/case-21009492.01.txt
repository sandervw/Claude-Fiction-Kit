Claimant filed an application for alternate medical care on October 7, 2022, 
requesting that defendants be ordered to schedule an appointment for pain 
management or a neurosurgical evaluation. Hearing was scheduled for October 19, 
2022, at 10:30 a.m. 
Prior to hearing on October 19, 2022, the parties advised the undersigned that 
they had reached an agreement concerning Claimant’s Petition for Alternate Medical 
Care. The parties request a Consent Order be entered in this case, in lieu of a hearing.  
Defendants have authorized Justin Wikle, M.D., a pain management specialist at 
the University of Iowa Hospitals and Clinics. The parties agree that within fourteen (14) 
days of the date of this order, defendants will have an appointment date and time for 
claimant’s initial appointment with Dr. Wikle. Defendants will notify claimant’s counsel of 
the date and time of the appointment as soon as possible. A hearing is therefore 
determined to be unnecessary, based on the agreement of the parties. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
Within fourteen (14) days of the date of this order, defendants will have an 
appointment date and time for claimant to see Justin Wikle, M.D. Defendants will notify 
claimant’s counsel of the date and time of the appointment as soon as possible.
