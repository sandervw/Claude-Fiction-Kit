On May 8, 2025, Brittany Henagar filed a petition concerning application for 
alternate care under Iowa Code section 85.27 and Iowa Administrative Code rule 876—
4.48. The defendants, employer Crossroads, Inc., and insurance carrier SFM Mutual 
Insurance Co., answered, accepting liability for Henagar’s back injury. Counsel for the 
parties and the undersigned discussed the parties’ positions at the time of hearing and 
the framework for an agreement memorialized in a consent order took shape. Because 
of the parties’ agreement, the hearing was not needed. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) On or before June 10, 2025, the defendants shall schedule an appointment 
for Henagar with a neurosurgeon and inform her of the date, time, location, 
and provider. 
 
2) The telephone alternate care hearing in this case is CANCELLED. 
Signed and filed this 20th day of May, 2025.
