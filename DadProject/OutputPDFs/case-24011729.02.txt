This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, Ashley Sample.  An alternate medical 
care hearing was scheduled for May 22, 2025.  Prior to the hearing, the undersigned 
engaged in an email discussion with the parties with regard to the claimant’s requested 
medical care.  The parties agreed that a consent order was appropriate.  
 
 
The defendants consent and agree to arrange an appointment for a neurological 
examination.  The defendants are attempting to arrange for an appointment with a 
neurologist at the University of Iowa.  An appointment for a neurological examination 
shall be arranged within fifteen (15) days of the date of this order.  The appointment 
itself need not occur within fifteen (15) days of the date of this order. 
 
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  The parties consented to the entry of a consent order via email on May 
21, 2025, thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
 
 
 
ORDER 
 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
 
The parties have agreed that Defendants will arrange for an appointment for a 
neurological examination, including attempting to arrange for an appointment with a 
neurologist at the University of Iowa.
