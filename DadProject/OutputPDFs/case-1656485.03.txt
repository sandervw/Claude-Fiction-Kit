Claimant filed a petition seeking alternate medical care.  A telephonic hearing on 
this petition was scheduled for February 5, 2021.   
Claimant’s petition seeks treatment for her right shoulder with Dr. Patterson.  At 
the time of the scheduled hearing, the undersigned held a phone conference with 
counsel for each party.  Prior to the start of the hearing, the parties reached an 
agreement that negated the need for an alternate care hearing.  The parties consented 
to the following: 
Claimant requested an appointment with Dr. Patterson.  Defendants have agreed 
to schedule an appointment for Ms. Peterson with Dr. Patterson.  As soon as 
defendants have a date and time of the appointment for Ms. Peterson, they will provide 
that information to claimant’s counsel.   
Therefore, defendants shall promptly schedule an appointment for Ms. Peterson 
to see Dr. Patterson.
