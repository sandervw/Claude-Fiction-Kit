This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 IAC 4.48 is 
invoked by claimant, Tomasa Gomez.  The alternate medical care claim was scheduled 
for telephone hearing on October 14, 2020, at 1:00 p.m.   
Prior to the commencement of the hearing, defendants’ attorney filed exhibits 
indicating that defendants consent to provide medical care with Dr. William Samuelson 
from Tri State Specialists.  Defendants agreed to provide care for the low back injury 
asserted.  Due to the ongoing and evolving Covid-19 pandemic situation, the parties 
agreed that treatment with Dr. Samuelson would be scheduled as soon as practicable.   
The undersigned proposed this consent order in lieu of a formal hearing.  All 
parties consented to the entry of a consent order via e-mail, thus no alternate medical 
care hearing was necessary.   
ORDER 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
Defendants agree to authorize care with Dr. Samuelson.
