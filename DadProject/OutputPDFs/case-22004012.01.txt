Claimant filed a petition seeking alternate medical care on June 21, 2022.  A 
telephonic hearing on this petition was scheduled to occur on July 1, 2022 at 10:30 A.M.  
 
 
Prior to the alternate medical care hearing, the parties reached a resolution by 
consent.  Defendants have confirmed that they have authorized claimant to be seen by 
an occupational medicine physician for evaluation of her alleged mental health 
symptoms.  Defendants are currently attempting to schedule an appointment with 
occupational medicine.  Defendants agree to schedule claimant to be seen by 
occupational medicine as soon as possible, but not later than July 15, 2022. 
 
By reaching this agreement and consenting to this order, neither party waives 
any rights they may have in the future to address medical treatment. 
 
 
The parties desired that a consent order be entered in this case, in lieu of a 
hearing.  There is no justiciable issue for hearing and determination at this time.  Both 
parties agree that a consent order is appropriate, and a formal hearing is unnecessary 
to resolve the contested case dispute.    
 
 
IT IS SO ORDERED.
