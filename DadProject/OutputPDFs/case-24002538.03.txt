This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, William Lofquist.  Prior to proceeding 
with hearing on the alternate medical care claim on August 1, 2024, the defendants filed 
an answer and supporting documents indicating that the defendants were attempting to 
arrange for the requested care.  The undersigned reached out to the parties inquiring as 
to the feasibility of a consent order.  The parties indicated a desire to enter into a 
consent order. 
 
The defendants consent, stipulate, and agree to provide care to the claimant’s 
back.  As part of this, the defendants agreed to authorize care with Dr. Keith Kohout for 
a neurological evaluation.   
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order via e-mail on July 
31, 2024; thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The parties have agreed that defendants will authorize care for the claimant’s 
back, including authorizing a neurological evaluation with Dr. Keith Kohout in Des 
Moines, Iowa.   
 
The appointment with Dr. Kohout shall be scheduled within fourteen (14) days of 
the date of this order; however, the appointment itself may occur after this time.
