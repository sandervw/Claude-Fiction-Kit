This is a contested case, alternate medical care, proceeding under Iowa Code 
chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 IAC 4.48 is invoked by 
claimant, Chrystal Vinall-Williams.  The alternate medical care claim was scheduled for 
telephone hearing on March 31, 2020.   
Prior to the commencement of the hearing, defendants’ attorney indicated that 
defendants consent to provide additional medical care through Des Moines Orthopaedic 
Surgeons, either through the previously authorized surgeon or through a different 
provider at the clinic, if necessary.  Defendants agreed to provide care for the bilateral 
wrist and bilateral knee injuries asserted.  Defendants noted difficulties in 
communicating with the medical providers, which is likely due to the Covid-19 pandemic 
situation currently affecting Iowa.   
The undersigned proposed this consent order in lieu of a formal hearing.  All 
parties consented to the entry of a consent order and the alternate medical care hearing 
was cancelled. 
ORDER 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
Defendants shall communicate with Des Moines Orthopaedic Surgeons and 
schedule an evaluation with a qualified medical provider at that facility to evaluate and 
treat claimant’s bilateral wrists and bilateral knees.
