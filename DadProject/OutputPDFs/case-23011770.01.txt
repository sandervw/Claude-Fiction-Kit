CONSENT ORDER 
 
COME NOW the parties and hereby submit the instant Consent Order regarding the 
Alternate Medical Care Petition.  
1. The parties have agreed that Defendants will authorize care and treatment which is 
reasonable and causally related to Claimant’s work-related condition. Defendants 
agree to authorize an appointment with an orthopedic specialist, specifically Dr. 
Smith at Iowa Ortho. The parties agree that this appointment will be scheduled for a 
date as soon as possible. The appointment should be scheduled no later than October 
31, 2024, but the appointment itself does not need to occur before this date.

On October 11, 2024, the claimant applied for alternate care under Iowa Code section 85.27 and
Iowa Administrative Code rule 876—4.48. The agency scheduled the matter for a hearing to be
held October 23, 2024. On October 22, 2024, counsel for the parties notified the undersigned they
had reached an agreement regarding care and a hearing was no longer needed. On October 23,
2024, the parties jointly filed a proposed consent order memorializing that agreement.
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED:
1)  The parties' proposed consent order is hereby APPROVED AND ADOPTED.
2)  The hearing scheduled in this matter is CANCELLED.
