 On October 17, 2023, claimant filed petitions for alternate medical care 
requesting that claimant be allowed to have her lower left extremity and back/BAW 
evaluated and treated. A hearing was set for October 29, 2024, at 10:30 A.M.  
 
The parties have agreed that the defendant will authorize an appointment with 
Dr. Archer at his earliest availability and will authorize and pay for any referral to see an 
orthopedic specialist Dr. Archer may make. 
 
THEREFORE IT IS ORDERED, the claimant’s petition is GRANTED and 
claimant shall be seen by Dr. Archer at his earliest availability as well as any orthopedic 
specialist to whom Dr. Archer may refer claimant.
