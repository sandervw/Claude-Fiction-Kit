Claimant filed an Original Notice and Petition for Alternate Medical Care with this 
agency on July 8, 2025. 
 
Based on an agreement between the parties, and a proposed consent order, the 
parties agree that the alternate medical hearing in this matter scheduled for July 18, 
2025 at 10:30 a.m., is unnecessary as the parties have agreed to a Consent Order. 
Specifically, defendants have agreed to authorize the medications prescribed by Calli 
Snyder, DNP, at Mercy Occupational Health Prairie Creek on 5/29/2025 which include 
tramadol, diclofenac, and cyclobenzaprine. Defendants also have agreed to authorize 
the physical therapy and dry needling recommended by Calli Snyder, DNP, on 
5/29/2025. 
 
This consent order is not intended to reflect findings of fact or conclusions of law 
relative to this claim.  Rather, it is intended to document and enter an order that is 
consistent with both parties’ positions and acceptable to both parties.  A formal alternate 
medical care hearing was not conducted as all parties consented to entry of this order 
and waived the formal hearing. 
 
THEREFORE IT IS ORDERED: 
 
Pursuant to the agreement of the parties, defendants will authorize the 
prescribed medications, physical therapy and dry needling as recommended by the 
authorized treating provider.
