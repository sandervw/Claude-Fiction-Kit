On July 31, 2020, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care.  On August 
11, 2020, defendants filed an answer to the petition.  The matter was scheduled for 
hearing on August 12, 2020.   
Prior to the hearing, the parties confirmed that an agreement had been reached 
wherein defendants agreed to authorize the left shoulder surgery recommended by 
Matthew Bollier, M.D.  The parties reported that the agreement resolves the pending 
alternate medical care petition and requested entry of a consent order to confirm and 
document the agreement.   
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED:  
Defendants agree to provide the left shoulder surgery recommended by Dr. 
Bollier, which is in the process of being scheduled.
