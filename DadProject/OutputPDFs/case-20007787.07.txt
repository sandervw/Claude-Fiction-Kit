CONSENT ORDER 
 
This Alternate Medical Care hearing took place on Monday, October 18, 2021.  
Prior to going on the record, the parties came to an agreement as follows:  Claimant will 
undergo the FCE recommended by Dr. Johnson and further, follow-up with Dr. Johnson 
to discuss future medical treatment options.  If Dr. Johnson makes a treatment 
recommendation, then the Claimant shall continue his treatment with Dr. Johnson.  
However, if Dr. Johnson has nothing further to offer, then the Claimant will be allowed to 
seek a second opinion with Kyle Galles, M.D.
