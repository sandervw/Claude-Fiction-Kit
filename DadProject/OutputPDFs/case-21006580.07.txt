Claimant filed an Original Notice and Petition for Alternate Medical Care with this 
agency on September 9, 2022.  
Based on a conference call with counsel for both parties, and a proposed 
consent order, defendants agree to pay all outstanding bills with Sheri Roggy, O.D. on 
or before October 5, 2022. If the bills are not paid by that time, defendants agree to 
authorize and pay for treatment for claimant with Vision in Motion in Cedar Rapids, 
Iowa. 
To avoid an unnecessary hearing, and yet protect the interests of their clients, 
the parties agree to entry of a consent order, requiring defendants to pay all outstanding 
bills with Sheri Roggy, O.D. on or before October 5, 2022. If the bills are not paid by that 
time, defendants agree to authorize and pay for treatment for claimant with Vision in 
Motion in Cedar Rapids, Iowa. 
This consent order is not intended to reflect findings of fact or conclusions of law 
relative to this claim. Rather, it is intended to document and enter an order that is 
consistent with both parties’ positions and acceptable to both parties. A formal alternate 
medical care hearing was not conducted, as all parties consented to entry of this order 
and waived the formal hearing. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
Pursuant to the agreement of the parties during the scheduled September 21, 
2022, alternate medical care hearing, defendants shall pay all outstanding bills with
