The undersigned presided over an alternate medical care hearing on November 6, 
2025 at 10:30 a.m. Claimant was represented by attorney Nicholas Shaull, while 
defendants were represented by attorney James Nubel. Testimony was offered by 
claimant, Megan Miller. Upon the conclusion of testimony, the parties consented to an 
order reflecting that defendants would provide the following home modifications: 
 
 Defendants will install a color-changing, temperature-sensitive faucet in claimant’s 
kitchen sink. (Claimant’s Exhibits 5 and 9) 
 
 Defendants will install a handrailing inside the front entrance to claimant’s home.  
           (Claimant’s Exhibit 6; Defendants’ Exhibit 9) 
 
 Defendants will install a one-inch rubber threshold ramp from the kitchen to the 
back patio. (Claimant’s Exhibit 7) 
 
 Defendants will install a grab bar that drops down from the wall by the toilet in 
claimant’s bathroom. (Claimant’s Exhibits 8 and 10)  
 
 Defendants will install a color-changing, temperature-sensitive faucet in claimant’s 
bathroom. (Claimant’s Exhibit 10) 
 
 Defendants agree to install the above home modifications within one (1) month of 
receipt of the associated materials (e.g., faucets).
