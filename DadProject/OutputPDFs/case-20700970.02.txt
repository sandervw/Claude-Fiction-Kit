On November 4, 2020, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care.  The 
matter was scheduled for hearing on November 18, 2020.  Samuel Aden appeared for 
claimant and Eric Lanham appeared for defendants. 
At the time of the hearing, the parties confirmed that an agreement had been 
reached wherein defendants have agreed to provide medical treatment for claimant’s 
work injury.  The parties reported that the agreement resolves the pending alternate 
medical care petition and requested entry of a consent order to confirm and document 
the agreement.   
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED:  
Defendants shall, within the next ten (10) days, authorize medical care 
reasonably suited to treat the injury or condition alleged by claimant without undue 
inconvenience to the claimant.
