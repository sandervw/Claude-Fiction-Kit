On May 22, 2023, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care. Defendants 
filed an answer on June 1, 2023, admitting liability for the claim. Hearing was scheduled 
to take place on June 2, 2023. 
 
Prior to the time of hearing, the parties contacted the undersigned and indicated 
they had reached an agreement regarding the requested care. Given the agreement of 
the parties, the hearing was determined to be unnecessary. The parties requested entry 
of a consent order to confirm and document the agreement. 
 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED:   
 
Within 10 days of the date of this order, defendants will pay any outstanding 
balances owed for claimant’s past treatment at Pain Specialists of Iowa. Additionally, 
within 10 days of the date of this order, defendants will authorize, make payment 
arrangements, and schedule an appointment for pain management at Pain Specialists of 
Iowa for claimant’s cervical spine injury and related musculoskeletal pain. 
 
Signed and filed this    2nd    day of June, 2023.
