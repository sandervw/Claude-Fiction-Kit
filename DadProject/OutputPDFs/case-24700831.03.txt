On March 31, 2025, claimant filed an original notice and petition for alternate 
medical care in this file under Iowa Code section 85.27, invoking the provisions of rule 
876 IAC 4.48. Claimant sought an order authorizing him to return to the authorized 
treating physician for treatment of his ongoing left wrist symptoms. 
 
Hearing was scheduled for April 9, 2025. Shortly before hearing, the parties 
contacted the undersigned to advise they had reached an agreement concerning the 
claimant’s request. Due to the parties’ agreement, the hearing was deemed 
unnecessary. The parties requested a consent order to document and record their 
agreement. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
 
The parties agree that defendants will contact Steindler Orthopedic Clinic within 
one week of the date of this order to confirm their authorization of a return visit for 
claimant to be seen by Thomas Ebinger, M.D., and for the appointment to be 
scheduled.
