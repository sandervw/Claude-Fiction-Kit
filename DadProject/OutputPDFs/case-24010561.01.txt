Claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care on January 28, 2025.  This 
matter was set for a telephone hearing on February 6, 2025 at 8:30 A.M. 
 
 
Prior to the hearing, the parties reached a resolution by consent.  Specifically, 
Defendants have authorized Claimant to be evaluated by a board-certified orthopedic 
spine surgeon regarding her October 6, 2024 work injuries.  Defendants shall ensure 
that Claimant's appointment is scheduled and confirmed at the earliest available date 
and no later than February 12, 2025. 
 
By reaching this agreement and consenting to this order, neither party waives 
any rights they may have to address medical treatment in the future. 
 
 
The parties agreed to the undersigned issuing this consent order confirming the 
agreement reached. 
 
 
IT IS SO ORDERED.
