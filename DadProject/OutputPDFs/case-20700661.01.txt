This alternate medical care matter was scheduled for hearing on August 5, 2020.  
Prior to hearing, the parties confirmed that an agreement had been reached wherein 
defendants will authorize and schedule a second opinion with an orthopedic knee 
specialist for claimant’s right knee as specifically ordered by Waqas Hussain, M.D., on 
January 28, 2020.  The parties reported to the undersigned that the agreement resolves 
the pending alternate medical care petition and requested entry of a consent order to 
confirm and document the agreement.   
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED  
Defendants agree to provide a second opinion with an orthopedic knee specialist 
for claimant’s right knee as specifically ordered by Dr. Hussain on January 28, 2020. 
Signed and filed this        6th       day of August, 2020.
