On October 9, 2025, Claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care.  The 
matter is scheduled for hearing on October 20, 2025, at 10:30 a.m. 
 
 
Claimant’s requested relief is for the defendants to transfer care to Florida, where 
she now resides.  Prior to the hearing, defendants agreed to authorize the requested 
care in Orlando, FL, and will schedule an authorized appointment as soon as possible. 
 
 
The parties report that the authorized care resolves the pending alternate 
medical care petition and request entry of a consent order to confirm and document the 
agreement. 
 
 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
 
 
Defendants agree to provide the authorized care requested in claimant’s 
alternate medical care petition.
