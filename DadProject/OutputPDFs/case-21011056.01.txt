This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, Kenneth Haley.  The alternate medical 
care claim proceeded with a telephone hearing on August 30, 2022, at 10:30 a.m.   
 
After discussing their positions, the parties discussed the entry of a consent order 
in this matter.  When the claimant returned to work, he returned to another employer, 
represented as Paramount Millwright Services.  The defendants requested additional 
time to investigate whether the claimant sustained a further injury caused by his 
subsequent employment.  The defendants indicated if no connection was found 
between the claimant’s subsequent employment and his current complaints, they would 
be inclined to proceed with the recommendations of the treating physician, Dr. Hussain, 
regarding the right shoulder.  The defendants consent, stipulate, and agree to promptly 
investigate the potential liability of Paramount Millwright Services, and decide within two 
weeks of the date of this order whether they will accept or deny the recommendations of 
Dr. Hussain.   
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order verbally on August 
30, 2022, thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.
