This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, Richard Sheehan.  Prior to proceeding 
with hearing on the alternate medical care claim on September 16, 2024, the parties 
reached out to the undersigned expressing the desire for the undersigned to enter a 
consent order. 
 
The defendants consent, stipulate, and agree to authorize and provide pain 
management treatment to the claimant. 
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  The parties consented to the entry of a consent order; thus no 
alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The parties have agreed that defendants will authorize care for the claimant in 
the form of pain management treatment.
