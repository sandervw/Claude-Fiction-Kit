Claimant filed an original notice and petition for alternate medical care on June 
21, 2024.  The agency scheduled this case for an alternate medical care hearing on 
July 2, 2024.  At the commencement of hearing, defendants noted that they would 
consent to entry of an order granting claimant’s request for evaluation and treatment 
through an orthopaedic surgeon in either Memphis, Tennessee or Nashville, 
Tennessee.  Claimant’s counsel confirmed that the defendants’ offer to entry of a 
consent order was sufficient to eliminate any further disputed issues in this alternate 
medical care proceeding.  Given this agreement between the parties, the undersigned 
did not engage in a formal hearing and no evidence was received. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED:  
Defendants shall timely authorize an evaluation and treatment by an orthopaedic 
surgeon in either Memphis, Tennessee or Nashville, Tennessee.
