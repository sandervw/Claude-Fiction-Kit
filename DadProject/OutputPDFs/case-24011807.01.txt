Claimant filed an application for alternate medical care on November 21, 2025. 
Hearing was scheduled to take place on December 8, 2025. 
On December 5, 2025, the parties confirmed that an agreement had been 
reached that resolves the pending alternate medical care petition, and requested entry 
of a consent order to confirm and document the agreement. A hearing is therefore 
determined to be unnecessary, based on the agreement of the parties.  
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
Defendants have contacted medical providers to schedule evaluations for 
claimant and await the dates of the evaluations. Pursuant to the agreement of the 
parties, defendants’ counsel will provide claimant’s counsel with the dates of an 
evaluation with Dr. Amy Mooney and Dr. Howard by December 12, 2025. If dates are 
not provided by that time, claimant has the right to refile the petition for alternate 
medical care. 
The hearing scheduled for December 8, 2025 is cancelled.
