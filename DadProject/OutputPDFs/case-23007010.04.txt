On September 3, 2025, claimant filed an application for alternate medical care 
under Iowa Code section 85.27 and rule 876 IAC 4.48. A hearing was held on 
September 15, 2025. All parties were given proper notice. 
 
Claimant sustained an injury on June 19, 2023, to his mental health, which arose 
out of and in the course of employment. Liability was established in the uncontested 
hearing decision issued on July 14, 2025. Claimant is dissatisfied with the medical 
treatment offered by the defendants. 
 
The hearing was recorded via digital voice recorder and a complete decision was 
dictated into the record on the day of the hearing. The decision will be reproduced in 
typewritten form only if the decision is appealed, and the parties are advised to follow 
the procedures outlined under the administrative code. Any rights of appeal will run from 
the date the decision was dictated into the record. 
 
The deputy ordered that claimant’s petition for alternate medical care be 
GRANTED. The decision was rendered orally on the record. 
 
Pursuant to a standing order of delegation of authority by the workers’ 
compensation commissioner pursuant to Iowa Code section 10A.305, the undersigned 
enters this decision for the workers’ compensation commissioner. There is no right of 
appeal of this decision to the workers’ compensation commissioner. Appeal of this 
decision, if any, would be by judicial review pursuant to Iowa Code section 10A.
