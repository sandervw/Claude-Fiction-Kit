On February 28, 2024, Jasminka Husic applied for alternate care under Iowa 
Code section 85.27 and Iowa Administrative Code rule 876—4.48. The defendants, 
employer Omega Cabinetry a/k/a Masterbrand Cabinets, Inc. and insurance carrier 
American Zurich Insurance Company, answered on February 29, 2024, accepting 
liability for the injury. The agency scheduled the matter for a telephone alternate care 
hearing to be held on March 11, 2024. The parties emailed the undersigned on March 8, 
2024, because they had reached an agreement on care and requested a consent order 
memorializing their agreement. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) Within thirty (30) days, the defendants shall authorize and arrange for an 
evaluation to occur promptly at a reasonable time and place by: 
a. Justin Wikle, M.D., at University of Iowa Hospitals & Clinics (UIHC); 
and 
b. A psychiatrist as referred by Joseph A. Buckwalter, M.D., of UIHC. 
2) The telephone alternate care hearing scheduled in this case is CANCELLED.
