This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, James Raney, II.  An alternate medical 
care hearing was scheduled for October 14, 2025.  Prior to the hearing, the parties 
agreed via email that a consent order would be appropriate in lieu of a formal decision.   
 
The defendants agree to promptly authorize treatment with a physician to 
address the claimant’s complaints regarding his left shoulder and left hip.   
 
The parties consented to the entry of a consent order via email on October 14, 
2025; thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
 
Should the defendants fail to abide by the stipulations in this consent order, 
sanctions may be imposed pursuant to 876 Iowa Administrative Code 4.36. 
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1. The defendants shall promptly authorize treatment with a physician to 
address the claimant’s complaints regarding his left shoulder and left hip.
