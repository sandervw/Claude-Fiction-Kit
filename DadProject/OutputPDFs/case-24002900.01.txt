Claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care on July 31, 2025.  This matter 
was set for a telephone hearing on August 13, 2025 at 10:30 A.M. 
 
Prior to the August 13, 2025 hearing, the parties reached a resolution by 
consent.  Specifically, Defendants have authorized and will coordinate scheduling for 
Claimant to undergo evaluation and treatment with a pain specialist at the University of 
Iowa Hospitals and Clinics.  Defendants agree to schedule Claimant’s initial 
appointment as soon as possible. 
 
By reaching this agreement and consenting to this order, neither party waives 
any rights they may have to address medical treatment in the future. 
 
The parties agreed to the undersigned issuing this consent order confirming the 
agreement reached. 
 
IT IS SO ORDERED.
