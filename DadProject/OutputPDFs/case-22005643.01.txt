On February 1, 2023, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care. No 
written answer has been filed. The matter was scheduled for hearing on February 13, 
2023. 
On February 6, 2023, the parties contacted the undersigned and indicated they 
have reached an agreement regarding the requested care. Defendants have agreed to 
authorize the surgery that has been recommended by Dr. William Jacobson. This is the 
care being sought by claimant.   
Given that the defendants have agreed to authorize the requested care, the 
hearing was determined to be unnecessary. Claimant requested entry of a consent 
order to confirm and document the agreement, and defendants agreed. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED:   
Defendants have agreed and are ordered to authorize the surgery that has been 
recommended by Dr. William Jacobson.   
The hearing scheduled for February 13, 2023 is cancelled.
