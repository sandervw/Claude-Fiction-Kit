Claimant filed two petitions seeking alternate medical care.  A consolidated 
telephonic hearing on these petitions was scheduled for Friday, December 9, 2022.  
Attorney James Ballard appeared on behalf of the claimant.  Claims specialist, Julie 
Blanchard, of Corvel appeared as the third-party administrator for the employer and 
insurance carrier.   
Prior to going on the record, the parties informed the undersigned that they had 
an agreement, which would satisfy claimant’s alternate medical care request.  
Specifically, defendants offered to schedule and authorize a second opinion evaluation 
at Capital Orthopaedics & Sports Medicine, P.C., in Des Moines, Iowa.  Claimant is 
agreeable to that offer for care. 
The parties having reached an amicable resolution, the undersigned concluded 
that a hearing was not necessary.  Instead, the undersigned proposed a consent order 
to document the above agreement.  All parties agreed to entry of the consent order in 
lieu of a hearing.  Accordingly, the December 9, 2022 hearing was cancelled. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
Defendants shall authorize a second opinion orthopaedic evaluation to occur at 
Capital Orthopaedics & Sports Medicine, P.C.
