On January 26, 2022, claimant filed an application for alternate medical care.  On 
February 4, 2022, defendant filed an answer.  The matter was scheduled for hearing on 
the 7th day of February, 2022.   
Prior to the hearing, the parties confirmed that an agreement had been reached 
wherein defendant agreed to authorize the requested care.  The parties reported that 
the agreement resolves the pending alternate medical care petition and requested entry 
of a consent order to confirm and document the agreement.   
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
The hearing scheduled for February 7, 2022, at 10:30 a.m. is cancelled. 
Defendant has agreed to authorize the care requested. 
Signed and filed this __4th __ day of February, 2022.
