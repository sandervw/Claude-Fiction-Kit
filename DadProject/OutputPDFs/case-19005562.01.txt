On January 20, 2021, Cody Chapman filed a petition concerning application for 
alternate care. The agency scheduled the matter for hearing on February 1, 2021. The 
defendants filed their answer prior to the hearing on February 1, 2021. 
Before the hearing, the defendants arranged care with Dr. Jasper. At the time of 
hearing, the parties informed the undersigned that the arranged care eliminated the 
need for a hearing. The parties agreed that a consent order was appropriate. They also 
pledged open communication regarding care. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
1) The defendants agree to provide care with Dr. Jasper. 
2) The parties are encouraged to maintain open lines of communication 
regarding the scheduling process and transportation for care.
