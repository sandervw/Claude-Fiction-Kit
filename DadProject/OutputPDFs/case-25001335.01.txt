On January 29, 2025, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care. Hearing 
was scheduled to take place on February 7, 2025. 
Prior to the scheduled hearing, claimant’s attorney advised that the parties had 
reached an agreement regarding the requested care and filed a proposed consent 
order. Given the agreement of the parties, the hearing was determined to be 
unnecessary. The parties requested formal entry of the consent order to confirm and 
document the agreement. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED:   
Per the agreement of the parties, Defendants agree to authorize ORA to treat 
claimant’s work-related conditions, including physical therapy that has already been 
recommended. Claimant requests the authorization be made by February 10, 2025. 
The hearing scheduled for February 7, 2025 is cancelled. 
Failure to comply with this order may result in sanctions under Iowa 
Administrative Code rule 876—4.36.
