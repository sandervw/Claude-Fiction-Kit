On July 22, 2022, Ramsey Frangul applied with the agency for alternate care 
under Iowa Code section 85.27 and agency rule 876 IAC 4.48 in the form of a referral to 
an ear nose and throat (ENT) specialist for alleged hearing loss and tinnitus caused by 
employment. The agency scheduled the matter for a telephone alternate care hearing to 
be held on August 4, 2022. The defendants, employer Black Cat Blades LLC and 
insurance carrier Accident Fund General Insurance Company, filed an answer on 
August 3, 2022, asserting they had approved the referral to an ENT specialist while 
continuing to investigate causation. The parties notified the undersigned by email they 
had agreed to a consent order regarding care in lieu of a hearing. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) The defendants shall authorize reasonable care with an ENT specialist for 
Frangul’s alleged work injuries. 
 
2) The alternate care hearing scheduled in this case is CANCELLED. 
Signed and filed this 4th day of August, 2022.
