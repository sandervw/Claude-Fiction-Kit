The parties came before the undersigned for an alternate medical care hearing on April 
12, 2023 at 10:30 a.m. The Claimant, Dallas Thurman, was represented by counsel, Nicholas 
Shaull. The Defendant, City of Des Moines-Parks Department, Self-Insured Employer, was 
represented by Molly Tracy.  
 
Claimant, through counsel, filed his Application for Alternate Medical Care on March 31, 
2023, identifying body parts affected, including left shoulder, physical-mental, and BAW (body 
as a whole), while seeking treatment specifically for mental health, recommended on March 21, 
2023 by the authorized providers at the University of Iowa, Dr. James Nepola, M.D. Defendant, 
through counsel, filed their answer on April 10, 2023 admitting liability for the left shoulder, 
mental/physical, and BAW.   
 
Prior to hearing on April 12, 2023, and before going on the record, counsel for the parties 
and the undersigned had a discussion regarding the underlying care matters currently at issue in 
this case. Counsel for Defendant confirmed that Defendant is admitting liability for the mental 
health and has been in the process of locating a mental health provider that accepts workers’ 
compensation patients and would see Claimant. As such, given that Defendant does not dispute
