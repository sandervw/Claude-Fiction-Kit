Claimant filed a petition seeking alternate medical care.  A telephonic hearing on 
this petition was held on November 27, 2023.  Prior to commencing a formal recording 
and record, the undersigned had discussions with counsel of record.  Ultimately, it was 
determined that defendants are willing to authorize the medical treatment requested by 
claimant.  The parties consented to entry of this consent order.   
Specifically, defendants agree to pay, or reimburse claimant, for crutches 
recommended by Blair Runde, P.A.  Defendants agree to provide further medical care 
for claimant’s low back at the University of Iowa and specifically with a spine surgeon.  
Defendants have already identified Cassim Igram, M.D., as a possible spine surgeon 
and sent him medical records to review.  However, Dr. Igram has not yet agreed to 
evaluate claimant and an appointment is not yet scheduled with Dr. Igram.  Defendants 
agree that if Dr. Igram declines to accept treatment, they will identify another qualified 
spine surgeon at the University of Iowa to provide care for claimant’s low back. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
Pursuant to the agreement of the parties at the November 27, 2023 alternate 
medical care hearing, defendants shall pay, or reimburse claimant (if already 
purchased), for crutches. 
Within thirty (30) days of the entry of this order, defendants shall schedule an 
appointment for claimant to be evaluated by Cassim Igram, M.D., or another qualified
