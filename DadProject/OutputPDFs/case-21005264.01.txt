On October 20, 2021 claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care. The 
matter was scheduled for hearing on November 1, 2021.   
At the time of the hearing, the parties confirmed that an agreement had been 
reached wherein defendants authorized Dr. Nelson to perform the recommended low 
back surgery that is currently scheduled for November 9, 2021.  
The parties reported that the agreement resolves the pending alternate medical 
care petition and requested entry of a consent order to confirm and document the 
agreement.   
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED  
Defendants agree they have authorized Dr. Nelson to perform the recommended 
low back surgery that is currently scheduled for November 9, 2021. 
At the time of this consent order and continuing through surgery, defendants 
agree that Dr. Nelson remains the authorized treating physician.
