Claimant filed an application for alternate medical care for file number 
24004579.01 on August 22, 2025. Claimant filed a second application for alternate 
medical care for file number 25007095.01 on August 27, 2025. The applications were 
consolidated for hearing to take place on September 3, 2025.   
Prior to the hearing, the parties confirmed that an agreement had been reached 
that resolves the pending alternate medical care petitions, and requested entry of a 
consent order to confirm and document the agreement. A hearing is therefore 
determined to be unnecessary, based on the agreement of the parties.  
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
Pursuant to the agreement of the parties, defendants shall assign a nurse case 
manager (NCM) to claimant’s case. The NCM will contact claimant and schedule a time 
to meet claimant at the Iowa Ortho Urgent Care clinic downtown. The meeting between 
the NCM and claimant at the Iowa Ortho Urgent Care clinic downtown shall take place 
no later than Friday, September 5, 2025. The NCM will request that Iowa Ortho Urgent 
Care conduct an orthopedic examination of claimant and order an urgent MRI of 
claimant’s lumbar spine. The lumbar MRI will be completed as soon as possible, 
preferably the same day, via the MRI located at Iowa Ortho’s main facility 
downtown. Defendants shall then authorize Dr. Rosenthal at Iowa Ortho, or authorize 
another orthopedic spine surgeon or neurosurgeon if Dr. Rosenthal is unavailable or 
unwilling, to evaluate and/or treat claimant’s work-related lumbar spine injury. The 
evaluation by an orthopedic spine surgeon or neurosurgeon shall occur as soon as 
possible.
