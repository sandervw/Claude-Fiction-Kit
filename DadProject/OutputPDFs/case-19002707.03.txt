This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 IAC 4.48 is 
invoked by claimant, Mitchell Proehl.  The alternate medical care claim was scheduled 
for telephone hearing on September 4, 2020, at 1:00 p.m.   
 
Prior to commencement of the hearing, the claimant indicated that the 
defendants’ authorized treatment to the left upper extremity.  Claimant further noted that 
the left upper extremity treatment was no longer at issue.  Defendants’ attorney 
indicated that defendants consent to provide the requested medical care via a surgical 
procedure as recommended to the left knee.  Due to the ongoing and evolving Covid-19 
pandemic situation, the parties agreed that the surgery would be scheduled as soon as 
practicable.   
 
The undersigned proposed this consent order in lieu of a formal hearing.  All 
parties consented to the entry of a consent order and the alternate medical care hearing 
was canceled.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
Defendants agree to authorize a left knee surgical procedure.
