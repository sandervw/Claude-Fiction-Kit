This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, Carmela Alva.  On December 2, 2024, 
the parties reached out to the undersigned via e-mail indicating that the defendant 
agreed to authorize the care requested by the claimant.  The parties indicated that they 
wished to enter into a consent order rather than proceed with a hearing on the issue. 
 
The defendant consents, stipulates, and agrees to authorize the claimant to 
proceed with work hardening physical therapy at Rock Valley Physical Therapy.  The 
defendant also agreed to schedule the authorized therapy as soon as possible, but no 
later than December 16, 2024.   
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order via e-mail on 
December 2, 2024; thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The parties have agreed that defendant will authorize the claimant to proceed 
with work hardening physical therapy at Rock Valley Physical Therapy.   
 
The defendant shall schedule work hardening physical therapy at Rock Valley 
Physical Therapy as soon as possible, but no later than December 16, 2024.
