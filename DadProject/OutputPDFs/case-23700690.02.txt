Claimant filed an Original Notice and Petition for Alternate Medical Care with this 
agency on February 13, 2024. 
Based on an agreement between the parties, and a proposed consent order, 
defendants agree to authorize and pay for the following care: pain management 
treatment with Dr. Klein at Iowa Ortho; treatment with Dr. Schmitz at Iowa Ortho; 
physical therapy at Rehab Visions; mental health counseling with Travis Semmens, 
LISW; and mental health medication management with Angela Thompson, ARNP, at 
MercyOne Indianola.  Defendants authorize MercyOne Indianola to manage and 
prescribe medications to treat claimant’s mental health issues and authorize Iowa Ortho 
to manage and prescribe medications to treat claimant’s October 14, 2022, back injury. 
Defendants have authorized physical therapy with Rehab Visions for the October 14, 
2022, injury. Defendants scheduled an appointment with Dr. Klein at Iowa Ortho for 
February 27, 2024.  Claimant will contact Travis Semmens LISW, and MercyOne 
Indianola directly to schedule his appointments. 
To avoid an unnecessary hearing, and yet protect the interests of their clients, 
the parties agree to entry of a consent order, requiring defendants to authorize 
treatment as described above. 
This consent order is not intended to reflect findings of fact or conclusions of law 
relative to this claim. Rather, it is intended to document and enter an order that is 
consistent with both parties’ positions and acceptable to both parties. A formal alternate
