This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by the claimant.  Prior to proceeding with the 
alternate medical care contested case proceeding on May 1, 2024, the parties reached 
out to the undersigned and indicated a desire to enter into a consent order. 
 
The defendants have confirmed that they have authorized the claimant to follow-
up with Dr. Meloy for a return visit.  At the time of this order, the defendants were 
attempting to schedule the claimant’s initial appointment with Dr. Meloy.  The 
defendants agreed to schedule the claimant’s initial appointment with Dr. Smith within 
two weeks of the date of this order. 
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order thus no alternate 
medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
That the Defendants will authorize a follow-up visit with Dr. Meloy as noted 
herein.
