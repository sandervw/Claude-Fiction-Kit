This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A. The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, Lindsey Miller. An alternate medical 
care hearing was scheduled for June 9, 2025. Prior to the hearing, the undersigned 
engaged in an email discussion with the parties with regard to the claimant’s requested 
medical care. The parties also engaged in a discussion electronically following the 
scheduled time for the hearing. The parties agreed that a consent order was 
appropriate. 
The defendants consent and agree to arrange for the provision of a Circaid 
Profile lower leg with outer jacket, size 3/regular length. The defendants agree to 
authorize the aforementioned item within five (5) days of the date of this order. The 
defendants agree to order the Circaid Profile lower leg with outer jacket, size 3/regular 
length within twenty (20) days of the date of this order. 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision. The parties consented to the entry of a consent order verbally on June 
9, 2025, thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued. 
Should the defendants fail to abide by the stipulations in this consent order, 
sanctions may be imposed pursuant to 876 Iowa Administrative Code 4.36.
