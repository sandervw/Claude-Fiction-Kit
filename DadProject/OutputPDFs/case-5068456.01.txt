Claimant filed an original notice and petition seeking alternate medical care.  
Hearing for the alternate medical care request was scheduled for May 17, 2021.  Prior 
to the commencement of hearing, the parties reached an agreement regarding the 
request for alternate medical care.  
Specifically, defendants consented to authorize the right, total knee replacement 
recommended by Craig R. Mahoney, M.D.  Defendants proposed entry of a consent 
order in lieu of a formal hearing.  Claimant acknowledged the offer of care was 
sufficient.  Claimant similarly acknowledged that entry of a consent order was sufficient 
without the formal necessity of an alternate medical care hearing. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED:  
The May 17, 2021 alternate medical care hearing is cancelled. 
Defendants shall promptly authorize the recommended right total knee 
replacement with Dr. Mahoney.
