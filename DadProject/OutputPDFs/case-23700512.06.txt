On January 20, 2025, claimant filed a Petition for Alternate Medical Care. 
Hearing was scheduled to take place on January 30, 2025. 
Prior to the scheduled hearing, counsel for the parties advised the undersigned 
that they had reached an agreement regarding the requested care. Defendants agree to 
send claimant to Dr. Abernathey for a surgical consultation in addition to Dr. Tallman for 
pain psychological treatment.  Given the agreement of the parties, the hearing was 
determined to be unnecessary. The parties requested entry of a consent order to 
confirm and document the agreement.   
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
Defendants agree to authorize a surgical consultation with Dr. Abernathey and 
treatment with Dr. Tallman.
