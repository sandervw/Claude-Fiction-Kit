Claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care on August 7, 2023.  This matter 
was set for a telephone hearing to be held on August 17, 2023 at 1:00 P.M. 
 
 
Prior to the August 17, 2023 hearing, the parties reached a resolution by consent.  
Defendants have authorized Claimant to be seen for his right knee at UnityPoint Health 
– Allen Occupational Health located at 7024 Nordic Drive, Cedar Falls, Iowa 50613.  
Defendants have scheduled Claimant to be seen by Erin Kennedy, M.D. at Allen 
Occupational Health on August 23, 2023 at 11:30 A.M. 
 
 
By reaching this agreement and consenting to this order, neither party waves any 
rights they may have in the future to address medical treatment. 
 
 
The parties agreed to the undersigned issuing this consent order confirming the 
agreement reached.
