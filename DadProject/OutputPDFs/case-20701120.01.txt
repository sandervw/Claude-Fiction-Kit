On November 25, 2020, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care.  The 
matter was scheduled for hearing on December 10, 2020.  Joseph Powell appeared for 
claimant and Lindsey Mills appeared for defendants. 
At the time of the hearing, the parties confirmed that an agreement had been 
reached wherein defendants have agreed to provide medical treatment for claimant’s 
work injury.  The parties reported that the agreement resolves the pending alternate 
medical care petition and requested entry of a consent order to confirm and document 
the agreement.   
The defendants have authorized Kyle Galles, M.D., to provide a second opinion 
and possible treatment pursuant to Iowa Code section 85.27 regarding the claimant’s 
left shoulder. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED:  
Defendants shall, provide medical care agreed to above without undue 
inconvenience to the claimant.
