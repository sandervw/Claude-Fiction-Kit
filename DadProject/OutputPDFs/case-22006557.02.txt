On June 10, 2024, claimant filed an original notice and petition for alternate 
medical care in this file under Iowa Code section 85.27, invoking the provisions of rule 
876 IAC 4.48. Claimant sought an order that defendants approve a home IFC unit, per 
the recommendation of claimant’s physical therapist. 
Hearing was scheduled for June 20, 2024. Prior to hearing, the parties advised 
they had reached an agreement with respect to the requested care, making the hearing 
unnecessary. The parties requested a consent order to document and record their 
agreement. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
The parties agree therapist Jamie Blum, DPT, has recommended a home IFC 
unit for claimant, and it is the parties’ understanding that Dr. Music’s office will be 
providing a written prescription for the unit to be ordered. Upon receipt of the 
prescription, defendants agree to promptly order and pay for a home IFC unit to be 
delivered to claimant’s home or therapist’s office. As such, the parties agree there is no 
need for a hearing.     
The hearing scheduled for June 20, 2024 is cancelled.
