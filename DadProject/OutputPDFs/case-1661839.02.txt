This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 IAC 4.48 is 
invoked by claimant, Greg Wolf.  The alternate medical care claim was scheduled for 
telephone hearing on June 13, 2022, at 8:30 a.m.   
 
On June 10, 2022, the parties and the undersigned discussed the entry of a 
consent order in this matter.  The defendants consent, stipulate, and agree to authorize 
a medication consult with either Mr. Wolf’s primary care provider or another medical 
practitioner.   
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal hearing.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order via e-mail on June 
10, 2022; thus no alternate medical care hearing is necessary.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The parties have agreed that Defendants authorize a medication consult with 
either Mr. Wolf’s primary care provider, or another medical practitioner.
