On September 6, 2023, Draeger filed an application for alternate care under Iowa 
Code section 85.27 and agency rule 876 IAC 4.48, requesting treatment for an injury to 
her left lower extremity. The defendants, Amazon.com, and LM Insurance Corp., filed an 
answer on September 18, 2023, admitting liability for claimant’s left leg injury. The hearing 
in this matter was scheduled for September 19, 2023, at 10:30 a.m.  
 
 
At the start of the hearing the parties indicated they had reached an agreement 
concerning claimant’s requested care. Under the parties’ agreement, defendants have 
agreed to authorize the left knee arthroscopy recommended by William Jacobson, M.D., 
the authorized provider for this claim. The parties requested a consent order from the 
agency memorializing the agreement in lieu of a hearing and determination of the 
application.   
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED:  
 
1. Defendants shall authorize the surgery recommended by Dr. Jacobson, the 
authorized provider. 
 
2. The alternate care hearing scheduled in this matter is cancelled.
