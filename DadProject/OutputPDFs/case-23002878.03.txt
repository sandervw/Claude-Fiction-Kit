This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, Carmela Alva.  On January 19, 2025, 
the parties reached out to the undersigned indicating that they wished to enter into a 
consent order rather than proceed with a hearing on the issue.  
 
The defendant consents, stipulates, and agrees to authorize the claimant to 
return to ORA Orthopedics for her left shoulder.  The defendant also agreed to schedule 
the authorized treatment as soon as possible, but no later than January 31, 2025. 
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  The parties consented to the entry of a consent order via e-mail on 
January 19, 2025; thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The parties have agreed that defendant will authorize the claimant to proceed 
with return treatment with ORA Orthopedics.   
 
The defendant shall schedule treatment as soon as possible, but no later than 
January 31, 2025
