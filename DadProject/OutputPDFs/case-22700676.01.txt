On July 8, 2022, Angelica Alva filed an application for alternate care under Iowa 
Code section 85.27 and agency rule 876 IAC 4.48. The agency scheduled the case for 
a telephone alternate care hearing to be held on July 20, 2022. The parties jointly filed a 
proposed consent order on July 19, 2022, that outlines the agreement they have 
reached regarding the care which is reasonable and causally related to Alva’s work-
related conditions as recommended by the authorized medical provider, Dr. Rhea Allen. 
Because of the parties’ agreement, a hearing is no longer necessary. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) The defendants shall authorize care and treatment which is reasonable and 
causally related to Alva’s work-related conditions as recommended by Dr. 
Allen in the form of physical therapy that is consistent with Dr. Allen’s June 
28, 2022 order, which will be reviewed by Dr. Allen at Alva’s next follow-up 
visit. 
 
2) The telephone alternate care hearing scheduled in this case is CANCELLED. 
Signed and filed this 19th day of July, 2022.
