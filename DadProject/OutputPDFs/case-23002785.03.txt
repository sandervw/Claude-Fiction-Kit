On June 24, 2025, Claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care. The matter 
is schedule for hearing on July 3, 2025, at 10:30 a.m. 
Claimant’s requested relief is for ongoing physical therapy and an in-home 
stationary bike as recommended by authorized provider, Mark Mysnyk, M.D. Prior to the 
hearing date, Defendants authorized the requested care. 
The parties report that the authorized care resolves the pending alternate 
medical care petition and request entry of a consent order to confirm and document the 
agreement. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
Defendants agree to provide the authorized care requested in Claimant’s 
alternate medical care petition.
