 Claimant filed a petition for alternate care seeking treatment. Prior to the 
hearing, the parties worked out an agreement regarding the requested care. In doing 
so, Defendant indicated no dispute with regard to the authorized prescription and 
agreed to provide any assistance needed in order to ensure Claimant receives the 
authorized medication. The Defendant will provide a prescription card to the petitioner to 
facilitate filling of prescriptions from authorized treating physicians.  
 
ORDER

The claimant’s petition for alternate medical care is granted. Defendant shall 
authorize the treatment they have agreed upon.
