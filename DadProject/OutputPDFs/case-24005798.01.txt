On June 26, 2024, Paul Huyck applied for alternate care under Iowa Code 
section 85.27 and Iowa Administrative Code rule 876—4.48. The defendants, employer 
Weiler, Inc., and insurance carrier Continental Casualty Co., did not file an answer. The 
agency scheduled the matter for a hearing to be held on July 9, 2024, at 10:30 a.m. The 
parties notified the undersigned on July 8, 2024, that the hearing was no longer needed 
because they reached an agreement regarding care. They requested that the agency 
issue a consent order detailing their agreement in lieu of a hearing. 
 
Huyck requested alternate care in the form of a carpal tunnel release surgery 
recommended by Dr. ZeHui Han. The defendants have authorized the right carpal 
tunnel release surgery recommended by Dr. Han. The parties are awaiting the surgery 
to be scheduled by Iowa Ortho. Therefore, an alternate care hearing and decision are 
no longer needed. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) The defendants shall provide reasonable care in accordance with the parties’ 
agreement detailed above. 
 
2) The alternate care hearing scheduled in this case is CANCELLED.
