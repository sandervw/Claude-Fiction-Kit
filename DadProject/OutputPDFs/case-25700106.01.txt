This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, Channing Ponxs, III.  An alternate 
medical care hearing commenced on February 10, 2025.  After taking testimony from 
the claimant, the defendants agreed to authorize medical treatment as requested by the 
claimant in their petition.  The parties agreed that a consent order was appropriate.  
 
The defendants consent, stipulate, and agree to authorize treatment including an 
L5-S1 posterior lumbar decompression instrumented fusion with likely TLIF as 
recommended by Dr. Trevor Schmitz.  The defendants also agreed to authorize the 
recommended surgery within ten (10) days of the date of this order. 
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  The parties consented to the entry of a consent order verbally on 
February 10, 2025; thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The parties have agreed that Defendants will authorize surgical treatment as 
recommended by Dr. Trevor Schmitz. 
 
The defendants shall authorize surgery as soon as possible, but no later than ten 
(10) days from the date of this order.
