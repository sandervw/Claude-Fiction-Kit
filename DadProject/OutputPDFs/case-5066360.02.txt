On June 10, 2022, Craig Wiese applied for alternate care under Iowa Code 
section 85.27 and agency rule 876 IAC 4.48 in the form of authorization of the reverse 
total left shoulder arthroplasty as recommended by authorized treated physician Dr. 
Justin Khoriaty at Iowa Ortho. Before the hearing, the defendants, employer Lennox 
Industries, Inc. and insurance carrier Indemnity Insurance Company of North America, 
agreed to authorize the procedure. The defendants also filed an answer, averring they 
had authorized the requested care. The parties then agreed to a consent order 
memorializing their agreement. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) The defendants shall authorize care in accordance with the parties’ 
agreement. 
 
2) The alternate care hearing is CANCELLED. 
 
3) Nothing in this order shall be interpreted to infringe on the defendants’ rights 
regarding care for the work injury under Iowa Code section 85.27.
