Claimant filed an application for alternate medical care on August 19, 2020, 
seeking authorization for treatment of her left ankle condition by a podiatrist or 
orthopedic surgeon in the Waterloo area. The matter was scheduled for hearing on 
August 31, 2020.  
Prior to hearing, the parties contacted the undersigned Deputy Commissioner 
and indicated that defendants have agreed to authorize and schedule an evaluation of 
claimant by podiatrist Dr. Travis Tidwell in Waterloo, Iowa. Claimant is satisfied with this 
authorization of care by defendants.  
Claimant desired that a Consent Order be entered in this case, in lieu of a 
hearing. 
A hearing is therefore determined to be unnecessary, based on the agreement of 
the parties.  
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
Defendants are ordered to provide written authorization to Dr. Travis Tidwell, 
authorizing him to evaluate claimant, within the next ten (10) days. 
Signed and filed this ___21st ____ day of August, 2020.
