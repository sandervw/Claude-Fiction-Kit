On November 17, 2022, Young filed an application for alternate medical care 
under Iowa Code section 85.27 and agency rule 876 IAC 4.48, requesting treatment for 
an alleged low back injury.  The defendants, Premium Plant Services, Inc., and Everest 
National Insurance Company, filed an answer on the morning of December 2, 2022, 
admitting liability for claimant’s low back injury.  The hearing in this matter was 
scheduled for December 2, 2022, at 10:30 a.m.  
At the start of the hearing the parties indicated they had reached an agreement 
concerning claimant’s requested care.  Under the parties’ agreement, defendants have 
consented to authorize appointments for claimant at the University of Iowa Hospitals 
and Clinics (UIHC), specifically an appointment with Katie Sander, ARNP on December 
12, 2022, and an appointment with Matt Howard, M.D., on January 31, 2023, or sooner 
if another appointment date becomes available or it becomes necessary.  Defendants 
have also agreed to authorize prescriptions from Heather Bingham, M.D., at UIHC for 
Gabapentin and Diclofenac.  The parties requested a consent order from the agency 
memorializing the agreement in lieu of a hearing and determination of the application.   
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
1) Defendants shall authorize appointments for claimant at UIHC, specifically an 
appointment with Katie Sander, ARNP on December 12, 2022, and an 
appointment with Dr. Howard on January 31, 2023, or sooner if another 
appointment date becomes available or it becomes necessary; and
