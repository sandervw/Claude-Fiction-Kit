Case No. CVCV012778 
JUDGMENT ON REMAND 
 
Respondents, Serta/National Bedding Co., LLC (“Serta”) and Safety National Casualty 
Corp., timely appealed the Ruling on Petition for Judicial Review that the Court entered on  
April 11, 2022 (the “Ruling”).  After the Court of Appeals affirmed the Ruling, the Supreme 
Court granted further review, reversed the decision made by this Court, and remanded the case 
with instructions.  Procedendo issued from the Supreme Court on February 17, 2024. 
 
Consistent with the instructions of the Supreme Court, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED AND 
ADJUDGED THAT the Petition for Judicial Review filed by Lorri Hagen on June 10, 2021, is 
denied.  The Appeal Decision issued by Workers’ Compensation Commissioner Joseph S. 
Cortese II on May 17, 2021, is affirmed in its entirety. 
 
All of the costs on judicial review in this action are assessed against Petitioner.
