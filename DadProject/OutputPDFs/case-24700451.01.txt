On June 4, 2024, claimant filed an original notice and petition for alternate 
medical care in this file under Iowa Code section 85.27, invoking the provisions of rule 
876 IAC 4.48. Claimant sought an order that defendants direct care under Iowa Code 
section 85.27 within ten days of the hearing. On June 11, 2024, defendants filed an 
answer. The answer stated that defendants were unable to admit or deny liability 
pending an evaluation with an occupational medicine physician. 
Hearing was scheduled for June 12, 2024. Prior to hearing, the undersigned 
issued an order of dismissal, due to liability being at issue. 
Shortly after the order of dismissal was filed, the parties contacted the 
undersigned and a short telephone conference took place. Defendants agreed to accept 
liability for the alleged injuries, and have scheduled an appointment for claimant with an 
occupational health physician to take place on Friday, June 14, 2024. As such, the 
parties requested a consent order to document their agreement and clarify the record. 
The order of dismissal has been rescinded. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
Defendants have now accepted liability for the claim. Per the agreement of the 
parties, defendants have scheduled an appointment with an occupational health 
physician to take place on Friday, June 14, 2024.           
The hearing scheduled for June 12, 2024 is cancelled.
