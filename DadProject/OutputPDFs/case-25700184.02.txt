This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, Christopher Edmonds.  An alternate 
medical care hearing was scheduled for September 8, 2025.  Prior to the hearing, the 
parties reached out to the undersigned and proposed that a consent order be issued to 
memorialize the agreement of the parties. 
 
The defendants agree to promptly authorize care for the claimant’s right shoulder 
with Dr. John Langland.   
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  The parties consented to the entry of a consent order via email on 
September 8, 2025; thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
 
Should the defendants fail to abide by the stipulations in this consent order, 
sanctions may be imposed pursuant to 876 Iowa Administrative Code 4.36. 
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1. The defendants will authorize care for the claimant’s right shoulder with Dr. 
John Langland.
