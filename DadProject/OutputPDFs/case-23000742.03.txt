 In order to resolve the issues raised in the January 14, 2025 Petition for 
Alternate Care filed by Claimant, the parties agree as follows: 
 
1. Dr. Hahn concluded that Claimant’s need for a repeat EMG does not relate to 
her work injury.   
 
2. Accordingly, Defendants do not authorize a repeat EMG.   
 
3. Dr. Hahn concluded that Claimant needs a cervical spine MRI to further 
evaluation the source of her bilateral lower extremity complaints.   
 
4. Accordingly, Defendants authorize a cervical spine MRI.   
 
5. Defendants authorize additional treatment appointment(s) with Dr. Hahn, for 
treatment of her work injury.   
 
6. Defendants have agreed to authorize a mental health evaluation for 
Claimant.
