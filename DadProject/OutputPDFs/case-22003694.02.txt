This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, Areej Alebeed.  The alternate medical 
care claim proceeded with a telephone hearing on August 24, 2022, at 10:30 a.m.   
 
After discussing their positions, and a recently received x-ray report, the parties 
discussed the entry of a consent order in this matter.  The defendants consent, 
stipulate, and agree to send the right shoulder x-ray report dated July 30, 2022, to a 
mutually agreed upon orthopedic provider. 
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order verbally on August 
24, 2022; thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The parties have agreed that Defendants will send the July 30, 2022, right 
shoulder x-ray report to a mutually agreed upon orthopedic provider.
