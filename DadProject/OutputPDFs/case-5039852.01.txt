On March 5, 2020, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care.  The matter 
is scheduled for hearing on March 18, 2020, at 10:30 a.m. 
Claimant’s requested relief was authorization of Dr. Phu’s recommended 
injections as well as payment of bills from Des Moines Acupuncture Clinic.  Prior to the 
hearing date, defendants authorized Dr. Phu’s injections.  The undersigned was 
reassured by defense counsel and his legal assistant that the acupuncture bills would 
be paid; however, to date, they are still outstanding. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
Defendants agree that the acupuncture bills will be paid and that any future care 
recommended by Dr. Phu will be authorized.
