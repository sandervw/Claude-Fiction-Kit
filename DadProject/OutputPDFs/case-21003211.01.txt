STATEMENT OF THE CASE 
The claimant, Rene Hardy, filed a petition for arbitration and seeks workers’ 
compensation benefits from PR Waukee LLC, d/b/a Pizza Ranch, employer, and Liberty 
Mutual, insurance carrier.  The claimant was represented by James Hoffman.  The 
defendants were represented by James Nubel. 
The matter came on for hearing on September 6, 2022, before Deputy Workers’ 
Compensation Commissioner Joe Walsh in Des Moines, Iowa via Zoom 
videoconferencing system.  The matter was recorded through the agency’s computer 
software.  The claimant did not appear for the hearing.  The week prior to the hearing, 
claimant’s counsel indicated via email that claimant had “disappeared.”  The parties did 
not submit a prehearing report, nor any exhibits.  On the record, claimant’s counsel 
indicated that his only evidence of a work injury was the claimant’s testimony, who was 
unavailable. 
ISSUES 
The parties submitted the following issues for determination: 
1. Whether this matter should be dismissed.

FINDINGS OF FACT 
The claimant has presented no evidence of any work injury or entitlement to any 
benefits under Chapter 85. 
CONCLUSIONS OF LAW 
The claimant bears the burden of proving a work injury.  Claimant has failed to 
meet his burden of proof. 
ORDER 
THEREFORE IT IS ORDERED 
The claimant shall take nothing. 
Each party shall pay their own costs.
