On June 3, 2024, Jeffrey Moss applied for alternate care under Iowa Code 
section 85.27 and Iowa Administrative Code rule 876—4.48. The defendants, employer 
Koch Fertilizer, LLC, and insurance carrier Old Republic Insurance Company, did not 
file an answer. The agency scheduled the matter for a telephone alternate care hearing 
to be held June 12, 2024. 
On June 11, 2024, counsel for the parties emailed the undersigned because they 
had reached an agreement regarding care. Because of their agreement, they felt a 
hearing was no longer necessary. They requested a consent order memorializing their 
agreement. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) The defendants shall: 
a) Send a check in the amount of nine hundred fifty-seven and 00/100 
dollars ($957.00) to Vision Care Clinic for Moss’s spectacles;  
b) Schedule with the University of Iowa Hospitals & Clinics a neuro-
ophthalmology appointment for Moss and notify claimant’s counsel of 
the date and time within ten (10) days of the date of this order; and 
2) As part of the parties’ agreement memorialized in this order, the defendants 
shall not raise an authorization defense for Moss’s videonystagmogram 
testing at Iowa Ear Center on June 14, 2024.
