Claimant’s Original Notice and Petition Concerning Application for Alternate 
Care, filed May 2, 2022, comes before this Deputy Iowa Workers’ Compensation 
Commissioner, Amanda Rutherford on May 12, 2022.  Regarding alternate care, the 
parties have come to an agreement and hereby stipulate and agree to the following 
Consent Order in relation to claimant’s medical treatment.  
Defendants will authorize an evaluation or evaluations by Megan Hart-
Fernandez, ARNP, for purposes of determining whether any medications are medically 
necessary. Megan Hart-Fernandez will initiate such prescription medications as may be 
medically necessary, and defendants will be responsible for the costs of said 
prescriptions. However, until such time as a duality of therapy is medically necessary, 
defendants will not authorize Megan Hart-Fernandez to engage in therapy, as Don 
Damsteegt, PhD., is the authorized doctor and there has been no showing of 
dissatisfaction with the care provided by Dr. Damsteeg. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
The hearing scheduled for May 12, 2022 is cancelled.  
Defendants agree to authorize an evaluation or evaluations by Megan Hart-
Fernandez for purposes of determining whether any medications are medically 
necessary, and Megan Hart-Fernandez will initiate prescriptions for those medications;
