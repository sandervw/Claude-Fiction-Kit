On October 16, 2020, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care. The 
matter is scheduled for hearing on October 27, 2020, at 1:00 p.m. Claimant’s requested 
relief was a virtual appointment with Dr. Jacobson or an orthopedic physician near 
where claimant resides. Prior to the hearing date, defendants have indicated they are in 
the process of scheduling an appointment with Dr. Stuart Goldsmith, in Brandon, 
Florida. The parties report that the authorized care resolves the pending alternate 
medical care petition and request entry of a consent order to confirm and document the 
agreement.  
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED defendants agree to provide the authorized care 
requested in claimant’s alternate medical care petition.
