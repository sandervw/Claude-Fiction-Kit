The Claimant filed an Application for Alternate Medical Care on November 1, 2022. 
The Claimant is seeking authorization for treatment with an orthopaedic doctor at 
University of Iowa Hospitals and Clinics as recommended by Dr. Chelli/Concentra.   
 
Hearing on the Application for Alternate Medical care was set for November 14, 
2022, however, the parties mutually agreed prior to the hearing commencing that the 
requested care be authorized.   
 
Based upon the foregoing, IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
 
That the Defendants authorize treatment by an orthopaedic doctor at the University 
of Iowa Hospitals and Clinics.  Defendants shall arrange an appointment in the very near 
future without further delay.
