On January 21, 2025, claimant Karen Fisher applied for alternate care under 
Iowa Code section 85.27 and Iowa Administrative Code rule 876—4.48 for an alleged 
work injury to her right shoulder and arm. The defendant, self-insured employer Trinity 
Health Corp d/b/a MercyOne Waterloo (Trinity) answered, accepting liability for the work 
injury affecting Fisher’s right shoulder and arm. The agency scheduled the matter for a 
telephone alternate care hearing to be held on January 30, 2025. 
Before the scheduled hearing, the parties notified the undersigned by an email 
from defense counsel that they had reached an agreement on care that made the 
hearing unnecessary. They requested a consent order memorializing the agreement. 
After finalizing their agreement, the parties followed up with proposed draft language. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) Trinity shall authorize Dr. Knudson to evaluate and treat Fisher’s right 
shoulder condition and promptly schedule an appointment. 
 
2) The alternate care hearing scheduled in this matter is CANCELLED. 
Signed and filed this 30th day of January, 2025.
