On March 8, 2021, the claimant filed a petition concerning application for 
alternate care with the agency. The claimant sought authorization of magnetic 
resonance imaging (MRI) of the lumbar spine. Defense counsel appeared without filing 
an answer. The alternate care hearing was ultimately scheduled for March 22, 2021. 
Prior to hearing, the parties discussed the claimant’s care and came to an 
agreement. The defendants have agreed to authorize an MRI and follow-up care with 
Dr. Schmitz at Iowa Ortho. The parties also agreed that, due to the defendants’ 
authorization of the MRI and follow-up care, the alternate care hearing in this matter 
was not needed.  
 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
1) The defendants have agreed to authorize an MRI and follow-up care with Dr. 
Schmitz at Iowa Ortho. 
 
2) Because of the parties’ agreement regarding care, the alternate care hearing 
scheduled in this matter is no longer necessary and is therefore cancelled.
