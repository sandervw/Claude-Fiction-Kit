On January 2, 2025, claimant filed a Petition for Alternate Medical Care. Hearing 
was scheduled to take place on January 13, 2025.  
 
Prior to the scheduled hearing, counsel for the parties advised the undersigned 
that they had reached an agreement regarding the requested care.  Given the 
agreement of the parties, the hearing was determined to be unnecessary.  The parties 
requested entry of a consent order to confirm and document the agreement.  
 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
 
Defendants agree to authorize treatment and will schedule an appointment within 
the next seven (7) days at the University of Iowa Hospitals and Clinics with Matthew 
Bollier, M.D.  
 
The hearing scheduled for January 13, 2025, at 10:30 a.m., is cancelled.
