Claimant filed a petition seeking alternate medical care on July 13, 2022.  A 
telephonic hearing on this petition was scheduled to occur on July 26, 2022, at 10:30 
AM.  Prior to going on the record, counsel for Limited Brands and Safety National 
Casualty Corp. notified the undersigned that her clients had agreed to authorize the 
requested medical care.  The parties agreed that a consent order should be entered.   
 
With respect to the specific alternate medical care requested, claimant sought 
authorization to treat with Cedric Ortiguera, M.D. at the Mayo Clinic in Jacksonville, 
Florida.  Defendants have agreed to authorize the requested medical treatment.  
Defendants have notified the Mayo Clinic of the same.   
The parties desired that a consent order be entered in this case, in lieu of a 
hearing.  After discussion between the parties and an agreement to provide the 
requested care, there is no justiciable issue for hearing and determination.  Both parties 
agree that a consent order is appropriate, and a formal hearing is unnecessary to 
resolve the contested case dispute.   
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
Pursuant to the agreement of the parties prior to the scheduled July 26, 2022, 
alternate medical care hearing, defendants shall authorize and arrange medical 
treatment for claimant’s left knee injury with Dr. Cedric Ortiguera at the Mayo Clinic in 
Jacksonville, Florida.
