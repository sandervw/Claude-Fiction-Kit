On March 25, 2021, claimant filed an application for alternate medical care.  On 
April 6, 2021, defendant filed an answer.  The matter was scheduled for hearing on April 
7, 2021.   
Prior to the hearing, the parties confirmed that an agreement had been reached 
wherein defendant agreed to refer claimant to an appropriate provider for evaluation 
and treatment related to the claimed mental health sequelae.  Defendant is currently 
working to secure an appointment.  The parties reported that the agreement resolves 
the pending alternate medical care petition and requested entry of a consent order to 
confirm and document the agreement.   
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
The hearing scheduled for April 7, 2021, at 10:30 a.m. is cancelled. 
Defendant agreed to provide a referral to an appropriate provider for evaluation 
and treatment related to the claimed mental health sequelae. 
Defendant shall continue to make all reasonable efforts to schedule an 
appointment for claimant as soon as possible.   
Defendant will provide an update to claimant within 14 days of the date of this 
order, providing the date of the appointment, or an update as to the progress in securing 
an appointment. 
Signed and filed this __7th ___ day of April, 2021.
