On April 23, 2025, Amanda Locke filed a petition concerning application for 
alternate care under Iowa Code section 85.27 and Iowa Administrative Code rule 876—
4.48. The agency scheduled the case for a telephone hearing to be held on May 5, 
2025. The defendants, employer Schneider Electric USA and insurance carrier AIU 
Insurance Company, did not file an answer.  
 
Counsel for the parties notified the undersigned by email before the scheduled 
hearing that they have reached an agreement regarding the care at issue. Defendants 
have agreed to authorize an appointment for Locke to be seen by Dr. Evan Larson at 
Steindler Orthopedic Clinic. Because of the parties’ agreement on care, the hearing 
scheduled in this case is no longer needed. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) Defendants shall promptly authorize and arrange the appointment for Locke 
to be seen by Dr. Evan Larson at Steindler Orthopedic Clinic. 
 
2) The telephone alternate care hearing is CANCELLED. 
Signed and filed this 5th day of May, 2025.
