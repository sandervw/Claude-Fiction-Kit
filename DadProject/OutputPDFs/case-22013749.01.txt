                    CONSENT ORDER  
          RE: ALTERNATE MEDICAL CARE 
 
 
 
 
On November 29, 2023, claimant filed an application for alternate medical care 
under Iowa Code section 85.27 and agency rule 876 IAC 4.48, requesting that 
defendants be ordered to authorize care for her left shoulder condition and authorize 
additional testing for claimant’s right shoulder condition.  The alternate care hearing was 
scheduled for December 8, 2023, at 10:30 a.m.  
 
Prior to hearing, defendants agreed to authorize the requested treatment.  
Specifically, defendants consented to authorize Dr. Nepola as the treating physician for 
claimant’s right shoulder condition and will grant authorization for the right shoulder 
injection as he recommended.  Defendants also agree to authorize Dr. Nepola to 
evaluate the claimant’s left shoulder condition, which includes Dr. Nepola’s 
recommendations for additional testing of the left shoulder. 
 
The parties desired that a Consent Order be entered in this case, in lieu of a 
hearing.  A hearing is therefore determined to be unnecessary, based on the agreement 
of the parties.  
 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED THAT: 
 
 
Defendants shall authorize Dr. Nepola as the treating physician for claimant’s 
right shoulder condition and grant authorization for his recommended treatment of a 
right shoulder injection. Defendants will also authorize Dr. Nepola to evaluate the 
claimant’s left shoulder condition, including Dr. Nepola’s recommendations for additional 
testing of the left shoulder. 
 
 
IT IS SO ORDERED.
