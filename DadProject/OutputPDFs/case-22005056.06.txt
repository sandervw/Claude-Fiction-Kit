This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, Joshua Schuchhardt.  Prior to 
proceeding with the alternate medical care hearing on March 19, 2024, the parties 
reached out to the undersigned indicating a desire to enter into a consent order. 
 
The defendants have authorized the claimant to receive physical therapy 
treatment at Schofield Chiropractic, including the use of an Alter-G anti-gravity treadmill 
for cardiovascular endurance and gait training as recommended by Dr. Femino at UIHC.   
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision; thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The parties have agreed that defendants will authorize physical therapy care at 
Schofield Chiropractic, including the use of an Alter-G anti-gravity treadmill for 
cardiovascular endurance and gait training.
