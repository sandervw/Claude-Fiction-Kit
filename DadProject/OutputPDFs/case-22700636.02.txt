On August 12, 2022, claimant James Anderson applied for alternate care under 
Iowa Code section 85.27 and agency rule 876 IAC 4.48 for an alleged May 31, 2022 
work injury to his left shoulder. The agency scheduled a telephone alternate care 
hearing for August 25, 2022. Before the hearing, the parties notified the undersigned by 
email they had reached an agreement regarding care and a hearing was no longer 
necessary. They also requested a consent order reflecting their agreement. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) The defendants shall authorize the left-shoulder surgery recommended by 
Jason Stanford, D.O., in accordance with the parties’ agreement. 
 
2) Because of the parties’ agreement regarding care, the alternate care hearing 
scheduled in this case is CANCELLED. 
Signed and filed this 25th day of August, 2022.
