This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, Donnette Barber.  Prior to proceeding 
with the alternate medical care contested case proceeding on April 8, 2024, the 
undersigned spoke to the parties on the phone.  The parties indicated a desire to enter 
into a consent order. 
 
The defendants consent, stipulate, and agree to provide care to the left hip and 
left upper extremity.  As part of this, the defendants agreed to continue to authorize in-
home physical therapy as recommended by Mark Fish, D.O.  The defendants also 
agreed to authorize and provide in-home health care for the claimant.   
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order verbally on April 8, 
2024; thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
That the defendants will continue to authorize in-home physical therapy as 
recommended by Dr. Fish. 
 
That, not more than seven (7) days from the date of this order, the defendants 
will provide in-home health care to the claimant, as recommended by Dr. Fish.
