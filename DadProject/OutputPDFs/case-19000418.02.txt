On December 1, 2020, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care.  On 
December 4, 2020, defendants filed their answer to the petition.  The matter was 
scheduled for hearing on the December 11, 2020. 
At the time of the hearing, the parties confirmed that an agreement had been 
reached wherein defendants would authorize all care, including testing, recommended 
by Ryan Brimeyer, D.O.  Defendants indicated they are presently working to schedule 
the testing and care recommended by Dr. Brimeyer but are experiencing delays due to 
COVID-19. The parties are encouraged to maintain open lines of communication 
regarding the scheduling process.  
The parties reported that the agreement resolves the pending alternate medical 
care petition and requested entry of a consent order to confirm and document the 
agreement.   
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED:  
Defendants agree to provide all testing and care recommended by Dr. Brimeyer.
