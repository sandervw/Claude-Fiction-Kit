Claimant filed an application for alternate medical care on June 2, 2023 requesting 
Defendants be ordered to authorize treatment with Dr. Justin Elwood.  
 
Prior to hearing, the parties contacted the undersigned Deputy and indicated 
Defendants have authorized treatment with Dr. Justin Elwood, where an appointment has 
now been scheduled for June 19, 2023 at 10:00 a.m. 
 
Claimant desired that a Consent Order be entered in this case, in lieu of a hearing. 
 
A hearing is therefore determined to be unnecessary, based on the agreement of 
the parties.  
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED THAT: 
 
Defendants shall authorize and pay for the appointment scheduled with Dr. Justin 
Elwood on June 19, 2023 at 10:00 a.m. and shall not withdraw authorization in advance 
of that appointment.
