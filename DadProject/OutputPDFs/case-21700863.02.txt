Claimant filed a petition seeking alternate medical care.  A telephonic hearing on 
this petition was scheduled for Friday, March 18, 2022.  Prior to the hearing, defendants 
filed an answer and addendum to the answer.  In their addendum, defendants 
authorized certain medical treatment.  Claimant conceded the offered care was 
sufficient to satisfy and resolve the disputes in this alternate medical care proceeding if 
put into a consent order. 
The undersigned concluded that a hearing was not necessary given the above 
concessions.  Accordingly, the March 18, 2022 hearing was cancelled and defendants 
consented to entry of this order. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
Defendants shall authorize a disability evaluation by Dr. Rigoberto Marin. 
Defendants shall authorize a referral to a neurologist. 
If willing to evaluate claimant and accept claimant as a patient, defendants shall 
authorize either Dr. Igor Cherches or Dr. Stephen Croft, as the evaluating and treating 
neurologist.
