This alternate medical care proceeding came before the undersigned for a 
hearing on Friday, April 28, 2023.  Counsel for both parties participated via telephone 
conference call.  Prior to initiating a formal recording of the proceedings, counsel held 
an informal conversation with the undersigned and notified the undersigned the parties 
could enter into an agreement pertaining to the request for further medical care. 
Specifically, the parties each consent to entry of an order that directs defendant 
to promptly authorize further treatment with Chad D. Abernathey, M.D., in Cedar 
Rapids, Iowa.  To the extent that Dr. Abernathey may decline the request for a follow-up 
appointment with claimant, the parties consent to an order requiring defendant to 
authorize and schedule an evaluation with Shirley Pospisil, M.D. at WorkWell in Cedar 
Rapids, Iowa. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
Pursuant to the agreement of the parties, defendant is ordered to promptly 
authorize further treatment and an appointment with Chad D. Abernathey, M.D., 
in Cedar Rapids, Iowa. 
Should Dr. Abernathey decline or be unable to provide further evaluation 
or treatment of claimant, defendant shall promptly authorize treatment and 
schedule an evaluation for claimant to be evaluated by Shirley J. Pospisil, M.D. 
at WorkWell in Cedar Rapids, Iowa.
