This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 IAC 4.48 is 
invoked by claimant, Kelly Blake.  The alternate medical care claim was scheduled for 
telephone hearing on July 13, 2020, at 8:30 a.m.   
Prior to the commencement of the hearing, defendants’ attorney indicated that 
defendants consent to provide the requested medical care via a second opinion with 
Brent Overton, M.D.  Defendants agreed to provide care for the neck and body as a 
whole injuries asserted.  Prior to scheduling the second opinion, the defendants 
requested the treatment notes from the claimant’s June 16, 2020, office visit with her 
primary care provider.  The claimant agreed.  Due to the ongoing and evolving Covid-19 
pandemic situation, the parties agreed that the second opinion appointment with 
Dr. Overton would be scheduled as soon as practicable after receipt of the requested 
records.   
The undersigned proposed this consent order in lieu of a formal hearing.  A brief 
record was made to confirm agreement on the above issues.  All parties consented to 
the entry of a consent order and the alternate medical care hearing was cancelled.   
ORDER 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
Claimant shall provide defendants with treatment and/or office visit notes from 
the claimant’s June 16, 2020, office visit with her primary care provider.
