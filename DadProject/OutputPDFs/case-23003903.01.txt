On April 14, 2023, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care. No written 
answer has been filed. The matter was scheduled for hearing on April 26, 2023. 
On April 25, 2023, the parties contacted the undersigned and indicated they have 
reached an agreement regarding the requested care. Defendants have agreed to 
authorize a mental health treatment evaluation with a mental health provider, as 
detailed below.   
Given the agreement of the parties, the hearing was determined to be 
unnecessary. The parties requested entry of a consent order to confirm and document 
the agreement. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED:   
Defendants agree to authorize a mental health treatment evaluation with a 
mental health provider, and will provide appointment details for the first-available 
evaluation with Dr. Brooke by May 1, 2023. If defendants are unable to get an 
appointment set up with Dr. Brooke, they will find an alternative mental health provider 
and have appointment details to claimant by May 1, 2023.   
The hearing scheduled for April 26, 2023 is cancelled.
