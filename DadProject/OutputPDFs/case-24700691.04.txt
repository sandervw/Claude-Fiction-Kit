On December 17, 2024, Mark Greeley filed an application for alternate care 
under Iowa Code section 85.27 and Iowa Administrative Code rule 876—4.48. The 
agency issued an order setting the case for a telephone hearing on December 30, 2024. 
On December 27, 2024, the defendants, employer XPO Logistics, Inc. and insurance 
carrier Indemnity Insurance Company of North America, answered and accepted liability 
for the work injury. During the assigned hearing time, counsel for the parties were able 
to reach an agreement that on or before January 9, 2025, the defendants would 
schedule a physical therapy appointment for Greeley at Synergy Physical Therapy and 
Sports Medicine. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) On or before January 9, 2025, the defendants shall schedule a physical 
therapy appointment for Greeley at Synergy Physical Therapy and Sports 
Medicine. 
 
2) Failure to comply with this order may result in sanctions under Iowa 
Administrative Code rule 876—4.36.
