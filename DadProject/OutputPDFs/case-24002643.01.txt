On April 16, 2024, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care. Hearing 
was scheduled to take place on April 25, 2024. 
Prior to the scheduled hearing, counsel for the parties advised the undersigned 
that they had reached an agreement regarding the requested care. Given the 
agreement of the parties, the hearing was determined to be unnecessary. The parties 
requested entry of a consent order to confirm and document the agreement. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED:   
Per the agreement of the parties, defendants will authorize care and treatment 
that is reasonable and causally related to claimant’s work-related conditions as 
recommended by the authorized treating medical provider at UI Occupational Health. 
Defendants agree to authorize an MRI of the brain, treatment with On With Life, and 
treatment by an ophthalmologist at the University of Iowa Hospitals and Clinics. The 
parties agree that these appointments will be scheduled for a date as soon as possible, 
but no later than May 3, 2024.  
The hearing scheduled for April 25, 2024 is cancelled.
