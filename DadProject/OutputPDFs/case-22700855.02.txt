Claimant filed an application for alternate medical care on December 19, 2022, 
requesting that defendants be ordered to authorize treatment for her headaches and 
dizziness as recommended by Dr. Ashley Mathes, including a referral to Dr. Irving 
Wolfe. Hearing was scheduled for January 3, 2023, at 10:30 a.m. 
Prior to hearing on January 3, 2023, the parties advised the undersigned that 
they have reached an agreement concerning Claimant’s Petition for Alternate Medical 
Care. The parties request a Consent Order be entered in this case, in lieu of a hearing.  
Defendants have authorized claimant to be evaluated by Dr. Irving Wolfe, and 
there is an examination scheduled on January 18, 2023. A hearing is therefore 
determined to be unnecessary, based on the agreement of the parties. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
The parties agree that defendants have authorized claimant to be evaluated by 
Dr. Irving Wolfe, and have scheduled the examination for January 18, 2023. 
The hearing scheduled for January 3, 2023 is cancelled. 
Signed and filed this _3RD  day of January, 2023.
