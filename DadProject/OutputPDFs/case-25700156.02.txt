This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, Alexia Lepley.  An alternate medical 
care hearing was scheduled for March 19, 2025.  Prior to the hearing, the undersigned 
engaged in a discussion with the parties with regard to the claimant’s requested medical 
care.  The parties agreed that a consent order was appropriate.  
 
The defendants consent, stipulate, and agree to authorize treatment including an 
EEG scheduled to occur on March 24, 2025.  The defendants also agreed to authorize 
and schedule the recommended brain MRI within ten (10) days of the date of this order. 
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  The parties consented to the entry of a consent order verbally on 
March 19, 2025; thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The parties have agreed that Defendants will authorize the EEG scheduled for 
March 24, 2025, as recommended by Dr. Keith Kohout. 
 
The Defendants shall authorize and schedule a recommended brain MRI as soon 
as possible, but no later than ten (10) days from the date of this order.
