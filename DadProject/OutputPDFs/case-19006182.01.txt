Brian Platt filed an application for alternate medical care on March 3, 2021, seeking 
authorization for treatment of his head injury as recommended by Dr. Staci Boon. 
Specifically, Platt sought authorization of prescription glasses ordered by Dr. Boon, as 
well as authorization for the “Pinnacle Program” coordinated by Dr. DeAnn Fitzgerald 
recommended by Dr. Boon.  
Prior to hearing, the parties contacted the undersigned and indicated that the 
defendants have agreed to authorize both the prescription glasses and the Pinnacle 
Program. Platt is satisfied with this authorization of care by the defendants. Platt desired 
that a consent order be entered in this case, in lieu of a hearing. 
Because of the parties’ agreement on care, the hearing in this matter is 
unnecessary. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
 
1) If they have not already, the defendants must provide written authorization to 
Cedar Valley Eye Care to dispense the prescription glasses ordered by Dr. 
Boon and to Dr. Fitzgerald for claimant to proceed with the Pinnacle Program, 
both within the next ten (10) days.  
 
2) The alternate care hearing scheduled in this matter is cancelled. 
Signed and filed this __15th __ day of March, 2021.
