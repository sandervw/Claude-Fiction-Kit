On December 12, 2025, claimant filed a petition for Alternate Medical Care. 
Hearing was scheduled to take place on December 23, 2025. 
On December 23, 2025, counsel for the parties advised the undersigned that 
they had reached an agreement regarding the requested care.  
1. Defendant shall authorize an appointment with Dr. Naylor, an orthopedist in 
Black Hawk County, to examine and provide medical care for the claimant as 
it relates to her admitted injury. 
2. Review Dr. Manshadi’s October 4, 2025, IME report and opine whether he 
agrees or disagrees with Dr. Manshadi’s recommendations for treatment as it 
relates to the admitted injury.  
To protect the interests of their clients, the parties agree to entry of a consent 
order, requiring defendants to authorize treatment as described above. The parties 
requested entry of a consent order to confirm and document the agreement.  
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
Defendants will authorize the claimant to undergo further evaluation by the 
treating provider, Richard Naylor, D.O.  Additionally, Dr. Naylor will be requested to 
review the Independent Medical Examination conducted by Farid Manshadi, M.D., 
FAAPM&R, and provide an opinion on whether he concurs with the recommendations.
