Claimant requests an order authorizing additional physical therapy as a result of 
her December 4, 2020 injury.  Defendant admits the alleged injury and have authorized 
Naomi Chelli, M.D., as an authorized treating physician under Iowa Code section 85.27.   
Defendant agrees that any physical therapy referral or recommendation made by Dr. 
Chelli is also authorized treatment.  Accordingly, it appears the parties are in agreement 
that the requested physical therapy shall be authorized and both parties agree to entry 
of a consent order in this respect. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED:   
Pursuant to the agreement of the parties, the physical therapy requested by 
claimant and recommended by Dr. Chelli is authorized. 
Defendant shall complete any necessary bureaucratic work to authorize the 
physical therapy on or before March 31, 2021. 
Defendant shall satisfy and hold claimant harmless against all charges for 
physical therapy recommended by the authorized treating physician, Dr. Chelli.
