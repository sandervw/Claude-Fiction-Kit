Claimant is currently under the care of Laura Cunningham, PA-C, at Focused 
Life Clinic, an authorized mental healthcare provider. Ms. Cunningham has 
recommended an urgent referral to Rich Webster, MA, JD, LMHC, at Path to Peace 
Counseling for claimant’s PTSD symptoms. Ms. Cunningham has also referred claimant 
to Nate Soriano, CRNA, at Focused Life Clinic for evaluation for additional ketamine 
therapy.  
  
Defendants and claimant have reached an agreement to authorize the above-
referenced referrals in order to facilitate ongoing mental health care, subject to Iowa 
Code Section 85.27. Defendants agree to promptly, by January 13, 2026, provide 
written authorization for treatment and billing information to Rich Webster, MA, JD, 
LMHC, at Path to Peace Counseling, and Nate Soriano, CRNA, at Focused Life Clinic.  
 
This Consent Order is not intended to reflect the findings of fact or conclusions of 
law relative to this claim. Rather, it is intended to document and enter an Order that is 
consistent with both parties' positions and acceptable to both parties. A formal alternate 
medical care hearing was not conducted, as all parties consented to entry of this Order 
and waived the formal hearing.
