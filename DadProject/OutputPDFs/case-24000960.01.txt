Claimant filed an Original Notice and Petition for Alternate Medical Care with this 
agency on August 9, 2024. 
 
Based on an agreement between the parties, and a proposed consent order, the 
parties agree to follow the care and treatment plan of Dr. Elijah Miller.  In particular, the 
subject to this dispute was a referral for evaluation and care to the University of Iowa 
Pain Clinic program. Dr. Miller remains the authorized treating physician. 
 
This consent order is not intended to reflect findings of fact or conclusions of law 
relative to this claim.  Rather, it is intended to document and enter an order that is 
consistent with both parties’ positions and acceptable to both parties.  A formal alternate 
medical care hearing was not conducted as all parties consented to entry of this order 
and waived the formal hearing. 
 
THEREFORE IT IS ORDERED: 
 
Pursuant to the agreement of the parties, the parties agree to follow the care and 
treatment plan of Dr. Elijah Miller.  In particular, the subject to this dispute was a referral 
for evaluation and care to the University of Iowa Pain Clinic program. Dr. Miller remains 
the authorized treating physician. 
 
Claimant retains the right to file another petition for alternate medical care 
regarding this matter if necessary.
