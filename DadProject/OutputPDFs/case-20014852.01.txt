On January 24, 2024, Mark Vandivier filed an application for alternate care under 
Iowa Code section 85.27 and Iowa Administrative Code rule 876—4.48. The agency 
issued an order setting the case for a telephone hearing to be held February 6, 2024. 
The defendants, employer JB Hunt Transport, Inc. and insurance carrier Indemnity 
Insurance Company of North America, answered on February 2, 2024.  
On February 5, 2024, the parties emailed the undersigned, stating they have 
reached an agreement on care. The defendants have authorized care with Bryan Trout, 
D.P.M, at Iowa Ortho. The parties have arranged an appointment for later this week. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) The defendants shall authorize care with Bryan Trout, D.P.M., at the time and 
place agreed to by the parties. 
 
2) The alternate care telephone hearing scheduled for February 6, 2024, is 
CANCELLED. 
Signed and filed this 5th day of February, 2024.
