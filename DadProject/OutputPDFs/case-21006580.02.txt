Claimant filed a petition seeking alternate medical care.  A telephonic hearing on 
this petition was scheduled for September 7, 2021.  During the hearing, the parties were 
able to reach an amicable resolution of the alternate medical care dispute as it is related 
to the claimant’s request for authorization of Transderm-Scop patch and all parties 
consented to entry of a consent order reflecting their agreement.   
Specifically, claimant asserts a request authorization of Transderm-Scop patch.  
Defendants indicate that they will approve this medication.   
Having reached an agreement for entry of a consent order, all parties waived a 
formal hearing. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
Pursuant to the agreement of the parties, defendants shall authorize the 
prescribed Transderm-Scop patch.
