This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 IAC 4.48 is 
invoked by claimant, Christopher Cox.  The alternate medical care claim was scheduled 
for telephone hearing on July 1, 2021, at 8:30 a.m.   
 
Prior to the commencement of the hearing, on June 30, 2021, claimant’s attorney 
and defendants’ attorney reached out to the undersigned.  The parties indicated via e-
mail that they reached an agreement on treatment recommended by Dr. Myles 
Luszczyk.   
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal hearing.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order via e-mail on June 
30, 2021, thus no alternate medical care hearing is necessary.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The parties have agreed that Defendants will authorize care and treatment for 
Claimant’s work-related conditions as recommended by the authorized treating medical 
provider, Dr. Myles Luszczyk.   
 
Defendants are specifically ordered to authorize Claimant to be seen by Dr. 
Timothy Miller, pain specialist.
