On September 25, 2023, Susan Erlandson applied to the agency under Iowa 
Code section 85.27 and Iowa Administrative Code rule 876—4.48 for alternate care in 
the form of pain management for her right shoulder and arm. The agency assigned the 
case No. 22009139.02 and scheduled it for a telephone hearing to be held October 5, 
2023, at 8:30 a.m. The parties notified the undersigned by email that they had reached 
an agreement regarding care and a hearing was no longer needed. They also submitted 
a proposed consent order reflecting their agreement. 
The defendants have agreed to contact a physician to schedule an appointment 
with a pain management specialist within 14 days of this order, though the appointment 
can be scheduled for a date later than 14 days after this order. The parties have agreed 
to this consent order in lieu of a hearing. A hearing is therefore unnecessary. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) Within 14 days of the date of this order, the defendants shall contact a 
physician to schedule an appointment with a pain management specialist for 
Erlandson’s right shoulder and right arm. The appointment need not occur 
within 14 days of this order. 
 
2) The telephone alternate care hearing scheduled in this matter is 
CANCELLED.
