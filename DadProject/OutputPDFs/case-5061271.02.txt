This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by the claimant.  Prior to proceeding with the 
alternate medical care contested case hearing on May 6, 2024, the parties reached out 
to the undersigned and indicated a desire to enter into a consent order. 
The defendants agree to schedule an appointment with a neurologist within 30 
days of the date of this order.  The defendants also agreed that the appointment would 
occur within 30 days of the date of this order.   
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order; thus no alternate 
medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
That the defendants shall authorize and schedule an appointment with a 
neurologist within 30 days of the date of this order.   
That the authorized appointment with a neurologist shall occur within 30 days of 
the date of this order.
