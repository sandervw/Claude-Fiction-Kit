ORDER NUNC PRO TUNC 
 
 
COMES NOW the Court and enters this Order Nunc Pro Tunc to correct certain 
typographical errors discovered in the “Ruling on Petition for Judicial Review” (“Ruling”), 
filed May 12, 2020. 
 
WHEREFORE IT IS ORDERED: 
1. On page 30 of the Ruling, under issue number 6, it states, “This issue for judicial 
review, as stated by Petitioner, is really two issues: 1) incorrect citation by the 
agency to Iowa Code §85.47, rather than §853.48…”   
a. The reference to “§853.48” is a typographical error and should be to 
§85.48. 
b. This language is HEREBY AMENDED to read as follows:  “This issue 
for judicial review, as stated by Petitioner, is really two issues: 1) 
incorrect citation by the agency to Iowa Code §85.47, rather than 
§85.48.” 
2. Page 33 of the ruling begins with the language, “… the Court finds that the 
agency has complied with §86.42 by adequately ‘fixing’ the lump sum to be 
paid…”
