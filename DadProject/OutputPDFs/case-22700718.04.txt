This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  Claimant, Angela Rounds, invokes the expedited 
procedure of rule 876 Iowa Administrative Code 4.48.  Prior to proceeding with the 
hearing scheduled for the alternate medical care claim on October 2, 2023, the parties 
reached out to the undersigned and proposed a consent order regarding the alternate 
medical care petition.   
 
The defendants consent and agree to authorize physical therapy as ordered by 
Dr. Buckwalter at Physical Therapy Solutions (“PTS”) in Manchester, Iowa.  The 
claimant agreed to promptly schedule physical therapy visits with PTS.  The physical 
therapy visits would occur two times per week for six weeks.  The claimant agreed to 
attend the physical therapy visits, unless Dr. Buckwalter or PTS recommended that she 
cease physical therapy during the six-week period noted herein.  Finally, the claimant 
agreed to transmit a copy of her physical therapy schedule to the defendants so that the 
defendant-employer may build a work schedule for Ms. Rounds around her physical 
therapy schedule.   
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order on September 29, 
2023; thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.
