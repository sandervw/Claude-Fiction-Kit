This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, Michael Almendinger.  An alternate 
medical care hearing was scheduled for December 4, 2025.  Prior to the hearing, the 
parties agreed via email that a consent order would be appropriate in lieu of a formal 
decision.   
 
The defendants agree to authorize a return appointment to Dr. Kamaldeen 
Aderibigbe for evaluation of continuing pain and discomfort in the hip and pelvis.  The 
defendants shall schedule the appointment within the next 30 days. 
 
The parties consented to the entry of a consent order via email on December 2, 
2025; thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
 
Should the defendants fail to abide by the stipulations in this consent order, 
sanctions may be imposed pursuant to 876 Iowa Administrative Code 4.36. 
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1. Within 30 days of the date of this order, the defendants shall authorize and 
schedule an appointment for evaluation of the claimant’s hip and pelvis with 
Dr. Kamaldeen Aderibigbe.
