On June 24, 2025, claimant filed a Petition for Alternate Medical Care. Hearing 
was scheduled to take place on July 7, 2025. 
Prior to the scheduled hearing, counsel for the parties advised the undersigned 
that they had reached an agreement regarding the requested care. The parties 
requested entry of a consent order to confirm and document the agreement.   
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
Defendants agree to authorize the DRG trial as recommended by the authorized 
treating doctor. In terms of scheduling, the parties are aware that currently Dunes Pain 
Specialists is scheduling into August. By this order, Defendants are required to do 
whatever is necessary to facilitate the scheduling of the appointment at the earliest 
possible date. 
The hearing scheduled for July 7, 2025 is cancelled.
