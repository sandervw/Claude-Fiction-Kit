On January 16, 2026, claimant filed a petition concerning application for alternate 
care under Iowa Code section 85.27 and Iowa Administrative Code rule 876-4.48. 
Claimant requests chiropractic care with Asuta Health, including whole body vibration 
therapy, spinal decompression and pelvic chair treatment. Defendants have denied the 
requested treatment at Asuta Health other than pelvic chair treatment, per the opinion of 
Dr. Richard Glowacki.  
 
The parties have conferred and agreed to the following consent order:  
              
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED:  
 
1. Per the opinion of Dr. Richard Glowacki, the Defendants agree to authorize 
pelvic chair treatment at Asuta Health but deny Claimant’s other requests for 
chiropractic treatment.  
 
2. The telephone hearing in this case is CANCELLED.
