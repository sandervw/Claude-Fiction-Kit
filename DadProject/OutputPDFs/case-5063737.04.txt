On August 15, 2022, claimant filed a petition seeking alternate medical care.  A 
hearing was set for August 26, 2022.  On August 26, 2022, the parties agreed to the 
following: 
Defendants agree to send claimant back to Dr. Richard Garrels to re-
evaluate claimant’s condition including his right shoulder and lower back 
condition and determine a course of treatment.  
Based on the foregoing, the alternate care petition is dismissed pursuant to the 
consent of the parties.   
THEREFORE IT IS ORDERED, the above captioned petition is DISMISSED 
without prejudice by consent of the parties.
