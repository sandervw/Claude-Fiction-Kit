On December 23, 2024, Kodjo Kassabaihin Akakpo applied to the agency for 
alternate care under Iowa Code section 85.27 and Iowa Administrative Code rule 876—
4.48. The defendants, employer Amazon.com Services LLC and insurance carrier LM 
Insurance Corporation, did not file an answer. The agency scheduled the case for a 
telephone hearing to be held on January 3, 2025. On January 2, 2025, the parties 
emailed the undersigned and stated they had reached an agreement regarding care 
rendering a hearing on the dispute unnecessary and proposed draft language for a 
consent order memorializing their agreement. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) The defendants shall promptly schedule an electromyography (EMG), 
treatment at Iowa Ortho, and a second opinion evaluation at Des Moines 
Orthopaedic Surgeons (DMOS), each with an interpreter. 
 
2) The alternate care telephone hearing in this matter is CANCELLED. 
Signed and filed this 3rd day of January, 2025.
