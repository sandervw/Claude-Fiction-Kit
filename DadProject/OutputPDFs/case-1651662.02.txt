Having been presented an Application for Alternate Care from the above-
referenced Claimant and an Answer by the Defendant, the Iowa Workers' Compensation 
Commissioner was informed that an agreement had been reached.  The Agreement was 
as follows:  
 
1. 
The Defendants will authorize the spinal cord stimulator trial as 
recommended by Dr. Singh at Quincy Medical Group as well as the recommended 
thoracic MRI required as part of that process.  
 
2. 
The Defendants will authorize mental health care at Quincy Medical Group.    
 
THEREFORE, based upon the agreement amongst the parties, I hereby enter an 
Order requiring the Defendant to provide authorization for care with Dr. Singh at Quincy 
Medical Group as well accompanying thoracic MRI, and pain psychology mental health 
care at Quincy Medical Group.    
Signed and filed this 23rd day of March, 2020.
