On April 21, 2022, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care.  On April 
28, 2022, defendants filed their answer to the petition. The matter was scheduled for 
hearing on May 3, 2022.   
Prior to the hearing, the parties confirmed that an agreement had been reached 
wherein defendants will authorize care and treatment which is reasonable and causally 
related to claimant’s right shoulder.  Specifically, defendants agree to authorize claimant 
to be seen and treated by Camilla Frederick, M.D. and Matthew Bollier, M.D.  The 
parties reported that the agreement resolves the pending alternate medical care petition 
and requested entry of a consent order to confirm and document the agreement.  
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
The hearing scheduled for May 3, 2022 is cancelled. 
Defendants agree to authorize care and treatment which is reasonable and 
causally related to claimant’s right shoulder with Dr. Camilla Frederick and Dr. Matthew 
Bollier.  
Claimant’s alternate care petition is now moot due to the parties’ agreement.  
Claimant’s alternate care petition is dismissed.
