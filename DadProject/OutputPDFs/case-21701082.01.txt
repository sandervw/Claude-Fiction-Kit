On October 4, 2021, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care. The 
matter was scheduled for hearing on October 13, 2021.   
At the time of the hearing, defendants Solar Plastics, LLC, and American 
Compensation Insurance Company agreed to authorize a visit with John Hoffman, M.D. 
The parties indicated that the agreement resolves the pending alternate medical care 
petition and requested entry of a consent order to confirm and document the 
agreement.   
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED:  
Defendants shall, within the next fourteen (14) days, schedule the earliest 
available appointment with Dr. Hoffman.
