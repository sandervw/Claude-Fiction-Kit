Claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care on November 15, 2024.  This 
matter was set for a telephone hearing on December 2, 2024 at 10:30 a.m.  
 
Prior to the December 2, 2024, telephone hearing, the parties reached a 
resolution by consent.  Defendants confirmed that the referral by authorized provider 
Robert T. Wade, LMHC to have Claimant’s medication management be handled by Jodi 
Henning, ARNP with Des Moines Mental Health and Wellness will be authorized.     
 
By reaching this agreement and consenting to this order, neither party waives 
any rights they may have in the future to address medical treatment.  
 
The parties agreed to the undersigned issuing this consent order confirming the 
agreement reached.  Within fourteen (14) days of this Order, Defendants shall provide 
confirmation to Claimant’s counsel that an appointment for Claimant with Ms. Henning 
has been scheduled.   
 
IT IS SO ORDERED.
