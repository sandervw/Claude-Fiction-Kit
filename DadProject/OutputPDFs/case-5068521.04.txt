On September 15, 2021, claimant filed an application for alternate medical care 
under Iowa Code section 85.27 and rule 876 IAC 4.48.  A hearing was held on 
September 28, 2021.  All parties were given proper notice.  
Claimant sustained an injury on May 17, 2019, which arose out of and in the 
course of employment.  Claimant is dissatisfied with the medical treatment offered by 
the defendants. 
The hearing was recorded via a digital voice recorder and a complete decision 
was dictated into the record on the day of the hearing.  The decision will be reproduced 
in typewritten form only if the decision is appealed, and the parties are advised to follow 
the procedures outlined under the administrative code.  Any rights of appeal will run 
from the date the decision was dictated into the record September 28, 2021. 
The deputy ordered that claimant’s petition for alternate medical care be granted 
and that claimant be allowed physical therapy treatment for the low back and legs at the 
SouthEast Regional Iowa Medical Center in Wappello, Iowa.
