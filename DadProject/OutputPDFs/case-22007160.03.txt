This alternate medical care proceeding came before the undersigned for a 
hearing on Monday, August 7, 2023.  Counsel for both parties participated via telephone 
conference call.  Prior to initiating a formal recording of the proceedings, counsel 
exchanged e-mails and held an informal conversation with the undersigned.  Counsel 
notified the undersigned the parties could enter into an agreement pertaining to the 
request for further medical care. 
Specifically, the parties each consent to entry of an order that directs defendants 
to promptly contact, schedule, and authorize further evaluation by Kurt A. Smith, D.O. at 
Iowa Ortho.  All parties agree that defendants should be given seven (7) days to 
accomplish this, and that claimant should be scheduled for the first available 
appointment with Dr. Smith. 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
Pursuant to the agreement of the parties, defendants are ordered to 
contact Dr. Smith’s office, schedule a follow-up appointment, and authorize said 
appointment within seven (7) days of the entry of this order. 
 
Defendants shall schedule claimant for the first reasonably available 
appointment date with Dr. Smith and promptly notify claimant’s counsel of the 
appointment date and time.
