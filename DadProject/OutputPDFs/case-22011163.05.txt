This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, Lindsey Miller.  Prior to proceeding 
with hearing on the alternate medical care claim on August 27, 2024, the defendants 
filed an answer and supporting documents indicating that the defendants were 
attempting to arrange for the requested care.  The undersigned called the parties to 
initiate the alternate care hearing.  At the outset of the call, the parties indicated a desire 
to enter into a consent order. 
 
The defendants consent, stipulate, and agree to provide care to the claimant as 
recommended by Stephanie Dopheide, PT, including the provision of compression 
Velcro devices and physical therapy.   
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order verbally on August 
27, 2024; thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The parties have agreed that defendants will authorize care for the claimant as 
recommended by PT Dopheide, including the provision of compression Velcro devices 
and physical therapy.
