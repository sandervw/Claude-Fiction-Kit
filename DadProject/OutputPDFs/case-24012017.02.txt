Claimant filed an original notice and petition for alternate medical care on 
January 8, 2025.  The agency scheduled this case for an alternate medical care hearing 
on January 17, 2025.  Prior to commencement of the hearing, the parties notified the 
undersigned that they reached an agreement about future medical care.  I accept the 
parties’ agreement.  Specifically, the parties entered into the following agreement: 
The defendants agree to authorize 6 weeks of physical therapy per the 
recommendation of authorized treating physician, Dr. Daniel Lahr.  In addition, the 
defendants agree to authorize and schedule an appointment with a provider of their 
designation consistent with the recommendations of Dr. Lahr for a second opinion, 
as  stated in his report of December 10, 2024. The defendants agree to communicate 
the appointment scheduling information to Claimant’s counsel within 30 days of 
issuance of the Consent Order. 
SO ORDERED.
