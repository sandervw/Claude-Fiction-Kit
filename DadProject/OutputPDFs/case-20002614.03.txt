Claimant filed an Original Notice and Petition for Alternate Medical Care with this 
agency on August 17, 2022. In the Original Notice and Petition for Alternate Medical 
Care, claimant requested defendants be ordered to authorize treatment with a 
physiatrist. 
Based on emails with counsel for both parties, and a proposed consent order, 
defendants agree to schedule an appointment with Eric Aschenbrenner, M.D. in 
Coralville, Iowa. 
To avoid an unnecessary hearing, and yet protect the interests of their clients, 
the parties agree to entry of a consent order, requiring defendants to schedule an 
appointment with claimant with Dr. Aschenbrenner in Coralville, Iowa. 
This consent order is not intended to reflect findings of fact or conclusions of law 
relative to this claim. Rather, it is intended to document and enter an order that is 
consistent with both parties’ positions and acceptable to both parties. A formal alternate 
medical care hearing was not conducted, as all parties consented to entry of this order 
and waived the formal hearing. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
Pursuant to the agreement of the parties prior to the scheduled August 29, 2022, 
alternate medical care hearing, defendants shall schedule an appointment with claimant 
to be seen by Dr. Aschenbrenner in Coralville, Iowa.  Claimant retains the right to file 
another petition for alternate medical care regarding this matter if necessary.
