Claimant filed a petition seeking alternate medical care.  A telephonic hearing on 
this petition was scheduled for July 21, 2020.  Shortly before the commencement of the 
alternate medical care hearing, counsel notified the undersigned that the parties were 
able to reach an amicable agreement and that a consent order should be ordered.   
Specifically, claimant requests authorization of surgical intervention for an ulnar 
neuropathy, as recommended by Joseph A. Buckwalter, M.D.  Defendants agreed to 
authorize the recommended procedure, which includes an ulnar nerve decompression 
and possible transposition of the nerve.  Both parties agree that a consent order is 
appropriate and that a formal hearing is unnecessary to resolve this contested case 
dispute. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
Pursuant to the agreement of the parties, defendants shall promptly authorize 
and pay for the surgery recommended by Dr. Buckwalter, including the ulnar nerve 
decompression and possible transposition of the nerve.
