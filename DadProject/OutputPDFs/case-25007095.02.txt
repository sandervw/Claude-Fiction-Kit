Claimant filed applications for alternate medical care for file number 24004579.02 
and file number 25007095.02 on September 16, 2025. The applications were 
consolidated for hearing to take place on September 26, 2025.   
Prior to the hearing, the parties confirmed that an agreement had been reached 
that resolves the pending alternate medical care petitions, and requested entry of a 
consent order to confirm and document the agreement. A hearing is therefore 
determined to be unnecessary, based on the agreement of the parties.  
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
Pursuant to the agreement of the parties, defendants will promptly authorize and 
pay for claimant's lumbar spine surgery, as ordered by authorized treating surgeon, Dr. 
Rosenthal at Iowa Ortho, on 09/11/25. Defendants will promptly reimburse claimant for 
the cost of filling the prednisone prescription that was initially prescribed by Iowa Ortho 
Urgent Care on September 4, 2025. 
The hearing scheduled for September 26, 2025 is cancelled.
