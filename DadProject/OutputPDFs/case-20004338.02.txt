Claimant filed an application for alternate medical care on August 17, 2022 
requesting that defendants be ordered to authorize treatment for her cervical spine 
injury. On August 27, 2022, defendants filed an answer indicating they agree to the care 
recommended by claimant’s ARNP, have provided prescription coverage, and are 
working to set up the recommended treatments. 
Hearing was scheduled for August 29, 2022. The parties desired that a Consent 
Order be entered in this case, in lieu of a hearing. The parties agree that defendants will 
authorize and pay for claimant to attend physical therapy, massage therapy, continued 
counseling, prescription medications, and continued treatment and medication 
management through Brandi Booth, ARNP. A hearing is therefore determined to be 
unnecessary, based on the agreement of the parties.  
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
Defendants shall promptly authorize and arrange to pay for physical therapy with 
a provider of their choosing; massage therapy with a provider of their choosing; 
continued counseling with Rhonda Thompson, LISW; prescription medications 
(Meloxicam, Robaxin, BusPirone, and Venlafaxine); and continued treatment and 
medication management through Brandi Booth, ARNP, as the authorized treating 
provider.
