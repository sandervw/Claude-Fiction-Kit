Claimant filed an application for alternate medical care on November 10, 2023 
requesting Defendants be ordered to authorize a surgery recommended by Dr. Jason 
Stanford, an orthopedist, and scheduled to occur on December 1, 2023.   
Prior to hearing, the parties contacted the undersigned Deputy and indicated 
Defendants have agreed to authorize the December 1, 2023 surgery.  
Claimant desired that a Consent Order be entered in this case, in lieu of a 
hearing. 
A hearing is therefore determined to be unnecessary, based on the agreement of 
the parties.  
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
Defendants shall authorize the surgery with Dr. Stanford, which is scheduled for 
December 1, 2023.
