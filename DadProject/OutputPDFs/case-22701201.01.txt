Claimant filed a petition seeking alternate medical care.  A telephonic hearing on 
this petition was scheduled for December 21, 2022.  Prior to the commencement of the 
alternate medical care hearing, the parties notified the undersigned that they had 
reached an amicable agreement and that an evidentiary hearing was not necessary. 
Specifically, defendants agree they will promptly set up and authorize an EMG 
study for the claimant’s left arm and wrist.  I accept the parties’ stipulation and 
agreement and find it to be a reasonable resolution of this alternate medical care claim. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
Pursuant to the agreement of the parties, defendants shall promptly set up and 
authorize an EMG study for the claimant’s left arm and wrist. 
The alternate medical care hearing scheduled for December 21, 2022 is 
cancelled.
