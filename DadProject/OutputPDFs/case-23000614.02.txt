On June 9, 2023, Draeger filed an application for alternate medical care under 
Iowa Code section 85.27 and agency rule 876 IAC 4.48. Counsel for the defendants, 
Amazon.com and LM Insurance Corp., filed an appearance the same day. The hearing 
was scheduled for the afternoon of June 21, 2023. 
 
On the morning of June 21, 2023, counsel for the parties informed the undersigned 
they had reached an agreement as to claimant’s care, under which the defendants have 
agreed to authorize a second opinion evaluation with DMOS.  
  
The parties requested a consent order from the agency memorializing the 
agreement in lieu of a hearing and determination of the application. 
   
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
  
1. Defendants shall authorize a second opinion evaluation for the claimant with 
DMOS;  
AND 
 
2. The alternate care hearing scheduled in this matter is cancelled.
