Claimant filed an Original Notice and Petition for Alternate Medical Care with this 
agency on May 11, 2023. The application sought authorization of medical care in the form 
of an appointment with a neurologist. The hearing was scheduled for May 23, 2023. 
 
Prior to the hearing, the parties advised the undersigned that an agreement to 
provide care had been reached. The parties have requested a Consent Order be entered 
in lieu of hearing.  
 
Defendants will schedule an appointment with a neurologist within 21 days of this 
Order. A hearing is, therefore, unnecessary.  
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
Defendants shall schedule an appointment with a neurologist within 21 days of this 
Order. The hearing scheduled for May 23, 2023 was cancelled.
