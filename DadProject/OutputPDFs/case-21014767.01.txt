On January 28, 2022, Helen Hall applied with the agency for alternate care under 
Iowa Code section 85.27. The defendants did not answer. The agency set the matter for 
a telephone hearing on February 9, 2022. Counsel for the parties informed the 
undersigned they had reached an agreement on care under which the defendants have 
agreed to authorize Hall to see Dr. Justin Khoriaty, per a referral from Iowa Ortho, at the 
first available appointment. The parties requested a consent order from the agency 
memorializing the agreement in lieu of a hearing and determination of the application. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) The defendants shall authorize Hall to see Dr. Justin Khoriaty at the first 
available appointment. 
 
2) The alternate care hearing scheduled in this matter is CANCELLED. 
Signed and filed this _8th _ day of February, 2022.
