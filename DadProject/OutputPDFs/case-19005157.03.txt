This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 IAC 4.48 is 
invoked by claimant, Christopher Cox.  The alternate medical care claim was scheduled 
for telephone hearing on November 16, 2021, at 8:30 a.m.   
 
Prior to the commencement of the hearing, on November 15, 2021, the parties 
reached out to the undersigned with a proposed consent order.   
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal hearing.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order on November 15, 
2021; thus no alternate medical care hearing is necessary.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The parties have agreed that the defendants will authorize care and treatment 
which is reasonable and causally related to the claimant’s work-related conditions as 
recommended by the authorized treating medical providers, Dr. Myles Luszczyk and Dr. 
Timothy Miller.   
 
The defendants are specifically ordered to authorize and pay for the claimant to 
undergo a spinal cord stimulator trial, which will be performed by authorized treating 
provider Dr. Timothy Miller.
