Claimant filed an application for alternate medical care on March 26, 2021, 
seeking authorization of treatment of her September 6, 2020 work-related injury. Prior to 
hearing, the parties contacted the undersigned Deputy and indicated that the parties 
have resolved the matter. Specifically, the parties have agreed to the following:  
 
1. Defendant shall authorize an appointment with Dr. Teri Thomsen, a 
neurologist at the University of Iowa Hospitals & Clinics, to treat claimant and 
will schedule an appointment. Claimant understands that the timing of this 
appointment will depend on whether Dr. Thomsen wishes to see claimant 
before or after the recommendations she made on February 19, 2021 have 
been completed.  
 
2. Defendant shall authorize and schedule neuropsychological testing with either 
Dr. Daniel Tranel at the University of Iowa Hospitals & Clinics or with Dr. 
Karla Brennscheidt locally.  
 
3. Defendant shall authorize and schedule physical/occupational therapy 
pursuant to the order entered by Dr. Thomsen on February 19, 2021.  
 
4. Defendant shall authorize Dr. Sharad Bajaj to evaluate and treat claimant 
from a cardiology perspective for any cardiac condition related to her 
September 6, 2020 injury.  
 
5. Defendant shall authorize UnityPoint Psychiatry, specifically Merve Akova 
(ARNP), to treat claimant’s mental symptoms which are deemed related to 
her September 6, 2020 injury.
