ALTERNATE MEDICAL CARE 
CONSENT ORDER 
 
 
 
Claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care on August 24, 2022.  This 
matter was set for a telephone hearing on September 7, 2022 at 1:00 p.m. 
 
 
Prior to the September 7, 2022, hearing, the parties reached a resolution by 
consent.  Defendants agree and authorize claimant to be seen for mental health 
treatment at UnityPoint Health – Robert Young Center located at 1616 Cedar Street, 
Muscatine, Iowa 52761.  Defendants agree to schedule claimant to be seen at 
UnityPoint Health – Robert Young Center as soon as possible, but not later than 
September 21, 2022. 
 
By reaching this agreement and consenting to this order, neither party waves any 
rights they may have in the future to address medical treatment. 
 
 
The parties agreed to the undersigned issuing this consent order confirming the 
agreement reached. 
 
 
IT IS SO ORDERED.
