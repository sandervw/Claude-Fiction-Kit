On January 18, 2022, William Heiden applied for alternate care. The agency 
scheduled the case for a telephone hearing on January 28, 2022. The defendants 
answered. 
On January 26, 2022, counsel for the parties notified the undersigned via email 
they had reached an agreement regarding care. At the undersigned’s request, they 
provided a draft consent order. Under the parties’ agreement, the defendants will 
authorize reasonable care and treatment causally related to Heiden’s left ankle injury 
with Dr. Camilla Frederick. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) Within ten (10) days of the date of this order, the defendants shall make 
arrangements for Heiden to receive reasonable care and treatment causally 
relating to his left ankle injury with Dr. Camilla Frederick. 
 
2) The hearing scheduled in this matter is CANCELLED. 
Signed and filed this _27th _ day of January, 2022.
