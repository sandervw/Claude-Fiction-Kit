On August 11, 2023, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care. 
Defendants filed an answer on August 23, 2023, admitting liability for the claim. Hearing 
was scheduled to take place on August 24, 2023. 
A discussion was held off the record at the beginning of the hearing, in which the 
parties reached an agreement regarding the requested care. Given the agreement of 
the parties, the hearing was determined to be unnecessary. The parties requested entry 
of a consent order to confirm and document the agreement. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED:   
Pending receipt of documentation, defendants have agreed to authorize the 
functional capacity evaluation (FCE) requested by claimant.  
Defendants have begun the process of scheduling an independent medical 
evaluation (IME) for purposes of obtaining an impairment rating, which will be scheduled 
after the results of the FCE are available.
