On December 10, 2025, claimant Darrel Long initiated a contested case 
proceeding before the agency by filing a petition concerning application for alternate 
care seeking authorization of physical therapy by the defendants, employer Old 
Dominion Freight Line, Inc. (Old Dominion) and insurance carrier Indemnity Insurance 
Company of North America (Indemnity), under Iowa Code section 85.27 (2023) and 
Iowa Administrative Code rule 876—4.48 (2023). The agency scheduled the case for a 
telephone hearing on December 19, 2025, at 10:30 a.m. The defendants did not file an 
answer. Iowa Admin. Code r. 876—4.48(8) (“After being served with an application for 
alternate care, the employer shall file an appearance of answer as soon as practicable 
before the time of the hearing.”). 
Prior to the scheduled hearing, the undersigned spoke with counsel for the 
parties by telephone. Defense counsel shared that the defendants had authorized the 
requested physical therapy and the first session was scheduled for December 23, 2025, 
at 4:00 p.m. The parties then agreed to a consent order regarding the authorization of 
physical therapy sought in Long’s petition.  
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) The defendants shall authorize physical therapy as ordered by Dr. Blake 
Eyberg. 
 
2) The defendants shall retain the right under Iowa Code section 85.27 to 
choose the provider of the physical therapy.
