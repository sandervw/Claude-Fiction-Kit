This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  Claimant, Ruben Garcia, invokes the expedited 
procedure of rule 876 Iowa Administrative Code 4.48.  Prior to proceeding with the 
hearing scheduled for the alternate medical care claim on April 19, 2023, the parties 
filed a joint proposed consent order regarding the alternate medical care petition.   
 
The defendants consent and agree to authorize an evaluation for the claimant’s 
back with Dr. Howard at the University of Iowa Hospitals and Clinics.  The parties agree 
that the appointment will be scheduled for a date as soon as possible, but no later than 
May 15, 2023.  If an appointment cannot be secured with Dr. Howard before May 15, 
2023, the defendants agreed to promptly schedule the claimant to visit another 
orthopedic surgeon or neurosurgeon in the Quad Cities or Iowa City area.   
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order on April 19, 2023; 
thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.
