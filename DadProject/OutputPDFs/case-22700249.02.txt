Claimant filed an application for alternate medical care on October 18, 2023, 
requesting that defendants be ordered to authorize the referral to pain management 
physician Dr. Jaycox and that an appointment be scheduled. Hearing was scheduled for 
October 31, 2023 at 10:30 am by telephone. 
Prior to hearing on October 31, 2023, the parties advised the undersigned that 
they have reached an agreement concerning Claimant’s petition for alternate medical 
care. The parties request that a Consent Order be entered in this case in lieu of a 
hearing. 
Dr. Jaycox treats in Chicago, IL. Since filing his petition for alternate medical 
care, Claimant indicated to defendants that he is moving to Davenport, IA, 
approximately three hours from Chicago, IL. It is therefore no longer reasonable for 
treatment to be authorized with Dr. Jaycox. Defendants instead agree that pain 
management will be authorized with a pain management physician located a more 
reasonable distance from the Claimant’s new residence. A hearing is therefore 
determined to be unnecessary based on the agreement of the parties. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
The parties agree that defendants will authorize claimant to be evaluated by a 
pain management physician a reasonable distance from his new residence in 
Davenport, IA.
