On December 20, 2024, Steven Klinefelter filed an application for alternate care 
under Iowa Code section 85.27 and Iowa Administrative Code rule 876—4.48. The 
defendants, employer Old Dominion Freight Line, Inc. and insurance carrier Indemnity 
Insurance Company of North America, did not file an answer. The agency scheduled 
the case for a telephone hearing to be held on January 2, 2025. Before the hearing, the 
parties emailed the undersigned and stated they had reached an agreement regarding 
care, rendering a hearing on the dispute unnecessary.  
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) Within seven (7) days of the date of this order, the defendants shall provide 
Kllinefelter with confirmation that the post-surgical cervical physical therapy 
recommended by his authorized treating physician has been scheduled and 
the provider will cause no further delay in care. 
 
2) The alternate care telephone hearing is CANCELLED. 
Signed and filed this 2nd day of January, 2025.
