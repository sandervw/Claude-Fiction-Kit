Claimant filed an original notice and petition seeking alternate medical care.  
Hearing for the alternate medical care request was scheduled for July 16, 2025.  
 
Prior to the scheduled hearing, the parties reached an agreement regarding the 
requested care for the admitted right shoulder.  Specifically, defendants consented to 
authorize and secure the scheduling of an appointment with a physician at UI 
HealthCare to address Claimant’s ongoing right shoulder complaints no later than July 
29, 2025.   
The Defendants agree to the entry of a consent order in lieu of a formal hearing.  
Claimant acknowledged the offer of care is sufficient.  Claimant similarly acknowledged 
that entry of a consent order is sufficient without the formal necessity of an alternate 
medical care hearing. 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The alternate medical care hearing scheduled for July 16, 2025, at 10:30 a.m. is 
cancelled.   
 
No later than July 29, 2025, Defendants shall authorize and secure an 
appointment with a physician at UI HealthCare to address Claimant’s right shoulder 
complaints.
