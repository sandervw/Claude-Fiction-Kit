      RE: ALTERNATE MEDICAL CARE 
 
 
 
Claimant filed the above-captioned applications for alternate medical care on 
September 25, 2023, requesting that Defendant be ordered to authorize evaluation and 
treatment of Claimant’s right shoulder, right arm, left shoulder, neck, head, left arm, left 
hand, and left third finger.  
 
Prior to hearing, Defendant agreed to authorize an appointment with Dr. Robert 
Gordon to evaluate the alleged work injuries and to address treatment and causation if 
needed. 
 
Claimant desired that a Consent Order be entered in this case, in lieu of a 
hearing. 
 
A hearing is therefore determined to be unnecessary, based on the agreement of 
the parties.  
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
Defendant shall authorize an appointment with Dr. Robert Gordon to evaluate the 
alleged work injuries, and to address treatment and causation if needed.
