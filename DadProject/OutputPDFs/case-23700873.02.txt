This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, Lisa Turner.  Prior to proceeding with 
the alternate medical care claim on November 20, 2023, the undersigned spoke to the 
parties on the phone.  The parties indicated a desire to enter into a consent order. 
 
The defendants consent, stipulate, and agree to authorize an evaluation with On 
With Life.  The defendants agreed to authorize and schedule the appointment before 
December 1, 2023.  The actual appointment may occur after December 1, 2023. 
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order verbally on 
November 20, 2023; thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The parties have agreed that Defendants will authorize an evaluation with On 
With Life. 
 
The appointment with On With Life shall be scheduled prior to December 1, 
2023; however, the appointment itself may occur after this time.
