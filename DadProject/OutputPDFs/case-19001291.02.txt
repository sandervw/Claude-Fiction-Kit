 Claimant filed an application for alternate medical care on April 20, 2023, 
requesting Defendants be ordered to authorize treatment and schedule a medical 
appointment.  
 
Prior to hearing, the parties contacted the undersigned Deputy and indicated 
Defendants have authorized treatment with Dr. Barbara Malicka-Rozek and scheduled 
an appointment for May 10, 2023 at 12:30 p.m.  
 
Claimant desired that a Consent Order be entered in this case, in lieu of a 
hearing. 
 
A hearing is therefore determined to be unnecessary, based on the agreement of 
the parties.  
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED THAT: 
 
Defendants shall authorize and pay for the appointment scheduled with Dr. 
Barbara Malicka-Rozek on May 10, 2023 at 12:30 p.m. and shall not withdraw 
authorization in advance of that appointment.    
 
IT IS SO ORDERED.
