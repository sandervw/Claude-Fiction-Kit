 On May 2, 2024, claimant filed an application for alternate medical care under 
Iowa Code section 85.27 and rule 876 IAC 4.48. An answer was filed accepting 
responsibility for the purposes of the hearing on May 10, 2024.  A hearing was held on 
May 13, 2024.  All parties were given proper notice. 
 
Claimant sustained an injury on October 29, 2023, which arose out of and in the 
course of employment.  Claimant is dissatisfied with the medical treatment offered by 
the defendants which she alleges to be untimely scheduling of appointments and/or lack 
of follow up after referrals have been made. 
 
The hearing was recorded via digital voice recorder and a complete decision was 
dictated into the record on the day of the hearing.  The decision will be reproduced in 
typewritten form only if the decision is appealed, and the parties are advised to follow 
the procedures outlined under the administrative code.  Any rights of appeal will run 
from the date the decision was dictated into the record. 
 
The deputy ordered that claimant’s petition for alternate medical care be 
DENIED.  The decision was rendered orally on the record. 
 
Pursuant to a standing order of delegation of authority by the workers’ 
compensation commissioner pursuant to Iowa Code section 86.3, the undersigned 
enters this decision for the workers’ compensation commissioner.  There is no right of 
appeal of this decision to the workers’ compensation commissioner.  Appeal of this 
decision, if any, would be by judicial review pursuant to Iowa Code section 10A.
