This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 IAC 4.48 is 
invoked by claimant, Kenneth Stacey.  The alternate medical care claim was scheduled 
for telephone hearing on July 26, 2021, at 8:30 a.m.   
 
Prior to the commencement of the hearing, on July 26, 2021, the parties and the 
undersigned discussed the entry of a consent order in this matter.  The parties indicated 
that the defendant authorized care as of July 19, 2021, with Midwest Mental Health and 
Scott Goodman, M.D.  The defendant agreed that this care is authorized. 
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal hearing.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order verbally on July 26, 
2021; thus no alternate medical care hearing was necessary.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The parties have agreed that Defendant will authorize care and treatment for 
Claimant’s work-related conditions with Midwest Mental Health.   
The parties have also agreed that Defendant will authorize care and treatment for 
Claimant’s work-related conditions with Dr. Scott Goodman.
