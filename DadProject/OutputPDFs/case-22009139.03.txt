The parties previously entered into a consent order in File Number 22009139.02 
in which defendants agreed as follows: 
Within 14 days of the date of this order, the defendants shall contact a 
physician to schedule an appointment with a pain management specialist 
for Erlandson’s right shoulder and right arm.  The appointment need not 
occur within 14 days of this order. 
Defendants authorized an appointment with Dr. Timothy Miller in Dubuque, Iowa.   
 
Subsequently, claimant filed an original notice and petition concerning alternate 
medical care on November 20, 2023, requesting that the defendants be ordered to 
authorize pain management treatment with a provider in Waterloo, Iowa.  Prior to the 
hearing, the defendants authorized Allen Center for Pain Medicine to provide care.   
 
Claimant expressed a desire for entry of a consent order rather than a hearing.   
 
A hearing is therefore unnecessary, based upon the agreement of the parties.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The defendants shall contact a physician to schedule an appointment with a pain 
management specialist at Allen Center for Pain Medicine for claimant’s right shoulder 
and right arm within 14 days of the date of this order.
