On April 28, 2022, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care.  No written 
answer has been filed. The matter was scheduled for hearing on May 10, 2022.   
On May 9, 2022, the parties contacted the undersigned and indicated they have 
reached an agreement regarding the requested care. Defendants have agreed to 
authorize and schedule an appointment for pain management treatment with Dr. Kurt 
Smith or Dr. Daniel Moyse – whoever can see claimant the soonest. Defendants have 
also agreed to authorize and schedule a renal ultrasound; back ultrasound; and a 
referral to a kidney specialist.   
Given that the defendants have authorized the requested care and are in the 
process of scheduling appointments, the hearing was determined to be unnecessary.  
Claimant requested entry of a consent order to confirm and document the agreement, 
and defendants agreed.   
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
Defendants have agreed to authorize and schedule an appointment for pain 
management treatment with Dr. Kurt Smith or Dr. Daniel Moyse – whoever can see 
claimant the soonest. Defendants have also agreed to authorize and schedule a renal 
ultrasound; back ultrasound; and a referral to a kidney specialist.   
Defendants shall continue to make all reasonable efforts to schedule the 
appointments for claimant as soon as possible.
