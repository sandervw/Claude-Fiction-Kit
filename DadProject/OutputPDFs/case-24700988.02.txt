On January 3, 2025, claimant Tara Ellis filed a petition concerning application for 
alternate care under Iowa Code section 85.27 and Iowa Administrative Code rule 876—
4.48. The defendants, employer Georgia Pacific and insurance carrier ESIS, answered, 
accepting liability for the alleged work injury. The agency scheduled the matter for a 
telephone alternate care hearing to be held January 14, 2025. 
Before the scheduled hearing, the parties notified the undersigned by email that 
they had reached an agreement regarding care. The defendants agreed to authorize 
and facilitate scheduling surgery with Dr. Nelson. Because of their agreement, the 
hearing was no longer needed. They requested a consent order memorializing it. 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
1) The defendants shall promptly authorize and facilitate scheduling surgery with 
Dr. Nelson. 
 
2) The telephone alternate care hearing is CANCELLED. 
Signed and filed this 14TH day of January, 2025.
