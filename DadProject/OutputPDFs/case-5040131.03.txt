On February 21, 2024, claimant filed a petition for Alternate Medical Care. Hearing 
was scheduled to take place on March 5, 2024. 
Prior to the scheduled hearing, counsel for the parties advised the undersigned that 
they had reached an agreement regarding the requested care. Given the agreement of the 
parties, the hearing was determined to be unnecessary. The parties requested entry of a 
consent order to confirm and document the agreement.   
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
Defendants have authorized Botox and trigger point injections ordered by Dr. 
Stanley Mathew, and this will be communicated to the clinic so claimant can call the clinic 
and schedule these injections.  
The hearing scheduled for March 5, 2024 is cancelled.
