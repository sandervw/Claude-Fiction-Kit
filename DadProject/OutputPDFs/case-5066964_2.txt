On April 24, 2020, claimant filed a Nunc Pro Tunc motion requesting that the 
order section be modified to match the percentage disability stated in the conclusions of 
law. Defendants resist on the grounds that the original finding was erroneous given the 
2017 statutory change limiting functional disabilities to the impairment guidelines in the 
American Medical Association. Defendants have appealed on this basis, among other 
things. Iowa Code section 85.34(2)(x) (2019) 
Both parties are correct. There is a scrivener’s error in the order section. It should 
read,  
That defendants are to pay unto claimant one hundred sixty (160) 
weeks of permanent partial disability benefits at the rate of six hundred 
sixty-two and 74/100 dollars ($662.74) per week from July 18, 2019. 
However, the defendants are also correct that a functional disability is subject to 
Iowa Code section 85.34(2)(x) (2019). Claimant was found to have suffered a functional 
disability rather than an industrial one and therefore,  
[W]hen determining functional disability and not loss of earning capacity, 
the extent of loss or percentage of permanent impairment shall be 
determined solely by utilizing the guides to the evaluation of permanent 
impairment, published by the American medical association, as adopted 
by the workers' compensation commissioner by rule pursuant to chapter

17A. Lay testimony or agency expertise shall not be utilized in determining 
loss or percentage of permanent impairment pursuant to paragraphs ‘a’ 
through ‘u’, or paragraph ‘v’ when determining functional disability and not 
loss of earning capacity. 
Iowa Code § 85.34(2)(x) (2019).  
The defendants’ resistance states that the erroneous finding is but one of the issues 
that is the subject of an appeal and have not filed a motion for rehearing. With the 
appeal filed, the undersigned must defer this issue to the Commissioner.  Thus, the 
motion for Nunc Pro Tunc shall be granted to correct the scrivener’s error.  
THEREFORE IT IS ORDERED, the claimant’s motion for Nunc Pro Tunc is 
granted. The order section shall read “that Defendants are to pay unto claimant one 
hundred sixty (160) weeks of permanent partial disability benefits at the rate of six 
hundred sixty-two and 74/100 dollars ($662.74) per week from July 18, 2019.”
