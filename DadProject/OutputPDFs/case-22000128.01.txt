This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by the claimant.  Prior to the alternate medical care 
contested case proceeding on May 31, 2024, the parties reached out to the 
undersigned and indicated a desire to enter into a consent order. 
 
The defendants agree to authorize a second opinion orthopedic surgery consult 
with Dr. Poulos or a comparable alternative orthopedic surgeon determined at a later 
date.  The parties agree that this appointment will be scheduled for a date as soon as 
possible. 
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order; thus no alternate 
medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
That the defendants shall authorize a second opinion orthopedic surgery consult 
with Dr. Poulos or a comparable alternative surgeon as soon as possible. 
 
 That the motion to dismiss filed on May 30, 2024, is moot due to this consent 
order.
