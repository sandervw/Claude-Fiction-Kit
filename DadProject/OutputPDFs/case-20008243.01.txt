Claimant filed a petition seeking alternate medical care.  A telephonic hearing for 
this petition was scheduled for August 2, 2021.     
Based on an off-the-record conversation with both counsels, the alternate 
medical care requested by claimant is for defendants to provide an authorized second 
opinion with a hand specialist.    
In an effort to avoid an unnecessary hearing, and yet protect the interests of their 
clients, the parties agree to entry of a consent order requiring defendants to provide 
claimant with an authorized second opinion with Joseph Buckwalter, M.D. 
This consent order is not intended to reflect findings of fact or conclusions of law 
relative to this claim.  Rather, it is intended to document and enter an order that is 
consistent with both parties’ positions and acceptable to both parties.  A formal alternate 
medical care hearing was not conducted, as all parties consented to entry of this order 
and waived the formal hearing.         
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED:   
Pursuant to the agreement of the parties prior to the scheduled August 2, 2021, 
alternate medical care hearing, defendants shall authorize and provide claimant with a
