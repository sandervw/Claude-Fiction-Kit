This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, Lindsey Miller.  Prior to proceeding 
with the alternate medical care hearing on March 11, 2024, the parties reached out to 
the undersigned indicating a desire to enter into a consent order. 
 
The defendants consent, stipulate, and agree to provide care to the claimant’s 
right ankle.  As part of this, the defendants agreed to authorize lymphedema care as 
recommended by Dr. Trout.  The appointment will be arranged within two weeks of the 
date of this order; however, the appointment need not occur within two weeks of the 
date of this order.   
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision; thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The parties have agreed that defendants will authorize care for the claimant’s 
lymphedema as recommended by Dr. Trout. 
 
The appointment shall be scheduled within two weeks of the date of this order; 
however, the appointment itself may occur after this time.
