On November 15, 2022, Koeller filed an application for alternate medical care 
under Iowa Code section 85.27 and agency rule 876 IAC 4.48.  The defendants, 
Cardinal Logistics Management Corporation and ACE American Insurance Company 
filed an appearance the same day.  The hearing was scheduled for December 1, 2022 
at 10:30 a.m. 
On November 30, 2022, counsel for the parties informed the undersigned they 
had reached an agreement as to claimant’s care under which the defendants have 
agreed to authorize claimant to treat with an orthopedic surgeon and will provide 
claimant with the date and time of the appointment within seven (7) days.  The parties 
requested a consent order from the agency memorializing the agreement in lieu of a 
hearing and determination of the application.   
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED:  
1) Defendants shall authorize claimant to treat with an orthopedic surgeon and 
will provide claimant with the date and time of the appointment within seven (7) days; 
and 
2) The alternate care hearing scheduled in this matter is cancelled. 
Signed and filed this __30th _ day of November, 2022.
