This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 IAC 4.48 is 
invoked by claimant, Janet Ketelsen.  The alternate medical care claim was scheduled 
for telephone hearing on March 12, 2021, at 10:30 a.m.   
 
Prior to the commencement of the hearing, claimant’s attorney and defendant’s 
attorney reached out to the undersigned.  The parties indicated that treatment with Dr. 
Hussain has been scheduled.  Additional care in the form of an EMG and an MRI is also 
to be scheduled.  Defendant agreed to provide care for the right upper extremity and/or 
shoulder as asserted in the alternate care petition.  The parties agreed that treatment 
with Dr. Hussain would be scheduled as soon as practicable.   
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal hearing.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order during a telephone 
conference on March 12, 2021, at the time of the hearing, thus no alternate medical 
care hearing was necessary.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
Defendant agreed to provide additional treatment to Claimant in the form of an 
EMG and MRI on Claimant’s right upper extremity.  Defendant has further agreed to 
schedule Claimant to be seen by Dr. Hussain following the completion of the EMG and 
MRI on Claimant’s right upper extremity.   
 
Defendant shall continue to make all reasonable efforts to schedule Claimant’s 
appointments as soon as practicable.
