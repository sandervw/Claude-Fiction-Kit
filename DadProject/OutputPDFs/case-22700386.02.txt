This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 IAC 4.48 is 
invoked by claimant, Janet Marshall.  The alternate medical care claim was scheduled 
for telephone hearing on May 17, 2022, at 10:30 a.m.   
 
Prior to the commencement of the hearing, on May 16, 2022, the parties and the 
undersigned discussed the entry of a consent order in this matter.  The defendants 
consent, stipulate, and agree to authorize Dr. Hussain at ORA for causally related and 
medically necessary and reasonable treatment of the claimant’s left hip and left 
hand/wrist, as determined by Dr. Hussain.   
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal hearing.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order via e-mail on May 
16, 2022; thus, no alternate medical care hearing is necessary.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The parties have agreed that Defendants will authorize care with Dr. Hussain 
related to the left hip and left hand/wrist, as noted herein.
