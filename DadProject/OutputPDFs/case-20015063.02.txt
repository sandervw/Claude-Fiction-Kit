On January 24, 2022, claimant filed an application for alternate medical care.  
The matter was scheduled for hearing on February 3, 2022. 
Claimant appeared personally and by her attorney, Andrew Bribriesco. 
Defendants appeared through their attorney Stephen Spencer. Nurse case manager 
Jane Collins was also present. 
Claimant’s exhibits 1 through 3 and defendants’ exhibits A through E were 
admitted as evidence. Prior to calling witnesses, the attorneys provided information 
about the case. Defendants had not filed a written answer but confirmed that the injuries 
alleged in the application for alternate care are accepted. Claimant’s attorney indicated 
that claimant is seeking an appointment with a physiatrist due to her ongoing symptoms. 
After discussion with the parties, an agreement was reached wherein defendants 
have agreed to schedule claimant for an appointment with physiatrist Joseph Chen, 
M.D. The agreement resolved the alternate care issue and the parties requested entry 
of a consent order to confirm and document the agreement.   
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
Defendants have agreed to schedule claimant for an appointment with physiatrist 
Joseph Chen, M.D. Defendants should advise claimant’s counsel once an appointment 
date is secured.
