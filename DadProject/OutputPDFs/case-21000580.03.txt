Trista Kalkhoff applied for alternate care from Plastico, LTD and Employers 
Mutual Casualty Company for an alleged work injury to her right lower extremity and 
right foot on July 30, 2019. Kalkhoff sought authorization of care with providers close to 
her residence in Manning, Iowa, and recommended by Dr. McClaws in the form of an 
orthopedic surgeon and pain specialist. After the agency scheduled the matter for 
hearing, the defendants filed an answer and brief in support of their position on care.  
Prior to the scheduled hearing, the parties reached an agreement on care for the 
work injury in which the defendants have agreed to authorize care with Nicholas 
Wischmeier, M.D., an orthopedic surgeon and consult with Dr. McClaws regarding a 
pain specialist near Kalhoff’s residence in Manning. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
1) The defendants shall arrange care with Dr. Wischmeier. 
 
2) The defendants shall arrange care with a pain specialist after consulting with 
Dr. McClaws.
