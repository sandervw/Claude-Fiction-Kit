ALTERNATE MEDICAL CARE 
CONSENT ORDER 
 
 
 
Claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care on December 4, 2023.  This 
matter was set for a telephone hearing on December 14, 2023 at 1:00 P.M.  
 
 
Prior to the December 14, 2023 hearing, the parties reached a resolution by 
consent.  Defendants have confirmed that Claimant’s surgery with Dr. Timothy Vinyard 
is authorized.  Defendants have been attempting to schedule surgery with Iowa Ortho.  
Defendants are agreeable with Mr. Stanley contacting Iowa Ortho directly in order to 
schedule his surgery. 
 
By reaching this agreement and consenting to this order, neither party waives 
any rights they may have in the future to address medical treatment. 
 
 
The parties agreed to the undersigned issuing this consent order confirming the 
agreement reached.  
 
 
IT IS SO ORDERED.
