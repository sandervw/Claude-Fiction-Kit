On September 28, 2023, claimant filed a Petition for Alternate Medical Care. 
Defendants filed an Appearance on October 10, 2023. The matter was scheduled for a 
hearing on October 11, 2023. 
Defendants have agreed to authorize the medication management of claimant’s 
right shoulder that has been recommended by James Nepola, M.D., the authorized 
treating physician. Defendants have agreed to authorize Stanley Mathew, M.D., 
UnityPoint Health, to provide medication management.  This is the care being sought by 
the claimant. 
Given that the defendants have agreed to authorize the requested care, the 
hearing was determined to be unnecessary. Claimant requested entry of a Consent 
Order to confirm and document the agreement, and defendants agreed. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
Defendants have agreed and are ordered to authorize Stanley Mathew, M.D., 
UnityPoint Health, to provide medication management that has been recommended by 
James Nepola, M.D., who remains authorized to provide all other treatment for 
claimant’s right shoulder. 
The hearing scheduled for October 11, 2023 is cancelled.
