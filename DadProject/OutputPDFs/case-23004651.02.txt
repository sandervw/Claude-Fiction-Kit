This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by the claimant.  Prior to proceeding with the 
alternate medical care contested case proceeding on April 16, 2024, the parties 
reached out to the undersigned and indicated a desire to enter into a consent order. 
 
The defendants have confirmed that they have authorized the claimant to 
establish care with Kurt Smith, D.O., of Iowa Ortho for continued treatment, 
management, and coordination of the claimant’s ongoing care.  This includes the 
claimant’s rehabilitation therapies related to his work injury.  At the time of this order, the 
defendants were attempting to schedule the claimant’s initial appointment with Dr. 
Smith.  The defendants agreed to schedule the claimant’s initial appointment with Dr. 
Smith as soon as possible.  However, the defendants made no guarantees that Dr. 
Smith would accept the claimant as a patient.     
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order; thus, no alternate 
medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
That the defendants will attempt to authorize ongoing medical care with Dr. Smith 
as noted herein.   
 
That the defendants will attempt to schedule claimant’s initial appointment with 
Dr. Smith as soon as possible.
