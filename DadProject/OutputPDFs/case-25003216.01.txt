Claimant filed an Original Notice and Petition for Alternate Medical Care with this 
agency on March 21, 2025. 
 
Prior to the scheduled hearing, counsel for the parties advised the undersigned 
that they had reached an agreement regarding the requested care. Defendants have 
agreed to authorize Claimant to see an occupational health specialist for an approved 
visit, specifically, Dr. Nate Brady at UnityPoint Occupational Medicine, or a similarly 
qualified occupational physician. Given the agreement of the parties, the hearing was 
determined to be unnecessary. The parties requested entry of a consent order to 
confirm and document the agreement. 
  
 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
 
 
Defendants agree to authorize medical care with an occupational physician, Dr. 
Nate Brady at UnityPoint Occupational Medicine, or a similarly qualified occupational 
physician.
