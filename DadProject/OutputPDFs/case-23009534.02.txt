This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by the claimant.  Prior to proceeding with the 
alternate medical care contested case proceeding on May 30, 2024, the parties reached 
out to the undersigned and indicated a desire to enter into a consent order. 
 
The defendants agree to schedule the left intra-articular hip injection as 
recommended by Thomas G. Klein, D.O., in the April 3, 2024, medical record.  The 
defendants shall schedule the appointment within ten (10) days of the date of this order.  
The appointment itself need not occur within the ten (10) day time period. 
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order; thus no alternate 
medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
That the defendants shall authorize a left intra-articular hip injection as 
recommended by Dr. Klein. 
 
That the defendants shall schedule the appointment within ten (10) days of the 
date of this order.   
 
That the appointment itself need not occur within the ten (10) day period noted 
above.
