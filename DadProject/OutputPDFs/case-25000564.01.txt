On May 5, 2025, claimant filed an original notice and petition for alternate 
medical care in this file under Iowa Code section 85.27, invoking the provisions of rule 
876 IAC 4.48. Claimant sought an order that defendants authorize the Mayo Clinic to 
provide treatment for claimant’s alleged injuries. 
 
Hearing was scheduled for May 14, 2025. On May 12, 2025, defendants filed an 
answer indicating they admit liability for the alleged injury to the cervical spine, 
specifically mechanical neck pain. Defendants deny liability for the other alleged 
injuries. The answer also states that the Mayo Clinic has been authorized, with an 
appointment pending. Claimant filed a motion for a consent order in order to document 
the agreement. Defense counsel advised that Mayo Clinic is still reviewing the request, 
but defendants will find another comparable provider should Mayo decline to see 
claimant. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
 
The parties agree that defendants will authorize the Mayo Clinic to provide 
treatment for claimant’s cervical neck injury, specifically mechanical neck pain.  
 
An appointment with Mayo Clinic is pending review. Should Mayo Clinic decline 
to provide treatment, defendants will authorize another comparable provider. 
 
The hearing scheduled for May 14, 2025 is cancelled.
