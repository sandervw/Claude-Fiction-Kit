This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  Claimant, Ronald Lindauer, invokes the expedited 
procedure of rule 876 Iowa Administrative Code 4.48.  Prior to proceeding with the 
hearing scheduled for the alternate medical care claim on October 5, 2023, the parties 
reached out to the undersigned and proposed a consent order regarding the alternate 
medical care petition.   
 
The claimant made an appointment for the requested injection for October 12, 
2023, with Dr. Mark Kline.  Defendants will make sure that the appointment is 
authorized.   
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order on October 4, 
2023; thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED:

The claimant made an appointment for the requested injection with Dr. Mark 
Kline for October 12, 2023.   
  
-
