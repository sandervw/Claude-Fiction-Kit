On February 19, 2025, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care, 
seeking treatment for alleged injuries to his left arm/shoulder, neck, and back. Hearing 
was scheduled to take place on February 28, 2025. 
Prior to the scheduled hearing, the parties advised that they had reached a 
tentative agreement regarding the requested care. After a telephone conference to 
confirm the terms of the agreement, the hearing was determined to be unnecessary. 
The parties requested formal entry of the consent order to confirm and document the 
agreement. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED:   
Per the agreement of the parties, defendants agree to authorize claimant for 
evaluation of his neck, back, and shoulder complaints at Iowa Ortho. Defendants intend 
to schedule claimant for the same with Trevor Schmitz, M.D. (spine), and Timothy 
Vinyard, M.D. (shoulder). Defendants will have the appointments scheduled within ten 
(10) days of the date of this order, for the first available date with each doctor. 
Defendants reserve the right to schedule claimant with other specialists at Iowa 
Ortho in order to facilitate the most prompt treatment possible. In referring claimant to 
Iowa Ortho for evaluation and treatment, defendants agree to provide all treatment 
recommended by claimant’s treating providers that is reasonably related to his August 
3, 2023 injury. 
The hearing scheduled for February 28, 2025 is cancelled.
