On July 2, 2025, claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care. Hearing was 
scheduled to take place on July 14, 2025. 
Prior to the scheduled hearing, the parties advised they had reached an 
agreement regarding the requested care. Given the agreement of the parties, the 
hearing was determined to be unnecessary. The parties requested formal entry of the 
consent order to confirm and document the agreement. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED:   
Per the agreement of the parties, Defendants agree to authorize Frederick 
Fisher, M.D., to perform sacroiliac (SI) joint fusion surgery, per his recommendation. 
The hearing scheduled for July 14, 2025 is cancelled. 
Failure to comply with this order may result in sanctions under Iowa 
Administrative Code rule 876—4.36.
