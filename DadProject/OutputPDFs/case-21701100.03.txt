                 CONSENT ORDER  
          RE: ALTERNATE MEDICAL CARE 
 
 
 
 
Claimant filed an application for alternate medical care on June 12, 2023 
requesting Defendants be ordered to authorize treatment with Dr. Brendan Patterson at 
The University of Iowa Hospitals and Clinics.  
 
Prior to hearing, the parties contacted the undersigned Deputy and indicated 
Defendants have authorized treatment with Dr. Patterson concerning a possible re-tear 
to Claimant’s rotator cuff. 
 
The parties desired that a Consent Order be entered in this case, in lieu of a 
hearing. 
 
A hearing is therefore determined to be unnecessary, based on the agreement of 
the parties.  
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED THAT: 
 
Defendants shall authorize and pay for an appointment with Dr. Brendan Patterson 
and shall not withdraw authorization in advance of that appointment.  
 
IT IS SO ORDERED.
