Claimant’s Original Notice and Petition Concerning Application for Alternate 
Care, filed August 24, 2022, comes before this Deputy Iowa Workers’ Compensation 
Commissioner, Amanda Rutherford on September 7, 2022.  Defendants have filed an 
answer denying liability for claimant’s back complaints.  However, regarding claimant’s 
request for ongoing medication management for his bilateral upper extremities, the 
parties have come to an agreement and hereby stipulate and agree to the following 
language as to a Consent Order in relation to claimant’s medical treatment for the 
accepted conditions.  
 
Defendants will authorize lidocaine injections for Mr. Desseyn’s left carpal tunnel 
symptoms and treatment for the right carpal tunnel symptoms with a local provider per 
Dr. Buckwalter’s recommendation on June 14, 2022. Defendants deny liability for 
claimant’s back condition and have not agreed to authorize care for his back.  
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
The hearing scheduled for September 7, 2022 is cancelled.
