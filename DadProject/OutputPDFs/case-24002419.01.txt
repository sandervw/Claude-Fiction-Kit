On May 14, 2025, claimant filed an original notice and petition for alternate 
medical care in this file under Iowa Code section 85.27, invoking the provisions of rule 
876 IAC 4.48. Claimant requests a second opinion for her cervical spine/neck. 
Defendants are agreeable to authorizing a second opinion appointment with a spine 
surgeon. 
 
Hearing was scheduled for May 23, 2025. The parties have conferred and agree 
to formal entry of a consent order to confirm and document their agreement. 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED: 
 
On or before June 12, 2025, defendants shall schedule an appointment for 
claimant with a spine surgeon for a second opinion evaluation, and inform her of the 
date, time, location, and provider.  
 
The hearing scheduled for May 23, 2025 is cancelled.
