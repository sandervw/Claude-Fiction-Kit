This is a contested case concerning alternate medical care, proceeding under 
Iowa Code chapters 85 and 17A.  The expedited procedure of rule 876 Iowa 
Administrative Code 4.48 is invoked by claimant, Joy Rankin.  The alternate medical 
care claim proceeded with a telephone hearing on October 4, 2022, at 10:30 a.m.   
 
After discussing their positions, the parties discussed the entry of a consent order 
in this matter.  The defendant consents, stipulates, and agrees to provide conservative 
care pursuant to the recommendations of the treating physician, Jonathon Geisinger, 
M.D.   
 
The parties proposed that the undersigned issue this consent order in lieu of a 
formal decision.  All parties consented to the entry of a consent order verbally on 
October 4, 2022, thus no alternate medical care decision will be issued.   
ORDER 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
 
The parties have agreed that Defendant will provide conservative care pursuant 
to the recommendations of Dr. Geisinger.
