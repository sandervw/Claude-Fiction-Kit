          CONSENT ORDER  
          RE: ALTERNATE MEDICAL CARE 
 
 
 
 
On August 1, 2023, claimant filed an application for alternate medical care under 
Iowa Code section 85.27 ang agency rule 876 IAC 4.48, requesting that defendants be 
ordered to authorize treatment providers for evaluation and treatment of injuries to 
claimant’s head and left shoulder. Claimant also requested pain management for 
symptoms in claimant’s neck and lower back/spine.  The alternate care hearing was 
scheduled for August 14, 2023, at 10:30 a.m.   
 
Prior to hearing, defendants scheduled a September 14, 2023 appointment with 
Jonathan Fields, M.D., for additional evaluation and treatment of Claimant’s work injury. 
In addition, defendants have indicated they contacted Ashar Afzal, M.D., to schedule an 
appointment for pain management services. Defendants have agreed to schedule an 
appointment within 14 days of this order. 
 
Claimant desired that a Consent Order be entered in this case, in lieu of a 
hearing.  A hearing is therefore determined to be unnecessary, based on the agreement 
of the parties.  
 
 
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED THAT: 
 
 
Defendants shall schedule an appointment with Dr. Afzal within 14 days of the 
date of this order. The appointment need not occur within 14 days of this order, but an 
appointment date shall be scheduled within that period of time. 
 
 
IT IS SO ORDERED.
