Proposed Consent Order Re: 
Alternate Medical Care 
 
 
On June 16, 2023, the Claimant filed a petition for alternate medical care. Defendant filed 
its answer on June 27, 2023. The matter was scheduled for a hearing on June 28, 2023. 
 
On June 28, 2023, prior to the hearing, the parties stipulated Defendant’s denial of the 
right shoulder injury and reached an agreement regarding the requested care for the lumbar 
spine.   
 
Defendants have agreed to authorize the MRI of Claimant’s lumbar spine that has been 
recommended by Dr. James Gill and Claimant’s primary care provider.  This is the care being 
sought by the Claimant.   
 
Given that the defendants have agreed to authorize the requested care, the hearing was 
determined to be unnecessary.  The Claimant requested entry of a consent order to confirm and 
document the agreement, and Defendants agreed. 
 
IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED:   
 
Defendants have agreed and are ordered to authorize the MRI that has been 
recommended by Dr. James Gill and Claimant’s primary care provider.   
 
The hearing scheduled for June 28, 2023 is cancelled.
