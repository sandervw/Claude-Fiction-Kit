Claimant filed an application for alternate medical care on January 13, 2022, 
which requested an order directing defendants to authorize Dr. Frank Hawkins of Allen 
Pain Clinic to treat his lumbar spine condition pursuant to the recommendations of Dr. 
Segal. 
Defendants have denied this request pursuant to the opinions of Dr. Moyse and 
Dr. Abernathey. 
Prior to hearing, the parties contacted the undersigned and indicated that 
defendants have agreed to authorize and schedule an evaluation of claimant by Dr. 
Hawkins in Waterloo, Iowa. Claimant is satisfied with this authorization of care by 
defendants.  
Claimant desired that a Consent Order be entered in this case, in lieu of a 
hearing. 
A hearing is therefore determined to be unnecessary, based on the agreement of 
the parties.  
THEREFORE, IT IS ORDERED: 
Defendants are ordered to within the next ten (10) days provide written 
authorization to Dr. Frank Hawkins authorizing him to evaluate claimant.   
Signed and filed this _27th _ day of January, 2022.
