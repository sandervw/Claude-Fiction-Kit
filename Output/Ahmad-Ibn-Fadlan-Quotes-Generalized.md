# Ahmad Ibn Fadlan — Quotes

_The 13th Warrior (1999), directed by John McTiernan_

## Original

1. "I am Ahmed Ibn Fahdlan, Ibn Al Abbas, Ibn Rasid, Ibn Hammad."
2. "I am an ambassador, damn it! I am supposed to talk to people!"
3. "There is only one God and Muhammad is His prophet."
4. "I cannot lift this."
5. "I listened!"
6. "Yes, I can draw sounds, and I can speak them back."
7. "Merciful Father, I have squandered my days with plans of many things. This was not among them. But at this moment, I beg only to live the next few minutes well."
8. "They bid me take my place among them."
9. "Goodbye, Northman."
10. "Across seas of monsters and forests of demons we traveled. Praise be to Allah."

## Generalized

### Quote 1

**Structure:** Formal self-introduction asserting identity through ancestral chain.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Single sentence with rhythmic repetition building through lineage
- Identity established through heritage rather than personal achievement
- Register: dignified, formal, culturally grounded

**Respecification Seed:** A character among strangers asserts who they are by invoking the weight of their lineage.

---

### Quote 2

**Structure:** Frustrated assertion of professional identity against hostile circumstances.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Two short sentences, both exclamatory; profanity for emphasis
- Emphasis on role and function ("supposed to")
- Register: exasperated, comic indignation

**Respecification Seed:** A character with specialized skills finds themselves where those skills are useless, and protests.

---

### Quote 3

**Structure:** Declarative creed statement affirming core belief under pressure.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Single sentence, two-part parallel declaration
- Formal assertion of absolute philosophical/spiritual position
- Register: unwavering, reverent, non-negotiable

**Respecification Seed:** A character states their fundamental belief when confronted with alien worldviews or mortal danger.

---

### Quote 4

**Structure:** Blunt admission of physical inadequacy without excuse.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Four words, no elaboration or deflection
- Complete absence of face-saving
- Register: matter-of-fact, unashamed honesty

**Respecification Seed:** A character confronts a task beyond their capability and simply states the truth.

---

### Quote 5

**Structure:** Defiant one-word justification of unexpected competence.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Two words, exclamatory
- Implicit rebuke to those who underestimated
- Register: triumphant, slightly defensive pride

**Respecification Seed:** A character reveals surprising knowledge and credits patient observation when questioned.

---

### Quote 6

**Structure:** Confirmation of ability described in terms the uninitiated can grasp.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Single sentence, affirmation followed by concrete explanation
- Mundane skill reframed as wonder for audience lacking context
- Register: patient explanation, quiet pride

**Respecification Seed:** A character describes their common skill in primitive terms, revealing a worldview gap.

---

### Quote 7

**Structure:** Pre-battle prayer moving from regret through acceptance to simple plea.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Three sentences building from past to present; direct address to higher power
- Contrast between "plans of many things" and immediate survival
- Register: intimate, penitent, resolved

**Respecification Seed:** A character facing likely death releases attachment to unlived futures and asks only for courage now.

---

### Quote 8

**Structure:** Simple announcement of acceptance into fellowship.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Single sentence, passive construction—belonging conferred, not claimed
- Understated significance; the momentousness is in the fact, not the phrasing
- Register: quiet wonder, gratitude

**Respecification Seed:** An outsider reports being invited into fellowship by those who once saw them as other.

---

### Quote 9

**Structure:** Terse farewell acknowledging someone by their origin or role.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Two words; no sentiment expressed directly
- Identity reduced to role or origin—respectful but maintaining distance
- Register: formal restraint masking emotion

**Respecification Seed:** A character parts from someone they've grown to respect, marking the bond with minimal words.

---

### Quote 10

**Structure:** Epic journey summary followed by religious gratitude.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Two sentences: first epic/poetic with parallel structure, second a stock phrase of thanksgiving
- "Seas of...forests of" parallelism evokes mythic scale
- Register: retrospective awe, devout closure

**Respecification Seed:** A character concludes an account of harrowing travel by naming its terrors and crediting higher power for survival.
