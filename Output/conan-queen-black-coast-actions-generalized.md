# Conan — Generalized Actions

_Source: "Queen of the Black Coast" by Robert E. Howard_

## Original Actions

1. Flees through Argos after killing a magistrate who demanded he betray a friend
2. Leaps aboard the departing merchant galley Argus to escape pursuing guards
3. Threatens the shipmaster Tito with his sword to demand passage
4. Sheathes his weapon and accepts the captain's invitation for ale
5. Requests a bow when the pirate ship Tigress approaches
6. Shoots arrows at Belit's spearmen from the poop deck during the attack
7. Rallies the Argus crew after Captain Tito is killed in battle
8. Leaps aboard the Tigress alone when the fight becomes hopeless
9. Kills numerous black warriors in furious close combat while surrounded
10. Freezes when Belit commands her warriors to spare him
11. Accepts Belit's offer to become her mate and co-leader of her pirates
12. Raids the Black Coast alongside Belit for years, earning the name "Amra"
13. Participates in slaughtering Stygian ships and coastal settlements with Belit's crew
14. Sails up the forbidden river Zarkheba with Belit seeking a lost city's treasure
15. Discusses theology with Belit, comparing Crom to her Shemite deities
16. Spots the winged creature atop a column and watches it fly into the jungle
17. Helps lift the altar in the ruined tower, triggering a collapse that kills four men
18. Examines the revealed crypt filled with gems beneath the fallen altar
19. Volunteers to lead twenty warriors into the jungle to find fresh water
20. Halts his men and steps behind a tree to investigate stalking presences
21. Inhales the Black Lotus and falls unconscious in the jungle
22. Awakens from drug-induced visions and tracks his lost spearmen
23. Kills N'Gora with his sword after the maddened warrior attacks him
24. Discovers the cliff where the remaining spearmen fell to their deaths
25. Rushes back to the galley and finds Belit hanged from the yard-arm
26. Wraps Belit's body in his scarlet cloak and lays her on a pyre of broken benches
27. Positions himself atop the pyramid with bow and arrows to await the monsters
28. Shoots down the charging were-hyenas in a hail of arrows
29. Fights the remaining creatures in brutal hand-to-hand combat atop the pyramid
30. Becomes trapped under debris when the pyramid partially collapses
31. Heaves away the stone pinning him after Belit's spirit intervenes
32. Charges the winged monster and bisects it with a single sword stroke
33. Cuts the Tigress loose and lets the current carry her toward the sea
34. Watches from the beach as the burning ship disappears over the horizon
