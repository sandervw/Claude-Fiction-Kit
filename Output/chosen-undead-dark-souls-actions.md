# The Chosen Undead — Actions

_Dark Souls by FromSoftware_

---

## Part I: Common Path — Undead Asylum to the Bells of Awakening

1. Receives the Dungeon Cell Key from a dying knight and escapes imprisonment
2. Claims an Estus Flask and key from the mortally wounded Oscar of Astora
3. Defeats the Asylum Demon guarding the only exit from the asylum
4. Curls into a ball at the cliff's edge, allowing the Giant Crow to carry them to Lordran
5. Speaks with the Crestfallen Warrior at Firelink Shrine to learn of the Bells of Awakening
6. Ascends through the Undead Burg, cutting down hollowed soldiers blocking the path
7. Defeats the Taurus Demon atop the battlements overlooking the bridge
8. Crosses the Hellkite Dragon's bridge by sprinting through sheets of flame
9. Kicks down the ladder shortcut connecting the Undead Burg to Firelink Shrine
10. Fights through the Undead Parish and defeats the armored Berenike Knight
11. Frees Lautrec of Carim from his cell in the church
12. Rescues the Fire Keeper's soul from an ambush in Blighttown (or witnesses its theft)
13. Ascends the bell tower and defeats the Bell Gargoyles in aerial combat
14. Rings the first Bell of Awakening atop the Undead Parish
15. Purchases the Basement Key and descends into the Lower Undead Burg
16. Defeats the Capra Demon and its attack dogs in the cramped arena
17. Ventures into the Depths and defeats the Gaping Dragon to access Blighttown
18. Descends through the poison-filled swamps of Blighttown
19. Confronts and defeats Chaos Witch Quelaag in her domain
20. Rings the second Bell of Awakening beneath Blighttown
21. Watches as the gates of Sen's Fortress grind open from Firelink Shrine

---

## Part II: The Journey to the Lordvessel

22. Navigates the deadly traps and swinging blades of Sen's Fortress
23. Defeats the Iron Golem at the fortress summit
24. Is carried by Batwing Demons over the walls into Anor Londo
25. Fights through the Silver Knights guarding the city of the gods
26. Crosses the perilous rafters above the cathedral
27. Defeats Dragonslayer Ornstein and Executioner Smough in the grand cathedral
28. Enters the chamber of the Princess of Sunlight
29. Receives the Lordvessel from Gwynevere, Princess of Sunlight
30. Warps to Firelink Shrine bearing the vessel of the gods

---

## Part III: Meeting the Primordial Serpent

31. Descends into the flooded ruins beneath Firelink Shrine
32. Meets Kingseeker Frampt, the Primordial Serpent who claims to have awaited the Chosen Undead

---

# PATH A: Link the Fire

_Following Kingseeker Frampt's guidance to rekindle the First Flame_

## Accepting Frampt's Covenant

33. Listens to Frampt's account of Gwyn's sacrifice and the prophecy of the Chosen Undead
34. Agrees to succeed Lord Gwyn and perpetuate the Age of Fire
35. Places the Lordvessel upon the Firelink Altar at Frampt's direction
36. Learns of the four Lord Souls needed to satiate the Lordvessel

## Gathering the Lord Souls

**The Duke's Archives and Crystal Cave:**

37. Enters the Duke's Archives through the golden fog gate
38. Is captured by Seath the Scaleless and imprisoned in the archives
39. Escapes the prison tower and navigates the deadly crystal archives
40. Locates and destroys the Primordial Crystal granting Seath immortality
41. Defeats Seath the Scaleless and claims his Bequeathed Lord Soul Shard

**New Londo Ruins and the Abyss:**

42. Purchases the Crest of Artorias from Andre the Blacksmith
43. Enters the sealed forest of Darkroot Garden
44. Defeats Great Grey Wolf Sif and claims the Covenant of Artorias ring
45. Obtains the Key to the Seal from Ingward in New Londo Ruins
46. Drains the flooded ruins by releasing the seal
47. Equips the Covenant of Artorias and leaps into the Abyss
48. Defeats the Four Kings and claims their Bequeathed Lord Soul Shard

**Tomb of the Giants:**

49. Descends through the Catacombs and defeats Pinwheel
50. Navigates the pitch-black Tomb of the Giants using a light source
51. Defeats Gravelord Nito, First of the Dead, and claims his Lord Soul

**Lost Izalith:**

52. Descends through the Demon Ruins past the Firesage Demon and Centipede Demon
53. Enters the ruins of Lost Izalith, birthplace of demons
54. Destroys the two glowing roots protecting the Bed of Chaos
55. Slays the Bed of Chaos, the Witch of Izalith's corrupted form, and claims her Lord Soul

## The Final Confrontation

56. Returns to Firelink Altar and offers the four Lord Souls to the Lordvessel
57. Watches as the passage to the Kiln of the First Flame opens
58. Walks the ash-strewn path through the Kiln, defeating five Black Knight sentinels
59. Enters the chamber of the First Flame and faces Gwyn, Lord of Cinder
60. Defeats the hollowed god in single combat
61. Approaches the dying First Flame and chooses to link the fire
62. Sacrifices their soul to rekindle the First Flame, becoming the new Lord of Cinder

# PATH B: Age of Dark

_Following Darkstalker Kaathe's guidance to usher in the Age of Dark_

## Rejecting Frampt's Deception

33. Refuses to immediately place the Lordvessel for Kingseeker Frampt
34. Seeks another path forward, suspicious of the serpent's true motives

## The Four Kings and the Second Serpent

**New Londo Ruins and the Abyss (before placing the Lordvessel):**

35. Purchases the Crest of Artorias from Andre the Blacksmith
36. Enters the sealed forest of Darkroot Garden
37. Defeats Great Grey Wolf Sif and claims the Covenant of Artorias ring
38. Obtains the Key to the Seal from Ingward in New Londo Ruins
39. Drains the flooded ruins by releasing the seal
40. Equips the Covenant of Artorias and leaps into the Abyss
41. Defeats the Four Kings and claims their Bequeathed Lord Soul Shard
42. Meets Darkstalker Kaathe, who rises from the darkness of the Abyss
43. Listens to Kaathe's revelation about the Furtive Pygmy and the stolen Dark Soul
44. Learns that Gwyn feared humanity and the Age of Dark that was meant to come
45. Accepts Kaathe's guidance to become the Dark Lord and usher in the Age of Man
46. Places the Lordvessel upon the Firelink Altar under Kaathe's direction

## Gathering the Lord Souls

**The Duke's Archives and Crystal Cave:**

47. Enters the Duke's Archives through the golden fog gate
48. Is captured by Seath the Scaleless and imprisoned in the archives
49. Escapes the prison tower and navigates the deadly crystal archives
50. Locates and destroys the Primordial Crystal granting Seath immortality
51. Defeats Seath the Scaleless and claims his Bequeathed Lord Soul Shard

**Tomb of the Giants:**

52. Descends through the Catacombs and defeats Pinwheel
53. Navigates the pitch-black Tomb of the Giants using a light source
54. Defeats Gravelord Nito, First of the Dead, and claims his Lord Soul

**Lost Izalith:**

55. Descends through the Demon Ruins past the Firesage Demon and Centipede Demon
56. Enters the ruins of Lost Izalith, birthplace of demons
57. Destroys the two glowing roots protecting the Bed of Chaos
58. Slays the Bed of Chaos, the Witch of Izalith's corrupted form, and claims her Lord Soul

## The Final Confrontation

59. Returns to Firelink Altar and offers the remaining Lord Souls to the Lordvessel
60. Watches as the passage to the Kiln of the First Flame opens
61. Walks the ash-strewn path through the Kiln, defeating five Black Knight sentinels
62. Enters the chamber of the First Flame and faces Gwyn, Lord of Cinder
63. Defeats the hollowed god in single combat
64. Turns away from the dying First Flame and walks out of the Kiln
65. Is greeted by the Primordial Serpents, who bow before their new sovereign
66. Becomes the Dark Lord and ushers in the Age of Dark — the Age of Man
