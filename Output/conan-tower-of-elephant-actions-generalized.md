# Conan — Generalized Actions

_Source: "The Tower of the Elephant" by Robert E. Howard_

## Original Actions

1. Listens to thieves in a tavern describe the fabled Heart of the Elephant jewel
2. Questions a Kothian rogue about the Elephant Tower and its defenses
3. Kills the Kothian in a tavern brawl after being mocked and attacked
4. Escapes the darkened tavern into the moonlit streets of Arenjun
5. Approaches the Tower of the Elephant after nightfall to steal the jewel
6. Scales the outer wall surrounding Yara's garden compound
7. Discovers a strangled guard and deduces another thief is present
8. Confronts Taurus of Nemedia and agrees to partner on the heist
9. Watches Taurus neutralize the garden lions with black lotus powder
10. Kills a surviving lion that attacks him with a decisive sword strike
11. Follows Taurus to the tower base and prepares to climb
12. Ascends the sheer tower wall using Taurus's grappling hook and rope
13. Reaches the jeweled parapet at the tower's summit
14. Discovers Taurus dead from a giant spider's venomous bite
15. Battles the enormous spider guardian in the treasure chamber
16. Crushes the spider to death by hurling a heavy chest of gems at it
17. Descends deeper into the tower searching for the Heart of the Elephant
18. Discovers Yag-kosha, a blinded and tortured alien prisoner
19. Listens to Yag-kosha recount his cosmic origins and betrayal by Yara
20. Agrees to fulfill the dying creature's request for vengeance
21. Takes the Heart of the Elephant jewel from its resting place
22. Kills Yag-kosha with a merciful blade stroke at the creature's request
23. Extracts Yag-kosha's heart from the corpse
24. Drips the heart's blood onto the Heart of the Elephant jewel
25. Carries the blood-infused gem to the sleeping sorcerer Yara
26. Places the enchanted jewel before Yara, triggering its magic
27. Watches as Yara shrinks and is drawn screaming into the gem
28. Flees the tower immediately as instructed by Yag-kosha
29. Emerges from the collapsing tower at dawn with only his sword and clothes

## Generalized Actions

1. Gathers intelligence on legendary treasure from underworld contacts
2. Questions local rogue about target location and its defenses
3. Kills antagonist in tavern brawl after provocation and attack
4. Escapes violence into city streets under cover of darkness
5. Approaches fortified target after nightfall to steal treasure
6. Scales outer wall surrounding sorcerer's compound
7. Discovers slain guard and deduces rival thief is present
8. Confronts fellow thief and agrees to partner on heist
9. Observes partner neutralize guard beasts with alchemical powder
10. Kills surviving beast with decisive sword strike when attacked
11. Follows partner to tower base and prepares vertical ascent
12. Ascends sheer tower wall using partner's climbing equipment
13. Reaches ornate parapet at the tower's summit
14. Discovers partner dead from guardian creature's venom
15. Battles monstrous guardian in treasure chamber alone
16. Kills guardian by improvising heavy object as crushing weapon
17. Descends deeper into tower searching for legendary treasure
18. Discovers imprisoned otherworldly being tortured by sorcerer
19. Listens to prisoner recount origins and betrayal by captor
20. Agrees to fulfill dying prisoner's request for vengeance
21. Takes legendary jewel from its resting place
22. Grants merciful death to suffering creature at its request
23. Extracts heart from prisoner's corpse for ritual purpose
24. Performs blood ritual on enchanted jewel as instructed
25. Carries enchanted gem to sleeping sorcerer as weapon
26. Places cursed jewel before sorcerer, triggering vengeful magic
27. Witnesses sorcerer's destruction as magic consumes him
28. Flees collapsing structure immediately as instructed
29. Emerges from destruction at dawn with only sword and clothes
