# Voice Revision: Radiant Optimist

**Source:** Input/input.md (Lord Gro's counsel from *The Worm Ouroboros*)
**Voice Guide:** output/voice-paladin.md

---

"Ohh, since you welcome my thoughts, Lord, I shall share them gladly. You know well that Corinius is no friend of mine... no friend at all. And yet, if I speak for him, surely you will see it is not self-interest that moves me. Hah! In truth, if Corund cannot go, then Corinius is your finest hope. A whole soldier, steadfast in purpose, bright with resolve. Once he rises to a task he does not sit down again until it is done. Send him to Demonland, I say. ...Surely we are stronger for it."

"Aha! Here is a thought, then. Give the glory to Corsus, readily believed, but let Corinius do the rude work of it beneath him. A secretary in name, a sword arm in deed. Your purpose shall be all the better served, Lord, and all shall be well."

"Ohh, my Lord the King, when I said I liked not Corsus, you did laugh. Quite right, quite right. Hah! But I did not say it from mere fussiness, I assure you. I fear he shall prove a false cloth... the sort that shrinks in the wetting and cannot endure a proper trial. These things trouble me, though I confess I may be wrong."

"Lord, in all your wise argument you have quite overturned me, and I welcome the correction. ...And yet my heart misgives me still. You would ride to Galing. You have chosen a horse for it with never a star in his forehead. Instead, I see there is a cloud in his face, and such horses prove commonly furious, dogged, full of mischief and misfortune. ...I do hope I am wrong. Truly, truly I hope it."
