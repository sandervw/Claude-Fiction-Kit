# The Nerevarine — Generalized Actions

_Source: The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind by Bethesda Softworks_

## Original Actions

1. Awakens aboard an Imperial prison ship docked in Seyda Neen
2. Completes registration at the Census and Excise Office to receive release papers
3. Accepts the coded package and orders to find Caius Cosades in Balmora

7. Meets Hasphat Antabolis at the Balmora Fighters Guild and agrees to retrieve a Dwemer artifact
8. Enters the Dwemer ruin Arkngthand and retrieves the Dwemer puzzle box
9. Returns the puzzle box and receives notes on the Sixth House cult
10. Visits Sharn gra-Muzgob at the Balmora Mages Guild
11. Travels to the Andrano Ancestral Tomb and retrieves the Skull of Llevule Andrano
12. Delivers the skull and receives notes on the Nerevarine Cult
13. Travels to Vivec City and speaks with three informants about the prophecies
14. Locates Hassour Zainsubani in Ald'ruhn and learns Ashlander customs
15. Reports findings to Caius Cosades and receives promotion to Blades Finder
16. Travels north to the Urshilaku Camp to meet the Ashlander leaders
17. Presents gifts to Ashkhan Sul-Matuul and requests initiation into the tribe
18. Enters the Urshilaku Burial Caverns and retrieves the Bonebiter Bow of Sul-Senipul
19. Returns the bow to Sul-Matuul and is named Clanfriend of the Ashlanders
20. Speaks with Wise Woman Nibani Maesa about the Nerevarine prophecies
21. Returns to Caius and receives orders to destroy a Sixth House base near Gnaar Mok
22. Infiltrates the Sixth House base at Ilunibi caverns
23. Confronts and slays the Ash Ghoul priest Dagoth Gares in combat
24. Contracts Corprus disease from Dagoth Gares's dying curse
25. Returns to Caius and learns of Divayth Fyr, who may have a cure
26. Travels to Tel Fyr and presents a Dwemer artifact to the wizard Divayth Fyr
27. Agrees to retrieve the Dwemer Boots of Flying in exchange for the cure
28. Enters the Corprusarium beneath Tel Fyr
29. Navigates past the afflicted inmates without harming them
30. Locates Yagrum Bagarn, the last living Dwemer, and receives the boots
31. Returns the boots to Divayth Fyr and receives the experimental cure potion
32. Drinks the potion and survives, becoming immune to all disease while retaining Corprus
33. Returns to Balmora and learns Caius has been recalled to the Imperial City
34. Receives final orders to find the lost prophecies and bring them to Nibani Maesa
35. Travels to the Hall of Wisdom in Vivec to locate Mehra Milo
