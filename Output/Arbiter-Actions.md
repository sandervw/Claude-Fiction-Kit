# The Arbiter (Thel 'Vadam) — Actions

_Halo 2 & Halo 3 by Bungie_

## Halo 2

### Pre-Arbiter (Prologue)

1. Commands the Fleet of Particular Justice in pursuit of the Pillar of Autumn
2. Wages power struggle with the Prophet of Stewardship over Installation 04 command
3. Orders ship evacuation and shoots down vessels showing signs of Flood contamination
4. Retreats from the ring's destruction, losing his fleet and the battle
5. Stands trial before the High Council for failing to protect the Sacred Ring
6. Receives the Mark of Shame—branded a heretic before the assembled Covenant

### Accepting the Mantle

7. Accepts the Prophets' offer to become Arbiter rather than face execution
8. Dons the ancient Arbiter armor and energy sword

### The Arbiter / The Oracle (Heretic Suppression)

9. Leads Special Operations Elites to the Threshold gas mine facility
10. Opens the hangar door to allow Phantom reinforcements inside
11. Fights through Heretic-controlled sections alongside loyal forces
12. Engages Forerunner Sentinels for the first time during the assault
13. Pilots a Heretic Banshee to pursue the fleeing Heretic Leader
14. Severs the facility's three support cables, sending the station plummeting
15. Corners and assassinates the Heretic Leader Sesa 'Refumee before his escape
16. Captures 343 Guilty Spark, discovering the AI's role in the "heresy"

### Sacred Icon / Quarantine Zone (Retrieving the Index)

17. Deploys to Installation 05 with Spec Ops Commander Rtas 'Vadumee
18. Battles through the Sentinel Wall to reach the Library
19. Deactivates the quarantine shield blocking access to the Sacred Icon
20. Fights through Flood-infested Quarantine Zone alongside Spec Ops forces
21. Uses active camouflage to approach Miranda Keyes and Sergeant Johnson
22. Headbutts Johnson unconscious and disarms Keyes to secure the Index
23. Presents the Index to Tartarus, expecting glory

### Betrayal

24. Learns from Tartarus that the Prophets have replaced Elites with Brutes
25. Gets blasted into a deep shaft by Tartarus using the Fist of Rukt

### Gravemind (Turning Point)

26. Awakens in the grip of the Gravemind alongside Master Chief
27. Listens as the Gravemind explains the true nature of the Halos
28. Agrees to a temporary alliance with his former enemy, the Demon
29. Gets teleported by the Gravemind to the surface of Delta Halo

### Uprising (Civil War Begins)

30. Lands in the jungle near Installation 05's Control Room
31. Rallies scattered Sangheili survivors against the Brute forces
32. Leads the counterattack against Brute patrols hunting his kind
33. Fights through Brute-held positions to reach the Control Room valley
34. Witnesses the full scope of the Great Schism—Elite against Brute

### The Great Journey (Final Confrontation)

35. Joins forces with Rtas 'Vadumee and the Sangheili separatists
36. Allies with Sergeant Johnson and the surviving humans
37. Commandeers a Wraith tank to assault the Control Room approach
38. Clears landing zones for Sangheili reinforcements
39. Pilots a Banshee to support Johnson's Scarab assault on the Control Room
40. Enters the Control Room as Johnson blasts open the doors
41. Confronts Tartarus over the true purpose of the Halos
42. Kills Tartarus in prolonged combat while Johnson provides sniper support
43. Helps Miranda Keyes remove the Index, stopping the firing sequence
44. Watches the Halo Array enter standby mode, threatening activation from the Ark
45. Changes his name from 'Vadamee to 'Vadam, severing ties to the Covenant

## Halo 3

### Return to Earth

46. Arrives at Earth aboard the Shadow of Intent with Johnson and Keyes
47. Discovers Master Chief's crashed pod in the Tanzanian jungle
48. Prevents a Marine from attacking the disoriented Spartan
49. Guides Chief through the jungle toward the extraction point

### Crow's Nest / Tsavo Highway

50. Defends Crow's Nest base alongside Chief during the Covenant assault
51. Fights through the base's corridors against Brute and Drone forces
52. Escapes the base before its self-destruct detonation

### The Storm / Floodgate

53. Participates in the Battle of Voi, clearing Covenant anti-air positions
54. Battles the Flood infestation after their ship crashes at Voi
55. Recovers Cortana's fragmented message from the Flood ship
56. Convinces Shipmaster Rtas 'Vadum to use a surgical strike rather than glass Earth

### The Ark

57. Joins the assault on the Ark through the portal at Voi
58. Fights alongside Chief at the Silent Cartographer facility
59. Leads Sangheili forces to deactivate one of three shield generators
60. Clears Flood forces from the third tower alongside Marines
61. Escorts 343 Guilty Spark to assess damage to the Ark

### The Covenant

62. Confronts the Prophet of Truth at the Ark's Citadel
63. Executes Truth with his energy sword as the Gravemind watches
64. Escapes through ventilation shafts as the Gravemind turns hostile

### Cortana / Halo

65. Fights through the Flood-infested High Charity alongside Chief
66. Uses a flamethrower to provide cover during the escape
67. Drives a Warthog across the partially-constructed Installation 08
68. Pilots the Forward Unto Dawn's bow section back through the portal to Earth
69. Attends the Hillside Memorial ceremony, honoring the fallen
70. Shakes hands with Admiral Hood, formalizing peace between their species
