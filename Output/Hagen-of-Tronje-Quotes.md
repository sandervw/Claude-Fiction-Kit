# Hagen of Tronje — Quotes

_The Nibelungenlied (c. 1200), Anonymous. Translations by Daniel Shumway, Margaret Armour, and A.T. Hatto._

1. "This is mighty Siegfried. He is the great warrior who slew the Nibelungs, then took possession of their treasure. We must receive him with chivalry and honor, and seek his friendship."

2. "Furthermore, the great hero slew a dragon and bathed in its blood, which made him invincible against all weapons. No mortal can defeat him in combat."

3. "What now, King Gunther? We are done for — the woman whose love you desire is a rib of the Devil himself!"

4. "No man who is firm in his purpose should leave the treasure to a woman. By means of her gifts she will bring things to the point where the brave sons of Burgundy will bitterly regret it."

5. "I predict that if Kriemhild marries King Etzel, she will use her newly gained power to do us great harm."

6. "I killed Kriemhild's husband with my own hand, and she will be seeking revenge against us all."

7. "We are moved by honor, not by dreams."

8. "My lords commanded that it be sunk in the Rhine, and there it shall remain forever!"

9. "Yes, it was I who killed Siegfried. I did so for the pain that you caused my mistress Brunhild. And if anyone dare avenge this act, man or woman, then let him or her try!"

10. "Now none knows of the treasure but God and I. It shall stay hidden from you forever!"
