# Lucien Lachance — Quotes

_The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion by Bethesda Game Studios_

## Original

1. "You sleep rather soundly for a murderer. That's good. You'll need a clear conscience for what I'm about to propose."
2. "Dear Brother, I do not spread rumors. I create them."
3. "Imagine a perfect, cloudless midnight, cold as winter ice and shrouded in shadow. That is Sithis."
4. "We kill for profit, for enjoyment, and for the glory of the Dread Father, Sithis."
5. "We are family, with bonds forged in blood and death."
6. "The slaying of Rufio was the signing of a covenant. The manner of execution, your signature. Rufio's blood, the ink."
7. "From this moment forward, you will walk the shadows as my Silencer."
8. "I'll be following... your progress. Welcome to the family."
9. "Go now, Child of Sithis. Walk in the shadow of fear, and bring glory to our Dread Father."
10. "Don't you see? You and I have been deceived. We must find out who is behind this betrayal!"

## Generalized

### Quote 1

**Structure:** Observational paradox followed by ominous approval and dark proposition.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Three sentences: first longer, next two short and punchy
- Opens with ironic observation about the addressee (peaceful sleep contradicting violent nature)
- "That's good" pivot creates menacing approval rather than judgment
- Register: calm, knowing, sinister seduction

**Respecification Seed:** A recruiter approaches someone who has crossed a moral line, offering belonging rather than judgment.

---

### Quote 2

**Structure:** Formal address + denial that pivots to superior claim.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Two sentences, second half the length of first
- Affectionate honorific establishes in-group intimacy
- Parallel structure with escalating verb (spread → create)
- Register: sardonic pride, theatrical confidence

**Respecification Seed:** A character corrects a misunderstanding of their role by claiming greater power than assumed.

---

### Quote 3

**Structure:** Evocative imagery command followed by terse identification.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Two sentences: first elaborate and sensory, second blunt
- "Imagine" as invitation into shared vision
- Stacked adjectives create atmospheric density
- Register: reverent, instructive, almost liturgical

**Respecification Seed:** A character explains an abstract or divine concept through sensory metaphor.

---

### Quote 4

**Structure:** Declaration of purpose through ascending triadic list.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Single sentence with triadic structure
- Escalation from base (profit) through transgressive (enjoyment) to sacred (glory)
- First-person plural establishes collective identity
- Register: proud, unapologetic, ritualistic

**Respecification Seed:** A character states their group's ethos, embracing rather than excusing its dark nature.

---

### Quote 5

**Structure:** Simple identity claim with violent metaphorical elaboration.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Single sentence, two clauses
- "Family" domesticates what follows; the violence becomes intimacy
- "Forged" implies permanence and intentionality
- Register: warm despite content, matter-of-fact about darkness

**Respecification Seed:** A character welcomes another into a group bound by shared transgression rather than birth.

---

### Quote 6

**Structure:** Extended contractual metaphor with escalating fragments.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Three units, progressively shorter (full sentence → fragment → fragment)
- Legal/signing metaphor sustained through all three
- Transforms violence into bureaucratic formality
- Register: formal, almost legalistic, ceremonial

**Respecification Seed:** A character reframes a violent act as the addressee's binding initiation into an organization.

---

### Quote 7

**Structure:** Temporal marker + role assignment with possessive claim.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Single sentence, formal announcement structure
- "From this moment forward" creates ritual boundary
- Possessive + role title establishes hierarchical intimacy
- Register: ceremonial, authoritative, conferring honor

**Respecification Seed:** A mentor formally elevates someone to a position of trust and purpose.

---

### Quote 8

**Structure:** Ominous promise with pause + warm invitation.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Two short sentences
- Ellipsis creates menacing ambiguity (following you, or your progress?)
- Pivot from surveillance to warmth
- Register: controlled, watchful, warmth undercut by implied threat

**Respecification Seed:** A superior welcomes a new member while making clear they will be observed.

---

### Quote 9

**Structure:** Benediction/sending with honorific address and mission statement.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Two sentences, imperative mood throughout
- Honorific title places addressee in lineage ("Child of...")
- Dual imperative: embodiment + action
- Register: liturgical, parental, blessing-as-command

**Respecification Seed:** A spiritual authority sends an agent forth on a mission framed as sacred duty.

---

### Quote 10

**Structure:** Rhetorical question + revelation + call to action.

**Rhetorical DNA:**
- Three short sentences, escalating urgency
- "You and I" creates conspiratorial alliance
- Shift from authority to apparent vulnerability (may itself be manipulation)
- Register: urgent, frustrated, seeking partnership

**Respecification Seed:** An authority figure pivots to seeking alliance when they discover shared victimhood.
