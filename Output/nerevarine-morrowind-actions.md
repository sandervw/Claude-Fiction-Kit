# The Nerevarine — Actions

_The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind by Bethesda Softworks_

---

## Part I: Arrival and the Blades

1. Awakens aboard an Imperial prison ship docked in Seyda Neen
2. Completes registration at the Census and Excise Office to receive release papers
3. Accepts the coded package and orders to find Caius Cosades in Balmora
4. Travels to Balmora and locates Caius Cosades in his home
5. Delivers the Emperor's sealed package and joins the Blades as a Novice
6. Accepts orders to gather information on the Nerevarine Cult and Sixth House

---

## Part II: Intelligence Gathering

7. Meets Hasphat Antabolis at the Balmora Fighters Guild and agrees to retrieve a Dwemer artifact
8. Enters the Dwemer ruin Arkngthand and retrieves the Dwemer puzzle box
9. Returns the puzzle box and receives notes on the Sixth House cult
10. Visits Sharn gra-Muzgob at the Balmora Mages Guild
11. Travels to the Andrano Ancestral Tomb and retrieves the Skull of Llevule Andrano
12. Delivers the skull and receives notes on the Nerevarine Cult
13. Travels to Vivec City and speaks with three informants about the prophecies
14. Locates Hassour Zainsubani in Ald'ruhn and learns Ashlander customs
15. Reports findings to Caius Cosades and receives promotion to Blades Finder

---

## Part III: The Urshilaku and the Sixth House

16. Travels north to the Urshilaku Camp to meet the Ashlander leaders
17. Presents gifts to Ashkhan Sul-Matuul and requests initiation into the tribe
18. Enters the Urshilaku Burial Caverns and retrieves the Bonebiter Bow of Sul-Senipul
19. Returns the bow to Sul-Matuul and is named Clanfriend of the Ashlanders
20. Speaks with Wise Woman Nibani Maesa about the Nerevarine prophecies
21. Returns to Caius and receives orders to destroy a Sixth House base near Gnaar Mok
22. Infiltrates the Sixth House base at Ilunibi caverns
23. Confronts and slays the Ash Ghoul priest Dagoth Gares in combat
24. Contracts Corprus disease from Dagoth Gares's dying curse
25. Returns to Caius and learns of Divayth Fyr, who may have a cure

---

## Part IV: The Corprus Cure

26. Travels to Tel Fyr and presents a Dwemer artifact to the wizard Divayth Fyr
27. Agrees to retrieve the Dwemer Boots of Flying in exchange for the cure
28. Enters the Corprusarium beneath Tel Fyr
29. Navigates past the afflicted inmates without harming them
30. Locates Yagrum Bagarn, the last living Dwemer, and receives the boots
31. Returns the boots to Divayth Fyr and receives the experimental cure potion
32. Drinks the potion and survives, becoming immune to all disease while retaining Corprus

---

## Part V: The Lost Prophecies

33. Returns to Balmora and learns Caius has been recalled to the Imperial City
34. Receives final orders to find the lost prophecies and bring them to Nibani Maesa
35. Travels to the Hall of Wisdom in Vivec to locate Mehra Milo
36. Discovers Mehra has been taken to the Ministry of Truth prison
37. Acquires the prison key and infiltrates the Ministry of Truth
38. Frees Mehra Milo from her cell and gives her a Divine Intervention scroll to escape
39. Meets Blatta Hateria on the Ebonheart docks and requests passage to Holamayan
40. Travels by boat to the secret monastery of the Dissident Priests
41. Enters Holamayan at dawn or dusk when Azura's magic unseals the entrance
42. Speaks with Gilvas Barelo and receives the lost prophecy texts

---

## Part VI: The Third Trial — Cavern of the Incarnate

43. Returns the prophecy texts to Nibani Maesa at the Urshilaku Camp
44. Learns of the Third Trial and the riddle of the Cavern of the Incarnate
45. Deciphers the riddle by speaking with Urshilaku Ashlanders
46. Travels to the Valley of the Wind in the Ashlands, marked by Airan's Teeth
47. Waits at the cavern entrance for dawn or dusk, when Azura's Star appears
48. Enters the Cavern of the Incarnate during the magical twilight hours
49. Takes the Moon-and-Star ring from the statue of Azura
50. Receives Azura's vision confirming identity as the Nerevarine reborn
51. Speaks with the ghosts of failed Incarnates and receives their enchanted gifts
52. Returns to Nibani Maesa to learn of the remaining trials

---

## Part VII: Fourth Trial — Hortator of the Great Houses

**House Redoran:**

53. Travels to Ald'ruhn and speaks with Athyn Sarethi about becoming Hortator
54. Infiltrates Venim Manor and rescues Varvur Sarethi from imprisonment
55. Returns Varvur to his father and earns Athyn Sarethi's vote for Hortator
56. Convinces the other Redoran councilors to vote for Hortator
57. Challenges Archmaster Bolvyn Venim after he refuses to support the claim
58. Duels and defeats Bolvyn Venim in the Vivec Arena
59. Returns to Athyn Sarethi and is named Hortator of House Redoran

**House Hlaalu:**

60. Travels to Vivec and meets Crassius Curio to learn about Hlaalu politics
61. Secures votes from Crassius Curio, Dram Bero, and Yngling Half-Troll
62. Confronts Orvas Dren at his stronghold and convinces or eliminates him
63. Secures the remaining votes from Velanda Omani and Nevena Ules
64. Returns to Crassius Curio and is named Hortator of House Hlaalu

**House Telvanni:**

65. Travels to Tel Vos and speaks with Master Aryon, who immediately supports the claim
66. Convinces or intimidates Mistress Dratha in Tel Mora to support the claim
67. Raises Master Neloth's disposition and secures his vote in Sadrith Mora
68. Speaks with the addled Mistress Therana in Tel Branora and gains her support
69. Slays Archmagister Gothren when he refuses to acknowledge the claim
70. Returns to Master Aryon and is named Hortator of House Telvanni

---

## Part VIII: Fifth Trial — Nerevarine of the Ashlander Tribes

**Urshilaku Tribe:**

71. Returns to Sul-Matuul, who names the Clanfriend as Urshilaku Nerevarine

**Ahemmusa Tribe:**

72. Travels to the Ahemmusa Camp in the Grazelands
73. Speaks with Wise Woman Sinnammu Mirpal about gaining the tribe's recognition
74. Clears the Daedric shrine of Ald Daedroth of hostile creatures
75. Escorts Sinnammu Mirpal safely into the inner shrine of Ald Daedroth
76. Is named Ahemmusa Nerevarine and receives the Madstone of the Ahemmusa

**Zainab Tribe:**

77. Travels to the Zainab Camp southwest of Vos
78. Speaks with Wise Woman Sonummu Zabamat and learns of the Ashkhan's demands
79. Presents Exquisite Shoes to Ashkhan Kaushad to gain an audience
80. Learns Kaushad desires a high-born Telvanni bride
81. Purchases a slave girl from Tel Aruhn and disguises her as a Telvanni noblewoman
82. Presents the "bride" to Kaushad and is named Zainab Nerevarine

**Erabenimsun Tribe:**

83. Travels to the Erabenimsun Camp south of Tel Aruhn
84. Speaks with Wise Woman Manirai and learns the Ashkhan will never accept an outlander
85. Slays Ashkhan Ulath-Pal and his three hostile supporters in combat
86. Recovers the sacred items from the slain warriors
87. Presents the sacred items to Gulakhan Han-Ammu and convinces him to become Ashkhan
88. Is named Erabenimsun Nerevarine by the new Ashkhan

---

## Part IX: Meeting with Vivec

89. Receives a summons from Archcanon Tholer Saryoni
90. Travels to the High Fane in Vivec and meets with the Archcanon
91. Learns that Vivec wishes to meet with the Nerevarine
92. Enters the Palace of Vivec and speaks with the god-king
93. Listens to Vivec's account of the Tribunal's history and Dagoth Ur's return
94. Receives the gauntlet Wraithguard from Vivec
95. Learns the plan to sever the Heart of Lorkhan using Kagrenac's Tools
96. Receives the locations of Sunder and Keening on the map

---

## Part X: Kagrenac's Tools

97. Travels to the Ghostfence and enters the ashlands within Red Mountain's crater
98. Enters the Sixth House citadel of Vemynal
99. Battles through Ash Creatures and reaches the Hall of Torque
100. Slays the Ash Vampire Dagoth Vemyn and claims Sunder from his corpse
101. Enters the Sixth House citadel of Odrosal
102. Defeats the Ash Vampire Dagoth Odros and takes his key
103. Ascends the Tower of Odrosal and claims Keening
104. Equips Wraithguard before handling Sunder and Keening to avoid fatal harm

---

## Part XI: Assault on Dagoth Ur

105. Enters Dagoth Ur's citadel at the heart of Red Mountain
106. Navigates through the Facility Cavern toward the inner sanctum
107. Confronts Dagoth Ur, who offers the Nerevarine a place at his side
108. Refuses Dagoth Ur's offer and initiates combat
109. Defeats Dagoth Ur's physical form, knowing it will regenerate
110. Takes the ring from Dagoth Ur's body and enters the Akulakhan Chamber
111. Equips Wraithguard and Sunder while facing the Heart of Lorkhan
112. Strikes the Heart of Lorkhan once with Sunder to weaken it
113. Strikes the Heart of Lorkhan five times with Keening to destroy it
114. Watches as Dagoth Ur perishes and Akulakhan collapses into ruin
115. Escapes the crumbling chamber as Red Mountain shakes

---

## Part XII: Azura's Prophecy Fulfilled

116. Returns to the Facility Cavern and meets the apparition of Azura
117. Listens as Azura proclaims the prophecy fulfilled and the curse lifted
118. Receives the Ring of Azura as a token of the Daedric Prince's favor
119. Emerges from Red Mountain as the Nerevarine, savior of Morrowind
