# Conan — Actions

_"Beyond the Black River" by Robert E. Howard_

1. Tracks a Pictish robber through the forest for several hours
2. Slays the Pict before he can ambush the settler Balthus
3. Identifies the headless corpse as merchant Tiberias, a victim of Zogar Sag's curse
4. Constructs a crude litter to carry the merchant's body toward Fort Tuscelan
5. Hurls his axe at a glowing supernatural figure attempting to steal Tiberias's head
6. Reports to Governor Valannus about the sorcerer threat and rising Pict aggression
7. Proposes a covert strategy of twelve men rather than a large, noisy battalion
8. Hand-picks ten soldiers to accompany him on a secret assassination mission
9. Leads a canoe expedition down the Black River toward Zogar Sag's village
10. Slips ashore with nine men while Balthus and one soldier guard the boat
11. Rescues Balthus by thrusting a spear through a giant serpent's neck during Zogar Sag's attack
12. Sets fire to village huts to create confusion during their escape
13. Flees deeper into the Pictish forest rather than retreating toward the river
14. Kills a leopard tracking them through the forest
15. Scratches a protective symbol on the ground to repel Zogar Sag's enchanted beasts
16. Mimics the Pict language to lure a Pict rower to shore
17. Kills the Pict rower with an arrow and steals his canoe
18. Crosses the Black River back to Aquilonian territory with Balthus
19. Reunites with Slasher, a feral dog he had previously rescued
20. Deduces an imminent Pictish attack on Fort Tuscelan and races northward
21. Splits from Balthus to warn salt-gathering fishermen while Balthus warns the settlers
22. Encounters a swamp devil on the road, revealed to be Zogar Sag's half-brother
23. Beheads the demon with a powerful swing, killing Zogar Sag through their mystical link
24. Leads warned settlers to safety in Velitrium
25. Vows to avenge Balthus's death with the heads of more Picts
